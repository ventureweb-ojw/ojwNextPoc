<div><p paraeid="{31b5dfaf-2417-4246-9c9b-9e43f13f12ad}{162}" paraid="1535064995">Fall in the mountains is a fan favourite for so many reasons! The days are shorter, providing time for extra shut-eye and blanket hogging before hitting the trails; the temperatures drop, waking wool sweaters from hibernation; and fewer crowds are lining up to get into some of Banff and Lake Louise’s hottest dining establishments.</p>
<h4 paraeid="{31b5dfaf-2417-4246-9c9b-9e43f13f12ad}{162}" paraid="1535064995">Take it on the Trails</h4>
<p paraeid="{31b5dfaf-2417-4246-9c9b-9e43f13f12ad}{190}" paraid="596475574">Before hitting the trails, make sure to get something to cozy up in or with while soaking up those mountain top views or golden larches. Try some of these local favourites to take out with you on the trails. </p>
<h5 paraeid="{31b5dfaf-2417-4246-9c9b-9e43f13f12ad}{200}" paraid="675306792"><a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Big Bear Trading Co." data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Souvenir" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2969" href="" target="_blank">Big Bear Trading Co.</a> and <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Rocky Mountain Flannel Company" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sporting goods &amp; Apparel" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2821" href="http://www.rockymountainflannelcompany.com" target="_blank">Rocky Mountain Flannel Co.</a>  </h5>
<p paraeid="{31b5dfaf-2417-4246-9c9b-9e43f13f12ad}{210}" paraid="1212322096">Find oversized sweaters, vintage long sleeves, and flannel PJs at these two downtown stores. While you’re there, pick up some long-burning candles, plush fleece blankets, and mugs.  </p>

<h4 paraeid="{8018365c-4b3c-4121-8725-9360f0b2d204}{11}" paraid="1244011668">Apres Hiking</h4>
<p paraeid="{8018365c-4b3c-4121-8725-9360f0b2d204}{11}" paraid="1244011668">After a day full of fresh alpine air, out exploring trails, kick your boots off, unwind and cozy up at these local restaurants. Get a local favourite cozy food like fondue, Alberta beef, or Italian and finish off with an after-meal coffee.</p>
<h4 paraeid="{8018365c-4b3c-4121-8725-9360f0b2d204}{21}" paraid="1779564767">Fondue </h4>
<p paraeid="{8018365c-4b3c-4121-8725-9360f0b2d204}{31}" paraid="1890267651">There’s nothing that comforts the soul more than a group of your favourite people gathering around a nice gooey pot of Swiss cheese. Find this traditional mountain fare and so much more here:  </p>
<h5 paraeid="{8018365c-4b3c-4121-8725-9360f0b2d204}{41}" paraid="354363003"><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Grizzly House Restaurant" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2304" href="http://www.banffgrizzlyhouse.com" target="_blank">The Grizzly House </a> </h5>
<p paraeid="{8018365c-4b3c-4121-8725-9360f0b2d204}{47}" paraid="1729361907">The famous Grizzly House is steeped in Banff history. If you’re craving exotic meats from all over the world, make sure you add this to your itinerary.  </p>
<h5 paraeid="{8018365c-4b3c-4121-8725-9360f0b2d204}{57}" paraid="1157392050"><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Waldhaus Pub &amp; Biergarten" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2483" href="https://www.banff-springs-hotel.com/dine/waldhaus-pub-biergarten/" target="_blank">Waldhaus Restaurant &amp; Pub </a></h5>
<p paraeid="{8018365c-4b3c-4121-8725-9360f0b2d204}{63}" paraid="570198398">Fairmont Banff Springs is well-known for its culinary offerings. A short walk from the main Castle, this Bavarian-style pub house will have you sipping a pint of beer fireside in no time.  </p>

<h5 paraeid="{8018365c-4b3c-4121-8725-9360f0b2d204}{73}" paraid="2107151674"><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="28.0000" data-gtm_label="Ticino Swiss-Italian Restaurant" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2177" href="http://www.ticinorestaurant.com" target="_blank">Ticino</a></h5>
<p paraeid="{8018365c-4b3c-4121-8725-9360f0b2d204}{79}" paraid="1637997686">Ticino serves up a Swiss, Italian, and Canadian fusion food and culture. Started by Swiss immigrant Erwin and his wife, Lorraine, the restaurant is now owned by his daughter, Jill, and her husband, Louis-Pierre Hélie, Canadian Olympian. </p>

<h4 paraeid="{8018365c-4b3c-4121-8725-9360f0b2d204}{89}" paraid="1271231702">Alberta Beef </h4>
<p paraeid="{8018365c-4b3c-4121-8725-9360f0b2d204}{99}" paraid="1971969595">While in Alberta, do as Albertans do and pull up a chair to indulge in homegrown beef. Many of our restaurants pride themselves on sourcing local, quality ingredients from farmers they know and love. Give these spots a try:  </p>
<h5 paraeid="{8018365c-4b3c-4121-8725-9360f0b2d204}{109}" paraid="1631832337"><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="The Bison Restaurant &amp; Terrace" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2156" href="http://www.thebison.ca" target="_blank">The Bison</a>  </h5>
<p paraeid="{8018365c-4b3c-4121-8725-9360f0b2d204}{115}" paraid="8750264">The absolute best time to visit The Bison is at dusk. The mountains are still visible, the twinkle lights turn on, and the glow of Bear Street below makes you feel the true magic of being in a mountain town. Let us assure you, the food is the cherry on top with fall-off-the-bone meats and imaginative dressings and sides.  </p>

<p paraeid="{8018365c-4b3c-4121-8725-9360f0b2d204}{131}" paraid="1676604346">Banff was once part of the wild west, and Chuck’s Steakhouse honours that past with an upscale twist.
