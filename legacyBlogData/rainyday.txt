<div><p>The sun can't shine every day in Banff National Park, so don't let a little rain get in your way! Here are just some of the options available for you if the rain is coming down in our Canadian Rockies.</p>
<p>Tip: Before you head out, check the most up-to-date weather conditions on the <a href="/trip-planning/webcams">webcams</a> page. </p>
<h4>Lake Cruise On Lake Minnewanka</h4>
<p>Head out to the infamous 'Devil's Gap' on the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="60.0000" data-gtm_label="Lake Minnewanka Cruise" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2384" href="https://bll.fyi/q9r5?utm_source=BLL&amp;utm_medium=PkgListings&amp;utm_campaign=BLL_Web13" target="_blank">Lake Minnewanka Cruise</a>. The ride out includes some great interpretive facts from the engaging guides, and you never know what <span style="color: #0000ff;"><span style="color: #000000;">animals</span></span> you might see on the shore. There are four exciting cruises to choose from including the new Beer Voyage, starting June 28.</p>

<h4>Take A Drive On The Bow Valley Parkway</h4>
<p>The drive on the <a href="/blog/8-must-visit-stops-bow-valley-parkway">Bow Valley Parkway</a> (Hwy 1A) in between Banff and Lake Louise is a spectacular stretch of road filled with great views, interpretive stops, beautiful cabins, numerous trailheads (like <a href="/johnston-canyon">Johnston Canyon</a> and Castle Mountain Lookout) and some tasty lunch options at places like <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Baker Creek Bistro" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2782" href="http://bakercreek.com/" target="_blank">Baker Creek</a> or nearby <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Storm Mountain Lodge &amp; Cabins" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2599" href="http://www.stormmountainlodge.com" target="_blank">Storm Mountain Lodge</a>. This route is also teeming with wildlife so please remember to follow the posted speed limits and be respectful when watching wildlife from the car.  </p>

<h4>Soak In the Banff Upper Hot Springs</h4>
<p>Rain won't matter when you are soaking in the <a data-gtm_category="Spa &amp; Wellness" data-gtm_event_value="7.3000" data-gtm_label="Banff Upper Hot Springs" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Day Spas" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2330" href="https://www.hotsprings.ca/banff" target="_blank">Banff Upper Hot Springs</a><a data-gtm_category="Spa &amp; Wellness" data-gtm_event_value="7.3000" data-gtm_label="Banff Upper Hot Springs" data-gtm_subcategory="Day Spas" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2330" href="http://www.hotsprings.ca/#!banff-upper-hot-springs/" target="_blank">,</a> located up Mountain Ave and near the <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Rimrock Resort Hotel" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2597" href="https://bll.fyi/algy" target="_blank">Rimrock Resort Hotel</a> and <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://bll.fyi/k1l4" target="_blank">Banff Gondola</a>. Reputed to have healing powers, the waters are heated from thermals under Sulphur Mountain. Read more in our <a href="https://www.banfflakelouise.com/blog/spas-and-hot-springs-banff-national-park">Complete Guide to Spas and Hotsprings</a>. </p>

<h4>Go To A Movie</h4>
<p>The <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="13.0000" data-gtm_label="Lux Cinema Centre" data-gtm_subcategory="Indoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2249" href="http://luxbanff.com/" target="_blank">Lux Cinema</a> in Banff has 4 theatres and shows the latest Hollywood features, including 3D showings. For more information on showtimes visit their <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="13.0000" data-gtm_label="Lux Cinema Centre" data-gtm_subcategory="Indoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2249" href="http://luxbanff.com" target="_blank">listings page</a> or swing by the ticket window on Bear Street. </p>
<h4>Hit The Bowling Alley </h4>
<p>Located on Banff Avenue, <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="High Rollers" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2752" href="http://www.highrollersbanff.com/" target="_blank">High Rollers</a> has six 10 pin bowling lanes, along with a great menu and 50 beers on tap. The <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Banff Springs" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2473" href="http://www.fairmont.com/banff-springs/" target="_blank">Fairmont Banff Springs</a> has bowling lanes available, as well as a new <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Banff Springs" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2473" href="http://www.fairmont.com/banff-springs/promotions/golfsimulator/" target="_blank">HD golf simulator</a> where you can play the Banff Springs course, or 20 others, inside and out of the rain. It is also fun to explore the hotel's history of being named the 'Castle In The Rockies' by walking through its impressive hallways and public rooms.  </p>

<h4>Make An Escape </h4>
<p><font color="#000000">Looking for a challenge? Banff is home to the Bow Valley's Only Escape Room at <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="30.0000" data-gtm_label="EureKa Banff" data-gtm_subcategory="Indoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2742" href="http://www.eurekabanff.ca/" target="_blank">Eureka Banff Escape Room Adventures</a>. Eureka is a fully immersive interactive puzzle room where you will need to work with your team to complete the set objective in under 1 hour to win.</font></p>
<p>The two rooms are themed and designed completely different, with no room having the same objective. You will need to figure out the puzzles in order to complete your task! </p>

<h4>Dine at A Scenic Restaurant</h4>
<p>Don’t let the chance of rain stop you from enjoying the views! While the weather may not be what you expected, there is still a lot of beauty to be seen in the mountains. Take the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://bll.fyi/lhpm" target="_blank">Banff Gondola</a> to the summit of Sulphur Mountain and dine above the clouds at <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Sky Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2119" href="https://bll.fyi/w1qa" target="_blank">Sky Bistro</a>. Or watch the weather go by at the <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Juniper Hotel Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2753" href="https://bll.fyi/kvse" target="_blank">Juniper Bistro</a> which offers seasonal cuisine and breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains.</p>
<p>For more options check out <a href="/dining-nightlife">Dining in Banff and Lake Louise</a>.</p>

<h4>Go Caving</h4>
<p>Join in a guided caving adventure, deep under Grotto Mountain in Canmore, with <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="100.0000" data-gtm_label="Canmore Cave Tours" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2334" href="http://www.canmorecavetours.com/" target="_blank">Canmore Cave Tours</a>. The 'Rat's Nest Cave' is filled with underground chambers and tunnels, with no hand rails or overhead lighting. No experience necessary.</p>
