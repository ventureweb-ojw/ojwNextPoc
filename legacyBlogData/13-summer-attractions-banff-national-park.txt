<div><p>With the change of the seasons comes the change of attractions in Banff and Lake Louise. Each diverse in location, experience, and accessibility, Banff’s summer attractions share their opening season with enticing patios that feature spectacular mountain views. Wondering when the best of Banff and Lake Louise starts up for summer? Here’s your guide to summer attractions in Banff National Park.</p>
<h4>Horseback tours - Open April</h4>
<p>Ranging from one-hour to overnight excursions, horseback riding in Banff and Lake Louise transports guests around the area in the same way that the pioneers travelled. Exploring via horseback is a peaceful, engaging, and immersive way to explore the Rockies at ease. Trot around Banff for the day with <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Trail Riders" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2205" href="https://bll.fyi/pyft" target="_blank">Banff Trail Riders</a>, or take advantage of their overnight backcountry hut trips and pack trips. Explore Lake Louise and its tea houses or take advantage of glacial and alpine exploring with <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Timberline Tours Ltd." data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2179" href="http://www.timberlinetours.ca" target="_blank">Timberline Tours</a> or <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Brewster Adventures" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2434" href="http://www.brewsteradventures.com" target="_blank">Brewster Adventures</a>. </p>
<h4>Lake Minnewanka Cruise - Open Mid-May</h4>
<p>Banff’s only lake cruise offers visitors an experience found nowhere else in the park. The <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="60.0000" data-gtm_label="Lake Minnewanka Cruise" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2384" href="https://bll.fyi/q9r5?utm_source=BLL&amp;utm_medium=PkgListings&amp;utm_campaign=BLL_Web13" target="_blank">Lake Minnewanka Cruise</a> makes its way across the scenic waters of Banff National Park's largest lake as your knowledgable captains share information on how the lake came to be, what is hiding beneath the surface, and the local and indigenous history of the area. </p>
<p>Tip: Cruises fill quickly, <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="60.0000" data-gtm_label="Lake Minnewanka Cruise" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2384" href="https://bll.fyi/q9r5?utm_source=BLL&amp;utm_medium=PkgListings&amp;utm_campaign=BLL_Web13" target="_blank">book online</a>​ to reserve your cruise departure time before you visit.</p>

<p>Banff’s only canoe rentals start paddling on the turquoise glacial waters of the Bow River and the Vermilion Lakes mid-May, offering a calm, quiet, and relaxing perspective of town. Wildlife like beavers, muskrats, elk, and deer are most commonly seen in and around the river and lakes. Rent a canoe, kayak, or SUP from <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="92.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Canoe Club" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2528" href="https://bll.fyi/yb11" target="_blank">Banff Canoe Club</a>. </p>
<h4>Whitewater Rafting - Open Mid-May</h4>
<p>The snow is melting, the rivers are filling, and the rapids are ready to paddle! Whitewater rafting trips with <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Hydra River Guides" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2211" href="https://www.raftbanff.com" target="_blank">Hydra River Guides</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="110.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Rafting Centre" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2392" href="https://bll.fyi/j9th" target="_blank">Chinook Rafting</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="79.0000" data-gtm_label="Canadian Rockies Rafting" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2331" href="http://www.rafting.ca" target="_blank">Canadian Rockies Rafting</a>, or <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Wild Water Adventures" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2463" href="http://www.wildwater.com" target="_blank">Wild Water Adventures</a> start as the season progresses with the first tours opening mid-May. Raft gentle Class 2-3 rapids with the family on the Kananaskis River, get more adventurous on Class 3-4 rapids on the Horseshoe Canyon or go deeper and bigger with up to Class 5 rapids on the Kicking Horse River.</p>

<p>For optimal viewing times, visit outside of peak hours in the early morning or evening. Under the Hoodoos and along the base of Mount Rundle, the tour gives guests of all ages an interpretive experience from a unique vantage point. Experience Banff from the river with a <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Rocky Mountain Raft Tours" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2030" href="http://www.banffrafttours.com" target="_blank">Rocky Mountain Raft Tour</a> starting late May. </p>

<h4>Banff Sunshine Summer Sightseeing - Closed summer 2020</h4>
<p>Banff National Park’s most accessible alpine hiking is at Sunshine Meadows. At the top of the gondola, ride the Standish Chair to the Standish Lookout and choose from long or short hikes that take guests to alpine lakes, mountaintop valleys, and across the border into British Columbia. For an added experience, book a night at Banff’s highest hotel - the <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="250.0000" data-gtm_label="Sunshine Mountain Lodge" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2498" href="http://www.sunshinemountainlodge.com/" target="_blank">Sunshine Mountain Lodge</a>.   </p>
