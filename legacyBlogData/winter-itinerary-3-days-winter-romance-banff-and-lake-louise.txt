<div><p>Step one in disconnecting from the every day and reconnecting with your romantic side is simple: head for the hills. In this case, the hills are the Canadian Rocky Mountains, and the moment you arrive, you’re guaranteed to be swept up by their presence. When wintertime comes in Banff National Park, a sense of togetherness descends with the falling snow and makes this winter wonderland the perfect time and place for a romantic holiday getaway.</p>

<h4> Day 1: Escape to the Mountains</h4>
<p>Make your base camp at the feet of giants in a charming alpine cabin. Perfectly rustic and romantic cabins are waiting amidst the forest at <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Castle Mountain Chalets" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2346" href="http://www.castlemountain.com" target="_blank">Castle Mountain Chalets</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="250.0000" data-gtm_label="Baker Creek Mountain Resorts" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="1981" href="http://www.bakercreek.com" target="_blank">Baker Creek Mountain Resort</a>, and the Woodland Cabins at the<a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Juniper Hotel" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2227" href="https://thejuniper.com/cabins/" target="_blank"> Juniper Hotel</a>.</p>
<p>Check-in early and bundle up for <a href="/blog/your-guide-dog-sledding-banff-and-lake-louise">dog-sledding</a> – an adventure made for two. Greet your eager dog team, and then cocoon yourselves inside the sled as your musher gives the dogs the signal they’ve been waiting for, and you glide smoothly across the sparkling white wilderness.</p>

<p>As the late afternoon sun dips down towards the peaks, take the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://bll.fyi/lhpm" target="_blank">Banff Gondola</a> upwards to the summit of Sulphur Mountain. Wander the observation decks and watch the night sky come to life as it only can in the crisp cold air of winter. To complete this ultimate date night, visit <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Sky Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2119" href="https://bll.fyi/w1qa" target="_blank">Sky Bistro</a> for an enchanting meal high above the glittering lights of Banff.</p>

<h4> Day 2: Charm &amp; Wonder in Lake Louise</h4>
<p>The village of Lake Louise – and the stunning peaks that surround it – are transformed during the holiday season. The magic of a mountain Christmas is alive here and carries through the winter season. To discover it for yourselves, rent ice skates in Lake Louise and carve twin tracks along the frozen surface of this immense lake.</p>

Dine amidst treasures from the past in the historic train station building, where the trip of a lifetime for countless couples began in the early days of mountain exploration.</p>
<h4> Day 3: Time for Two in Banff</h4>
<p>Walking through snowy downtown Banff clasping mittened hands, there’s no denying that the mountains bring out everyone’s inner romantic. Go ahead and indulge yourselves in the <a href="/shopping">shops</a> of Banff Avenue and Bear Street. The holiday spirit is evident in every smiling face and, most of all, at the wondrous <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="The Spirit of Christmas" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Souvenir" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2168" href="http://www.spiritofchristmas.ca" target="_blank">Spirit of Christmas</a> store.</p>
