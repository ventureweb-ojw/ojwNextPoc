<div><p>There is nothing quite like getting close to nature and hanging out by the campfire with good friends. <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Parks Canada oTENTik" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Campgrounds" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2610" href="http://www.pc.gc.ca/eng/pn-np/ab/banff/activ/camping/otentik.aspx" target="_blank">Parks Canada's new oTENTik experience</a> makes it easier than ever.  </p>
<p><a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Parks Canada oTENTik" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Campgrounds" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2610" href="http://www.pc.gc.ca/eng/pn-np/ab/banff/activ/camping/otentik.aspx" target="_blank">oTENTiks</a> are the perfect introduction to camping for families or new campers looking to explore the wonders of Banff National Park. </p>
<p>A cross between an A-frame cabin and a canvas tent, these units can fit up to six people and come equipped with almost everything you need to enjoy a proper "glamping" experience, including electricity, heat and bunk beds.  </p>

<p>In Banff National Park, the <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Parks Canada oTENTik" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Campgrounds" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2610" href="http://www.pc.gc.ca/eng/pn-np/ab/banff/activ/camping/otentik.aspx" target="_blank">oTENTiks</a> are located in the lakeside area of the<a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Two Jack Lakeside Campground" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Campgrounds" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2571" href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/en/pn-np/ab/banff/activ/camping/twojack2" target="_blank"> Two Jack campground</a><a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Two Jack Lakeside Campground" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Campgrounds" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2571" href="http://www.pc.gc.ca/eng/pn-np/ab/banff/activ/camping/twojack2.aspx" target="_blank">,</a> a beautiful setting located just a short drive from downtown Banff. They are also conveniently located at <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Tunnel Mountain Village 2 Campground" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Campgrounds" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2570" href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/en/pn-np/ab/banff/activ/camping/tunnel2" target="_blank">Tunnel Mountain Campground</a> - accessible from the town of Banff by public transit.</p>
