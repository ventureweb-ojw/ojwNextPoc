<div><p dir="ltr">The world’s fastest skiers are coming to Lake Louise, Alberta to compete in the historic <a href="https://bll.fyi/onsz">Lake Louise Audi FIS Ski World Cup</a>.</p>
<p>Experience the high-speed action of world-class alpine ski racing at Canada’s premiere ski event at the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="100.0000" data-gtm_label="The Lake Louise Ski Resort" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2603" href="https://bll.fyi/r495">Lake Louise Ski Resort</a>. It’s one of the select few venues that hold both the men’s and ladies speed events on the World Cup circuit and will play host to the first Audi FIS Ski World Cup downhill and super-G races of the season. </p>
<p>Fans are encouraged to come watch the event from the Free Fan Cheering Zone which opens an hour before race time - no lift ticket required! If you're looking for a bit more party, you can enjoy the fun games and local food and drink on the Banded Peak Basecamp Patio and cheer on 18 nations as they compete on the FIS Alpine World Cup tour</p>
<p>Grab a noisemaker and come cheer on the racers!   </p>
