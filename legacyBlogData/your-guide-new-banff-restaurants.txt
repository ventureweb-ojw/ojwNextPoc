<div><h4 paraeid="{e778d475-118e-49b1-9250-caae763bd8ed}{183}" paraid="2091052014">Shoku Izakaya</h4>
<p paraeid="{e778d475-118e-49b1-9250-caae763bd8ed}{195}" paraid="1259683821">Tantalize your taste buds in an environment that is as dynamic as the food selections. Delight in a variety of choices including rice bowls, noodles, and sushi. <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Shoku Izakaya" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2957" href="https://shokubanff.com/" target="_blank">Shoku Izakaya</a> deserves a place on any foodie’s bucket list. </p>
<p paraeid="{e778d475-118e-49b1-9250-caae763bd8ed}{231}" paraid="360795777">Taste of Place: Try the Tokyo Fries that Chef Stephane Prevost first made popular at Block Kitchen + Bar.  </p>
<p paraeid="{e778d475-118e-49b1-9250-caae763bd8ed}{249}" paraid="1447727219">Local’s Tip: Check out the current lunch/dinner features on offer for the chance to try something new! </p>
<h4 paraeid="{ce465704-7267-4669-af1a-746ce6269419}{8}" paraid="1761020685">Rocky Mountain Chocolate Factory</h4>
<p paraeid="{ce465704-7267-4669-af1a-746ce6269419}{16}" paraid="1806799205">While taking in the vibrant views of Cascade Mountain, you can indulge your senses in Banff’s newest dessert haven. Featuring exclusively handmade chocolate creations; <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Rocky Mountain Chocolate Factory" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2956" href="https://rockychoc.com/" target="_blank">Rocky Mountain Chocolate Factory</a> perfectly blends the majesty of the mountains with the sweet smell of chocolate.  </p>
<p paraeid="{ce465704-7267-4669-af1a-746ce6269419}{44}" paraid="1829830813">Taste of Place: Can’t decide between the rows of delectable desserts? Try the 11pc Rocky Reserve Collection featuring berries, nuts, and artisan chocolate with floral and fruit accents.  </p>
<p paraeid="{ce465704-7267-4669-af1a-746ce6269419}{50}" paraid="1252054157">Local’s Tip: Take your sweet treat down to Central Park and relax next to the Bow River.  </p>

<h4 paraeid="{ce465704-7267-4669-af1a-746ce6269419}{60}" paraid="730864795">Three Bears Brewery and Restaurant </h4>
<p paraeid="{ce465704-7267-4669-af1a-746ce6269419}{68}" paraid="2062963203">Banff’s <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Three Bears Brewery and Restaurant" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2950" href="https://threebearsbanff.com/" target="_blank">newest brewery</a> is known for bringing the great outdoors – indoors. Featuring a retractable roof for stargazing, a beer garden, and cozy patio spaces; you’ll find the atmosphere as appealing as the fare. Perfectly pair salads, sandwiches, and pizzas with craft beer and creative cocktails. </p>
<p paraeid="{ce465704-7267-4669-af1a-746ce6269419}{86}" paraid="642004154">Taste of Place: Fresh pizza dough made using a process known as “reverse ice water ferment”. It takes 72 hours but it is clearly well worth the wait! </p>
<p paraeid="{ce465704-7267-4669-af1a-746ce6269419}{96}" paraid="48969615">Local’s Tip: Try out Happy Hour from 3:00 pm – 5:00 pm daily. You can find plenty of patio seating if your VIP (very important pup) came along for the outing </p>

<h4 paraeid="{ce465704-7267-4669-af1a-746ce6269419}{156}" paraid="1296269559">Saffron Indian Bistro </h4>
<p paraeid="{ce465704-7267-4669-af1a-746ce6269419}{164}" paraid="1335851060">Banff’s <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Saffron-Indian Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2959" href="https://saffronbanff.ca/" target="_blank">newest Indian Bistro</a> highlights the most precious spice in the world; ranging from Saffron Special Paneer Tikka to Saffron Special Chicken Curry. The authentic cuisine will take you on a trip around the world; offering something to satisfy any hungry traveller. </p>
<p paraeid="{ce465704-7267-4669-af1a-746ce6269419}{186}" paraid="188874190">Taste of Place: Throughout history, Saffron has been used in food, drinks, and traditional herbal medicine. This spice is also rich in antioxidants.  </p>
<p paraeid="{ce465704-7267-4669-af1a-746ce6269419}{192}" paraid="1519900683">Local’s Tip: From 11 a.m. – 3 p.m. daily enjoy a Saffron Special Thali. These Thali’s include traditional Indian food, a variety of Tandoori sizzlers, as well as appetizers and curries. </p>
<h4 paraeid="{ce465704-7267-4669-af1a-746ce6269419}{226}" paraid="1828334783">The Uprising Bake Shop + Espresso Bar </h4>
<p paraeid="{ce465704-7267-4669-af1a-746ce6269419}{234}" paraid="541642537">This <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="15.0000" data-gtm_label="The Uprising Craft Bake Shop" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bakery &amp; Deli" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2968" href="http://www.theuprisingbakery.com" target="_blank">new addition to Banff Avenue</a> offers handcrafted bread products of the highest quality. Featuring muffins, pastries, sandwiches, and of course bread (including gluten-free) you’ll be spoiled for choice when it comes to a fresh-baked treat. </p>
<p paraeid="{ce465704-7267-4669-af1a-746ce6269419}{240}" paraid="1663054147">Taste of Place: Their signature is sourdough – so you can expect to see lots of sourdough varieties. </p>
<p paraeid="{ce465704-7267-4669-af1a-746ce6269419}{248}" paraid="1103688598">Local’s Tip: Each week they create a Bread Bag featuring select breads baked fresh and delivered the same day. </p>

<h4 paraeid="{70fa9a74-42c3-47ce-80d8-01d30796df9b}{8}" paraid="431257970">Melissa’s MisSteak *New Location*</h4>
<p paraeid="{70fa9a74-42c3-47ce-80d8-01d30796df9b}{20}" paraid="1774900630"><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Melissa's Restaurant &amp; Bar" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2073" href="http://www.melissasmissteak.com" target="_blank">Melissa’s Family Restaurant</a> is locally owned and operated and offers a selection of items that appeal to all budgets and tastes. Now you can find the same great food and friendly service in a new and exciting space! As you explore Banff Avenue be sure to stop into this familiar favourite at 201 Banff Avenue. </p>
<p paraeid="{70fa9a74-42c3-47ce-80d8-01d30796df9b}{44}" paraid="415957610">Taste of Place: This is a great option for families looking for an economic choice that doesn’t compromise on food quality or taste! </p>
<p paraeid="{70fa9a74-42c3-47ce-80d8-01d30796df9b}{50}" paraid="218862605">Local’s Tip: Enjoy Happy Hour from 3 p.m. – 6 p.m. daily. They also offer an array of daily specials ranging from drinks to all-you-can-eat prime rib. </p>
<h4 paraeid="{70fa9a74-42c3-47ce-80d8-01d30796df9b}{96}" paraid="1727504227">Peak Café (formerly Skoki’s Waffle and Frozen Yogurt) </h4>
<p paraeid="{70fa9a74-42c3-47ce-80d8-01d30796df9b}{114}" paraid="1199804759">Never fear! Your favourite frozen desserts are still available on Banff Avenue including sweet crepes, waffles, and soft-serve ice cream. However, now you can enjoy a hearty meal before digging into dessert with a new range of Vietnamese appetizers, soups, and rice bowls. Indulge in a range of savoury meal options and complete your visit with a delectable dessert all in a <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Peak Cafe" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bakery &amp; Deli" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2687" href="https://peakcafebanff.com/" target="_blank">one-stop-shop</a>. </p>
<p paraeid="{70fa9a74-42c3-47ce-80d8-01d30796df9b}{146}" paraid="984604857">Taste of Place: Enjoy fresh and affordable options ranging from smoothies to stir-fried vermicelli. Find classic favourites and new dishes to explore at the same great location. </p>
<p paraeid="{70fa9a74-42c3-47ce-80d8-01d30796df9b}{160}" paraid="1507871255">Local’s Tip: Tantalize your taste buds with over 16 flavours of Milk Bubble Tea. </p>
<h4 paraeid="{70fa9a74-42c3-47ce-80d8-01d30796df9b}{174}" paraid="334324443">Hello Sunshine </h4>
<p paraeid="{70fa9a74-42c3-47ce-80d8-01d30796df9b}{182}" paraid="200266148">A new one-of-a-kind restaurant in Banff, <a data-gtm_category="Services" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Hospitality Collective" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Professional Services" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2702" href="https://www.banffcollective.com/" target="_blank">Hello Sunshine</a> is a combination sushi and Japanese-barbecue karaoke joint. If you’re looking for a funky place to have drinks, dinner, or just a good time, look no further. This retro restaurant &amp; karaoke bar features eye-catching fire tables, a sushi bar, groovy décor and all the good vibes. Complete with 2 karaoke rooms and a bonus Tiny Bar, this place has everything you’re looking for and more.</p>
<p paraeid="{70fa9a74-42c3-47ce-80d8-01d30796df9b}{210}" paraid="1189202408">Taste of Place: Indulge in the Matcha Bubble Tea featuring organic matcha, Park vanilla vodka, oat milk, and tapioca pearls </p>
<p paraeid="{70fa9a74-42c3-47ce-80d8-01d30796df9b}{224}" paraid="1985007374">Local’s Tip: Pull up a seat at the sushi bar and connect with our local chefs</p>

<h4 paraeid="{70fa9a74-42c3-47ce-80d8-01d30796df9b}{248}" paraid="1761807362">The Prow (Buffalo Mountain Lodge) </h4>
<p paraeid="{b13e3f95-7881-4f99-b642-8ededa9192f0}{9}" paraid="1609524719">Newly opened in Buffalo Mountain Lodge, <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="The Prow" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2732" href="https://crmr.com/resorts/buffalo-mountain/dining/" target="_blank">The Prow</a> aims to bring guests amazing local cuisine in an approachable way and caters to all dietary needs. The menu includes mountain-themed tapas, game meats, excellent shareables, and pasta proudly made in-house. A culture of creative cocktails and local brews is boasted at this new locale.</p>
