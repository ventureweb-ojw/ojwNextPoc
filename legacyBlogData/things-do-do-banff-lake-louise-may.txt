<div><p>Every month in Banff National Park has its own unique qualities and May has the perfect cross-section of seasons! While slopeside activities go out with a bang, the summer’s best experiences begin to trickle in.</p>
<p>Here is your guide to exploring Banff National Park in May:</p>
<h4>Lace Up Your Hiking Boots</h4>
<p>As the snow continues to melt, some of Banff National Park’s best hiking trails start to open up. Ease into summer hiking and get those mountain legs working with lower-elevation hikes. You'll still discover scenic viewpoints and mountain lakes that are the first to thaw. Check out <a href="/blog/10-early-season-hikes-banff-national-park">10 Early Season Hikes</a> for some great options and <a href="/blog/guide-spring-hiking-banff-national-park">A Guide to Spring Hiking</a> to prepare.</p>

<h4>Spot Some Wildlife</h4>
<p>Did you know Banff National Park is home to 53 different species of mammals? Springtime is one of the best times to spot <a href="/blog/wildlife-banff-national-park">wildlife in the park</a> according to celebrated local photographer <a data-gtm_category="Services" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Paul Zizka Guiding &amp; Photography" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Professional Services" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2125" href="http://www.zizka.ca" target="_blank">Paul Zizka</a>. Keep an eye out for mountain goats grazing by the roadside or grizzly bears waking from their winter slumber. Remember to view wildlife from a safe distance and check out our blog about how you can be a more <a href="/blog/how-be-responsible-visitor-banff-national-park">responsible visitor in Banff National Park</a>!</p>

<h4>Take a Swing At It </h4>
<p>Every golfer knows the smell that sends tingles down their spine when they step onto the course. Around here, the fresh-cut grass is enhanced by the fresh mountain air. The <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Springs Golf Course" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2475" href="http://www.fairmontgolf.com/banffsprings" target="_blank">Fairmont Banff Springs Golf Course</a> (opens May 7) goes unrivalled when it comes to its scenic backdrops and thoughtful layout. A round here is the perfect way to spend a spring day.  </p>

<h4>Pick Up a Paddle</h4>
<p>Springtime means the waterways are melting. One of the best ways to experience the beautiful Bow River is right from the docks of the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="92.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Canoe Club" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2528" href="https://bll.fyi/yb11" target="_blank">Banff Canoe Club</a> right when it first opens. You will find it's quieter on the river than in peak season and higher water levels mean you may be able to paddle all the way to Vermilion Lakes! </p>

<h4>Gear Up for a Great Adventure</h4>
<p>Perhaps you’ve put some of Banff and Lake Louise’s larger attractions at the top of your bucket list? Be sure to linger long enough to tick more attractions off your list while you’re here. Take a cruise on Lake Minnewanka aboard the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="60.0000" data-gtm_label="Lake Minnewanka Cruise" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2384" href="https://bll.fyi/q9r5?utm_source=BLL&amp;utm_medium=PkgListings&amp;utm_campaign=BLL_Web13" target="_blank">Lake Minnewanka Cruise</a>, or get up-close-and-personal with icefields and canyons on the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="80.0000" data-gtm_label="Columbia Icefield Adventure" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2359" href="https://www.banffjaspercollection.com/attractions/columbia-icefield/" target="_blank">Columbia Icefield Adventure</a> and <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="32.0000" data-gtm_label="Columbia Icefield Skywalk" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2540" href="https://padlock.link/rlfx" target="_blank">Glacier Skywalk</a>.</p>

<h4>Early season horse riding</h4>
<p>Discover more of Banff National Park on the back of a horse. Join <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Trail Riders" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2205" href="http://www.horseback.com" target="_blank">Banff Trail Riders</a> for either a relaxing one-hour ride along the Bow River or a thrilling three-hour river crossing adventure. With tours running throughout May, experience Banff from a new perspective.</p>
