<div><p>School’s out, Spring is in. How are you planning to spend it?</p>
<p>If you're looking for a destination that offers the perfect mix of play time and down time, Banff National Park makes the top of the list! You’ll have plenty of chances to get your family out into the fresh air, keeping you connected through shared experiences in the great outdoors. </p>
<p>Sound like a plan? Here are some activity ideas (great for all ages!) to make this a trip you’ll all remember:</p>
<h4>1. Skiing and Snowboarding</h4>
<p>Spend the day at the hill to get your dose of adrenaline amid the most scenic ski runs the country has to offer. All ski areas – <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="65.0000" data-gtm_label="Mt. Norquay" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2098" href="https://bll.fyi/9jrv" target="_blank">Mt. Norquay</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="105.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Sunshine Village" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2143" href="https://bll.fyi/1i95" target="_blank">Banff Sunshine</a>, and <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="100.0000" data-gtm_label="The Lake Louise Ski Resort" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2603" href="https://bll.fyi/r495" target="_blank">Lake Louise Ski Resort</a> – offer on-site rentals and slopes appropriate for all levels of ability. </p>
<p>Tip: Spring is the perfect time to sign kids up for ski lessons. Parents get some turns in while kids are learning, and then join up for some family time. Travelling with little ones? Daycare options are available slopeside. </p>
<h4>2. Tubing</h4>
<p>Whether your ski legs have turned to jelly or you’re looking for a different way to enjoy the slopes, try your hand at tubing at <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="65.0000" data-gtm_label="Mt. Norquay" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2098" href="https://banffnorquay.com/winter-2/tubing/" target="_blank">Mt. Norquay</a>. The good news is that you don’t need to walk back up the hill – a towing system gets you to the top for the next run!</p>

<h4>3. Banff Gondola </h4>
<p>A trip to the top of Sulphur Mountain is more than a gondola ride with lofty views. Also greeting you at the top are <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Sky Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2119" href="https://bll.fyi/w1qa" target="_blank">one-of-a-kind dining experiences</a> and an interpretive centre where you can learn more about the history, wildlife and landscapes of Banff National Park. Visit the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://bll.fyi/lhpm" target="_blank">Banff Gondola </a>at sunset to experience an Alpenglow. Stand at the top of the mountain while the sun sets and watch as the sky turns a striking pink and the peaks of the surrounding mountains are illuminated. </p>
<h4>4. Hiking</h4>
<p>In late spring, some hiking trails start to open up, so be sure to pack your hikers! Be sure to check trail conditions in advance, but this guide of <a href="/blog/10-early-season-hikes-banff-national-park">Early Season Hikes</a> should get you started on the right track.</p>

<h4>5. Local Eats and Après-Ski </h4>
<p>Whether you’ve got a pack of hungry teenagers or young, picky eaters, there is no shortage of <a href="/dining-nightlife">dining options in Banff and Lake Louise</a>. Dip into fondue together for a truly memorable meal, or enjoy some après-ski appetizers for the ultimate comfort food experience. </p>
<h4>6. Indoor Activities</h4>
<p>A great way to enjoy Banff as a family is to tuck into a museum or historic site to learn more about the incredible region you’re visiting. Check out the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Whyte Museum of the Canadian Rockies" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2459" href="http://www.whyte.org" target="_blank">Whyte Museum of the Canadian Rockies</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="3.9000" data-gtm_label="Banff Park Museum National Historic Site of Canada" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2614" href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/en/lhn-nhs/ab/banff" target="_blank">Banff Park Museum</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Buffalo Nations Luxton Museum" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2442" href="http://www.buffalonationsmuseum.com" target="_blank">Buffalo Nations Luxton Museum</a> or <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="3.9000" data-gtm_label="Cave &amp; Basin National Historic Site" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2496" href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/cave" target="_blank">Cave &amp; Basin National Historic Site</a> for some out-of-school learning they’ll truly enjoy. </p>
<h4>7. Snacks and Souvenirs</h4>
<p>What family vacation would be complete without some street-side indulgences and souvenirs? Stroll the streets, enjoy <a href="/shopping">Banff’s vibrant shopping scene</a>, follow your nose to a tasty treat (try <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Beavertails" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Fast Food" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2620" href="http://www.beavertailsinc.com/" target="_blank">Beaver Tails</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Sweet Shoppe" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2397" href="http://www.banffsweetshoppe.com" target="_blank">Banff Sweet Shoppe</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Skoki's Waffle and Frozen Yoghurt" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bakery &amp; Deli" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2687" href="http://www.skokis.ca" target="_blank">Skoki's</a>, and <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Mountain Chocolates" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2086" href="https://mountain-chocolates.business.site/" target="_blank">Mountain Chocolates</a>) and poke around for the perfect memento.</p>
