<div><h4>Banff Centre Mountain Film and Book Festival: October 30 - November 7, 2021</h4>
<p>Every fall, authors, photographers, adventurers, and filmmakers from around the world arrive in Banff ready to explore the best in adventure film and literature. With a rich history stretching back to 1976, the week-long festival hosted at the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Centre For Arts And Creativity" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2601" href="http://www.banffcentre.ca" target="_blank">Banff Centre for Arts and Creativity</a> has become the world’s premier destination for all things mountain culture.</p>
<p>This year the Festival will consist of a limited number of select in-person events, and will also feature events and screenings at <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="13.0000" data-gtm_label="Lux Cinema Centre" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Indoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2249" href="http://luxbanff.com/" target="_blank">Lux Cinema</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Whyte Museum of the Canadian Rockies" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2459" href="http://www.whyte.org" target="_blank">The Whyte Museum of the Canadian Rockies</a>, artsPlace Canmore, and other venues downtown Banff.</p>
<p>Back by popular demand, Virtual Festival films will be available and accessible from the comfort of your own home, anywhere in the world! </p>

<h4>Enjoy a Cozy Cabin Getaway </h4>
<p>Take the one you love and head into the mountains for a cozy getaway. Check out some of the great cozy cabins of Banff National Park where you'll be able to spend the day adventuring and then settle into your accommodation to enjoy a warm fire or an authentic mountain meal featuring regional cuisine.  For more inspiration, check out our <a href="/blog/have-cozy-cabin-holiday-banff-national-park">guide to a cozy cabin holiday.</a> </p>

<h4>Try Something New</h4>
<p>Not only does November mark the start of the ski season, but it's also the perfect time to try some other great mountain adventures. Join a guide and travel deep into the earth with <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="100.0000" data-gtm_label="Canmore Cave Tours" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2334" href="http://www.canmorecavetours.com" target="_blank">Canmore Cave Tours.</a> Explore ancient geological formations as you climb, slide and squeeze your way more than 100 feet underground. Or soar to new heights with <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Alpine Helicopters Inc" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="1965" href="http://www.alpinehelicopter.com/" target="_blank">Alpine Heli​copters</a> or <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Rockies Heli Tours Canada" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2212" href="http://www.rockiesheli.com" target="_blank">Rockies Heli</a> on an unforgettable scenic flight over the awe-inspiring Rocky Mountains. </p>

<h4>Taste for Adventure</h4>

You can find participating restaurants and contest details <a href="https://www.banfflakelouise.com/tasteforadventure">here</a>.</p>

<p>As the temperature starts to drop and snow settles on the mountains, take the time to slow down and reconnect with yourself. Multiple dates and retreats are available throughout the month.</p>
