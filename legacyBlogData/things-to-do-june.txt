<p>The water is warming and so is the air temperature. Banff National Park comes alive in June as rivers run with snowmelt, lakes fill with eager paddlers, wildlife abounds, and travellers set their sights on all this mountain destination has to offer.</p>
<p>Looking for ideas to make the most of your stay this month? Consider these:</p>
<h4><b>Get on the Water</b></h4>
<p>June is one of the Rockies’ best months for <a href="/paddling">water sports</a> while the snow up high is still melting. Some trails might still be snowbound, but on the water, your options are wide open so choose your vessel and enjoy the ride! Visit the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="92.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Canoe Club" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2528" href="https://bll.fyi/yb11" target="_blank">Banff Canoe Club</a> to rent a canoe, kayak, or stand-up paddleboard, enjoy <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="120.0000" data-gtm_label="Discover Banff Tours" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2380" href="https://www.banfftours.com/things-to-do/banff-whitewater-rafting/" target="_blank">whitewater rafting</a> with an experienced guide or a leisurely river raft with <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Rocky Mountain Raft Tours" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2030" href="http://www.banffrafttours.com" target="_blank">Rocky Mountain Raft Tours</a>. If cruising is more your style, the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="60.0000" data-gtm_label="Lake Minnewanka Cruise" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2384" href="https://bll.fyi/q9r5?utm_source=BLL&amp;utm_medium=PkgListings&amp;utm_campaign=BLL_Web13" target="_blank">Lake Minnewanka Cruise</a> offers four different options from the Classic Cruise to the Family Cruise and new Beer Voyage.</p>

<h4><b>Up the Ante with Activities</b></h4>
<p>It’s summertime and the livin’ is easy - though no less exciting thanks to some of the area’s top attractions. Take the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://bll.fyi/lhpm" target="_blank">Banff Gondola</a> ride up 2,292 feet to some of the best views in the area, indulge in a bite at <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://www.banffjaspercollection.com/attractions/banff-gondola/dining/peak-patio/" target="_blank">Peak Patio</a>, explore the interpretive exhibits, and more. Glaciers on your mind? Take a two-pronged adventure with the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="80.0000" data-gtm_label="Columbia Icefield Glacier Adventure" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2359" href="https://bll.fyi/4wl6?utm_source=BLL&amp;utm_medium=PkgListings&amp;utm_campaign=BLL_Web13" target="_blank">Columbia Icefield Adventure</a> and glass-bottomed <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="32.0000" data-gtm_label="Columbia Icefield Skywalk" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2540" href="https://padlock.link/rlfx" target="_blank">Glacier Skywalk</a>.</p>

<h4>Dine with the Locals</h4>
<p>Banff and Lake Louise have more than enough <a href="/dining-nightlife">restaurants</a> to choose from, many featuring <a href="/blog/9-patios-you-need-try-summer-banff-lake-louise">lively patios</a> and exquisite views. If you're looking to dine with a view, <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Juniper Hotel Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2753" href="https://bll.fyi/kvse" target="_blank">Juniper Bistro</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Magpie &amp; Stump and Cantina" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2252" href="https://www.magpieandstump.ca/banff-patio-restaurant" target="_blank">El Patio by Magpie &amp; Stump</a>, and the <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Waldhaus Pub &amp; Patio" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2483" href="http://www.fairmont.com/banff-springs/dining/waldhauspub/?genid=waldhauspubandpatio_bsh_blltdining&amp;utm_source=waldhauspubandpatio&amp;utm_medium=paid_referrer&amp;utm_content=bsh&amp;utm_campaign=blltdining" target="_blank">Waldhaus Pub &amp; Patio</a> are some local favourites. If you're around Lake Louise, enjoy your outdoor surroundings and a cocktail at <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Baker Creek Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2902" href="http://www.bakercreek.com" target="_blank">Baker Creek Bistro</a> or <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Deer Lodge" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2369" href="https://crmr.com/resorts/deer-lodge/" target="_blank">Deer Lodge</a>. <h4><b>Soar through the Air</b></h4>
<p>One of the most unique ways of seeing the park is from a birds-eye perspective. <a href="/hiking/heli-hiking">Heli-sightseeing</a> gets you up high for sightseeing at its best. With snow still on the mountain tops and lakes below displaying incredible shades of blue, June is a perfect month to book a helicopter tour. Fly with <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Alpine Helicopters Inc" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="1965" href="http://www.alpinehelicopter.com/" target="_blank">Alpine Helicopters</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="CMH - Summer Adventures" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2547" href="https://www.cmhsummer.com/" target="_blank">CMH</a>, or <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Rockies Heli Tours Canada" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2212" href="http://www.rockiesheli.com" target="_blank">Rockies Heli Tours</a>. </p>

<h4><b>Go Wild for Wildlife</b></h4>
<p>There’s a good chance you’ll see <a href="/blog/wildlife-banff-national-park">wildlife</a> just going about your daily activities in Banff and Lake Louise. Even close to town, along the <a data-gtm_category="Services" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Parks Canada" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Community &amp; Government" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2548" href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/en/pn-np/ab/banff/activ/randonee-hiking/banff" target="_blank">Fenland Trail and Vermilion Lakes Road</a>, it’s common to see deer, elk, herons, eagles, loons, and more. (Remember to always keep a safe distance from wildlife and carry bear spray when you explore the area's trails.)</p>
<p>If you’re looking to increase your chances, join an <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="120.0000" data-gtm_label="Discover Banff Tours" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2380" href="https://dashboard.ventrata.com/checkout/en/evening-wildlife-safari-1-1?_ga=2.249133575.141512699.1622105503-1229507581.1604549917&amp;checkout=af60a542-fdba-4596-8360-83e8a5af063c" target="_blank">Evening Wildlife Safari</a> to learn more about the animals that call the park home, and see them in their natural environments. The <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="32.9500" data-gtm_label="The Lake Louise Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2238" href="https://www.skilouise.com/explore-summer/" target="_blank">Lake Louise Gondola</a> is also a great place to spot grizzly bears ambling along the grassy slopes.</p>

<p><strong>Local Tip:</strong> Thanks to a number of public transit options it has never been easier to get around Banff National Park. You can now travel from Banff to Canmore, the Lake Minnewanka Loop, Johnston Canyon, Bow Valley Parkway, and Lake Louise with <a href="https://roamtransit.com/" target="_blank">Roam Transit</a>. If you are travelling to Lake Louise, there are <a href="/explorethepark/transit-and-shuttles">various shuttle options</a> to get you to the Lake Louise lakeshore, Moraine Lake and the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="32.9500" data-gtm_label="The Lake Louise Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2238" href="https://www.skilouise.com/explore-summer/" target="_blank">Lake Louise Gondola</a>. For all transit and shuttle options visit <a href="https://www.banfflakelouise.com/explorethepark">explorethepark.ca</a>. </p>
