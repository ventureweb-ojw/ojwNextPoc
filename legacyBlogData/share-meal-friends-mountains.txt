<div><p>Immerse yourself in the mountain lifestyle by slowing down, taking a deep breath of fresh mountain air and of course, sharing a meal with friends. While your day will be packed with fun and adventurous activities, there’s no time like the present to take a break, connect with companions and enjoy a locally crafted communal meal in Banff and Lake Louise. </p>
<h4>Inspired Tapas at El Toro </h4>
<p>Tapas, coming from the Spanish tradition of sharing small plates, is perfectly rendered by chefs at <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="El Toro Restaurant" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2266" href="https://www.eltorobanff.com/our-menu/tapas/ " target="_blank">El Toro</a> who specialize in a cuisine with Spanish, Mexican, and Canadian roots. Gather with your friends and family over a bottle of wine to pair with their extensive Tapas menu ranging from their beef tartar to Sriracha and cream prawns, you’re sure to find a favourite (if not more than one!).</p>

<h4>Campfire Cooking at Park Distillery</h4>
<p>There’s nothing quite like gathering around a fire, roasting s’mores, drinking cowboy coffee and sharing stories of a day well spent with friends. You’re in luck with <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Park Distillery Restaurant &amp; Bar" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2671" href="http://parkdistillery.com" target="_blank">Park Distillery</a> where you can share dinner over a picnic table complete with a charcuterie board and house-distilled small-batch spirits.</p>

<h4>Wine and Charcuterie at Grapes </h4>
<p><a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Banff Springs" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2473" href="http://www.fairmont.com/banff-springs" target="_blank">Fairmont Banff Spring</a>’s <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Grapes Wine Bar" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2479" href="http://www.fairmont.com/banff-springs/dining/grapeswinebar/?genid=grapeswinebar_bsh_blltdining&amp;utm_source=grapeswinebar&amp;utm_medium=paid_referrer&amp;utm_content=bsh&amp;utm_campaign=blltdining" target="_blank">Grapes Wine Bar</a> is the perfect place to indulge in a large wine selection with your group while savouring curated charcuterie boards topped with preserves and artisan cheeses. Almost everything served is made in-house from their artisanal baked breads to their cured meats, it’s sure to be one of a kind. </p>
<h4>Alberta inspired plates at Chuck’s</h4>
<p>A trip to the Wild West isn’t complete without an authentic Alberta Beef experience. <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Chuck's Steakhouse Ltd." data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2750" href="http://www.chuckssteakhouse.ca/" target="_blank">Chuck’s Steakhouse</a> grills each steak over hardwood and mesquite then finishes it under an 1800 degree broiler – serving it on a plate to share! Choose your sides à la carte and create your very own custom meal.  </p>

<h4>Family style meals like the Greeks at The Balkan </h4>
<p>Immerse yourself in the rich Greek traditions <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Balkan the Greek Restaurant" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="1982" href="http://www.banffbalkan.ca" target="_blank">Balkan</a> brings to Banff. Serving up a family-style meal you can taste Greek favourites like horitiki salad, spanakopita, and baklava rolls. After your meal, delight in the dance performed by local artists Hala Jamal &amp; Mesa.</p>
