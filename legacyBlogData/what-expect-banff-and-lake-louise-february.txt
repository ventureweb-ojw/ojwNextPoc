<div><p paraeid="{eb6a9642-14f6-43b8-b06d-06569397999a}{175}" paraid="761812816">Find romance in the Rockies in February. Whether you’re on a path of self-discovery or looking to spend some time reconnecting with your significant other away from the hustle and bustle, Banff and Lake Louise offer up the right balance of solitude and social experiences. </p>
<p paraeid="{eb6a9642-14f6-43b8-b06d-06569397999a}{181}" paraid="384455318">From candle-lit dinners to hot springs and spas, there’s plenty of relaxation to indulge in—and once you’re wrapped in a cozy robe, you won’t want to leave! </p>
<p paraeid="{eb6a9642-14f6-43b8-b06d-06569397999a}{191}" paraid="1499439090">Here’s what you can expect to see, do, and experience in Banff and Lake Louise in February.  </p>

<h4 paraeid="{eb6a9642-14f6-43b8-b06d-06569397999a}{191}" paraid="1499439090">The Weather</h4>
<p paraeid="{eb6a9642-14f6-43b8-b06d-06569397999a}{215}" paraid="2000254079">Similar to <a href="/blog/things-do-banff-and-lake-louise-january">January</a>, February is the middle of our winter season here in Alberta.  </p>
<p paraeid="{eb6a9642-14f6-43b8-b06d-06569397999a}{223}" paraid="1916213045">You will likely arrive to cold temperatures with a good base of snow. Ideal for skiing, snowboarding, snowshoeing, and winter hiking, you’ll find plenty of ways to explore the mountains at this time of year. </p>
<p paraeid="{eb6a9642-14f6-43b8-b06d-06569397999a}{233}" paraid="222155511">Banff Daytime 0°C (32°F)  </p>
<p paraeid="{eb6a9642-14f6-43b8-b06d-06569397999a}{245}" paraid="352059076">Banff Overnight -12°C (10.4°F)  </p>
<p paraeid="{eb6a9642-14f6-43b8-b06d-06569397999a}{253}" paraid="577329537">Lake Louise Daytime -1°C (30.2°F) </p>
<p paraeid="{e06800c1-1485-4222-815c-fbc757b84615}{6}" paraid="1050377078">Lake Louise Overnight -17°C (1.4°F)  </p>

<h4 paraeid="{e06800c1-1485-4222-815c-fbc757b84615}{6}" paraid="1050377078">What to Pack</h4>
<p paraeid="{e06800c1-1485-4222-815c-fbc757b84615}{28}" paraid="648975855">For the most enjoyable experience—despite the winter temperatures—wear layers.  </p>
<p paraeid="{e06800c1-1485-4222-815c-fbc757b84615}{34}" paraid="314227925">You’ll see most locals and travellers alike dressed in winter coats, boots, mitts, toques, and scarves while around town. Out in the mountains, they sport snow pants and long underwear.  </p>
<p paraeid="{e06800c1-1485-4222-815c-fbc757b84615}{44}" paraid="1849364735">If you’re not accustomed to cold weather, be sure that you bring more layers than you think you might need, along with extra dry clothing. As you warm with activity, you may sweat and need to remove layers to stay dry, then re-add them as you cool down. </p>
<p paraeid="{e06800c1-1485-4222-815c-fbc757b84615}{54}" paraid="748644244">Water-resistant boots, pants, jackets, and gloves are all vital if you’re out playing in the snow.  </p>

<h4 paraeid="{e06800c1-1485-4222-815c-fbc757b84615}{54}" paraid="748644244">What to See and Do</h4>
<p paraeid="{e06800c1-1485-4222-815c-fbc757b84615}{82}" paraid="1309196714">Valentine’s day falls mid-month and brings couples from all over the world. Banff and Lake Louise is an inclusive destination that welcomes all with open arms.  </p>
<p paraeid="{e06800c1-1485-4222-815c-fbc757b84615}{88}" paraid="1090158025">If you’re looking to celebrate someone special, book a room with a mountain view, head to the <a href="/banff-hot-springs">hot springs</a>, and finish the day at one of our day <a href="/spa-and-treatments">spas</a>. Options for facials, scrubs, massages, and more await you.  </p>

<p paraeid="{e06800c1-1485-4222-815c-fbc757b84615}{98}" paraid="254260931">After a relaxing day, you’ll want to make a reservation for dinner, drinks, and dessert. Banff and Lake Louise are well-known for hand-crafted, Alberta-made spirits and beers and farm-to-table meals.  </p>
<p paraeid="{e06800c1-1485-4222-815c-fbc757b84615}{112}" paraid="910941069">Outside of Valentine’s Day festivities, there are plenty of ways to explore the area. Since the snow cover is so good, the opportunity for wildlife spotting reappears. Keep an eye out for tracks along roadsides, particularly at dawn and dusk, to view some of the animals active during the winter months, like elk, fox, and wolves. Observe the powerlines for a wide variety of birds like House Sparrows, Clark’s Nutcrackers, Pygmy Owls, and Sharp-shinned Hawks. </p>
<p paraeid="{e06800c1-1485-4222-815c-fbc757b84615}{122}" paraid="159743447">If you’re lucky enough to spot animals on your visit, please keep your distance. </p>
