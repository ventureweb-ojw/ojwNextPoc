<div><h4>1. "Learn To" Day at Lake Louise Ski Resort </h4>
<p>Try your hand at something new during the “Learn To" Day at the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="100.0000" data-gtm_label="The Lake Louise Ski Resort" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2603" href="https://bll.fyi/r495" target="_blank">Lake Louise Ski Resort</a> on December 1st, 2019. Watch the world’s fastest skiers compete in World Cup action and then try out your favourite winter sports, including downhill skiing, snowshoeing, and nordic skiing. Don’t forget that kids ski for free (with the purchase of an adult ticket) during Lake Louise World Cup weekend - so bring the little ones and hit the slopes!   </p>

<h4>2. Ski With Santa on Christmas Day </h4>
<p>Don your best Christmas character outfit (think Santa Claus, Mrs. Claus, or an elf), grab your free lift ticket, and partake in a Banff local tradition at Mt Norquay. <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="65.0000" data-gtm_label="Mt. Norquay" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2098" href="https://bll.fyi/2w1t9amo" target="_blank">Santa Shredz For Free</a> returns on December 25, 2019 - the only catch is that you need to be in full costumes... Santa hats alone won’t qualify you for the free lift ticket, so get crazy with it! </p>

<h4>3. Try Your Hand at Apres Ski   </h4>
<p>Indulge in an exquisitely prepared meal or join the locals on the après scene - with over<a href="/dining-nightlife"> 130 bars and restaurants</a> to choose from, there will be something for everyone in your group to enjoy. Discover the taste of Banff and Lake Louise by sampling some local craft beer and spirits or try an exotic cut of game meat. </p>

<h4>4. Not A Skier? Not a problem. </h4>
<p>If you love to ski, but someone in your group does not - don’t worry, Banff and Lake Louise offers a whole bunch of <a href="/winter">great winter adventures</a> to keep everyone excited about the snow! Looking for something unique? Check out scenic walking tours through frozen canyons, try your hand at tubing or join a lift-assisted guided snowshoe tour at <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="105.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Sunshine Village" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2143" href="https://bll.fyi/32m3r6w9" target="_blank">Banff Sunshine</a> or <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="100.0000" data-gtm_label="The Lake Louise Ski Resort" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2603" href="https://bll.fyi/sz6luyf0" target="_blank">Lake Louise ski resort</a>. Embrace the best of winter!   </p>

<h4>5. Christmas Torchlight Parade at Sunshine Mountain Lodge</h4>
<p>Guests of<a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="250.0000" data-gtm_label="Sunshine Mountain Lodge" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2498" href="http://www.sunshinemountainlodge.com/" target="_blank"> Sunshine Mountain Lodge</a> will enjoy a unique holiday spectacle with the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="105.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Sunshine Village" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2143" href="https://bll.fyi/0f32210p" target="_blank">torchlight parade</a> on December 24, 2019. Cozy up with a hot chocolate and watch as trained torchbearers take to the slopes after dark, painting their way down the slopes of Strawberry chair.  </p>

<h4>6. Magical Christmas Feast at Lake Louise Ski Resort</h4>
<p>Spend your Christmas eve at Lake Louise Ski Resort’s cozy Lodge of the Ten Peaks and partake in a holiday feast to remember. Enjoy a turkey dinner with all the fixings, try out night tubing, and get your chance to see Santa Claus himself! Tickets are limited and available now for purchase. </p>
<h4>7. The Holiday Season Is Here </h4>
<p>Starting in mid-November, Banff and Lake Louise come alive with holiday spirit! Don’t miss the iconic traditions, like <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Banff Springs" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2473" href="https://www.christmasatthecastle.ca/" target="_blank">Christmas at the Castle</a> at <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Banff Springs" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2473" href="http://www.fairmont.com/banff-springs" target="_blank">Banff Springs Hotel</a> and <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://bll.fyi/l750gnt6" target="_blank">Mountaintop Christmas</a> at the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://bll.fyi/l750gnt6" target="_blank">Banff Gondola</a>. The list of activities is as long as Santa’s “Nice” list, so check out <a href="/Christmas">Christmas in Banff and Lake Louise</a> for more ideas.  </p>
