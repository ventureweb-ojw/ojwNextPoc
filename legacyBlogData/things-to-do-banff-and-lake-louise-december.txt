<h4>Women's Alpine Ski World Cup and Super G - Nov. 30 - Dec. 5</h4>
<p>Skiing in Banff and Lake Louise is in full swing! As is tradition, the first weekend in December brings some of the finest alpine racers to the slopes of the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="100.0000" data-gtm_label="The Lake Louise Ski Resort" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2603" href="https://bll.fyi/gpj0" target="_blank">Lake Louise Ski Resort</a>. Get a few laps in and then join thousands of spectators to watch the first <a href="http://www.lakelouisealpineskiworldcup.com">Audi FIS Ski World Cup Women's Downhill and Super-G</a> races of the season.  </p>

<h4>Holiday Celebrations on Bear Street</h4>
<p>The Bear Street Holiday Market, Hot Chocolate Trail, and the Banff Craft Beer Festival are all taking over Bear Street during December.</p>
<p>Explore a European-inspired Christmas market at the <a href="/christmas/bear-street-holiday-market">Bear Street Holiday Market</a>. New vendors every weekend and daily hours for the week leading into Christmas, shopping on Bear Street has never been easier. Plus, you can always stop into one of the great storefronts as well.</p>
<p>The <a href="/banff-craft-beer-festival">Banff Craft Beer Festival</a> takes place from December 3-11 and is the only festival featuring exclusively Albertan brewers. Enjoy local brews and local eats all while exploring the brand new Bear Street.</p>
<p><a href="/christmas/hot-chocolate-trail">Banff's Hot Chocolate Trail</a> is an exploration of this classic holiday drink mixed with creativity and exploration of new flavours and classic styles. A number of restaurants are participating this year including <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Bear Street Tavern" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2155" href="http://www.bearstreettavern.ca" target="_blank">Bear Street Tavern</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Wild Flour Bakery" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Coffee Houses &amp; Tea Rooms" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2797" href="http://www.wildflourbakery.ca" target="_blank">Wild Flour</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Three Bears Brewery and Restaurant" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2950" href="https://threebearsbanff.com/" target="_blank">Three Bears Brewery and Restaurant</a>, and <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Magpie &amp; Stump" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2252" href="http://www.magpieandstump.ca" target="_blank">Magpie &amp; Stump</a>.</p>

<h4>Enjoy a Mountaintop Christmas at Banff Gondola</h4>
<p>Ride to the top of Sulphur Mountain and visit Santa's Workshop, enjoy art and craft activities, or take a family Christmas photo. As the sun goes down and the stars come out, take a blanket and cozy up around the fire on the rooftop and enjoy an evening exploring the galaxies with a professional photographer on hand to help you get the perfect shot. All activities are free with <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://bll.fyi/lhpm" target="_blank">Banff Gondola</a> admission.</p>
