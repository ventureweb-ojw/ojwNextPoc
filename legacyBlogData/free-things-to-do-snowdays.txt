<div><p><em>Please Note: Snowdays will still be taking place in 2021 but will look a little different this season. Stay tuned for more details.</em></p>
<p>Every January, visitors from around the world celebrate the magic of winter at <a href="/snowdays">SnowDays</a> in Banff and Lake Louise. Join us for this year’s 12-day event, which promises to be bigger and better than ever. Embrace winter and rejoice your inner child with these free outdoor happenings!</p>
<h4>1. Vote in the Snow Sculpture Competition </h4>
<p><strong>When</strong>: January 16 - 18 <br/>
<strong>Where</strong>: Bear Street</p>
<p>In the heart of downtown Banff, professional snow sculptors compete for 1st, 2nd, and People’s Choice awards. Bear Street transforms into a pedestrian-only zone, so you can see these enormous sculptures up close and personal while they are being carved.<br/>
Peoples' Choice Vote is January 18 from 4 p.m. - 6 p.m. Winners will be announced on January 18 at 6 p.m. </p>
<p><a class="btn" href="https://www.banfflakelouise.com/SnowSculptures">Find out more</a></p>
<h4>2. Wander the Snow Sculpture Exhibition</h4>
<p><strong>When</strong>: January 15 - 26<br/>
<strong>Where</strong>: Bear Street, Banff High School, <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="105.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Sunshine Village" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2143" href="https://bll.fyi/1i95" target="_blank">Banff Sunshine Village</a> and <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="65.0000" data-gtm_label="Mt. Norquay" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2098" href="https://bll.fyi/9jrv" target="_blank">Mt. Norquay</a></p>
<p>Missed the competition? Don’t worry, this exhibit lasts 12 full days. Come see the sculptures when they are complete, warm up around the fire, and wander the shops along the way. We recommend viewing these masterpieces at night, when they are all lit up. </p>

<h4>3. Experience the International Ice Carving Competition</h4>
<p><strong>When</strong>: January 15 - 17 <br/>
<strong>Where</strong>: <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2474" href="http://www.fairmont.com/lake-louise/" target="_blank">Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise</a> </p>
<p>A crowd favourite, this frosty <a href="/snowdays/ice-magic">competition </a>returns to Lake Louise in 2020. Come watch world-renowned carvers chip and carve away at blocks of ice to create imaginative pieces. <br/>
The winning team will be announced on January 17 at 5 p.m. <br/>
*Admission fees apply over weekends and peak-times. Find more details on <a href="/snowdays/ice-magic">admission</a>.</p>

<h4>7. Experience the Thrill of Skijoring</h4>
<p><strong>When</strong>: January 19, 2 p.m. - 4 p.m.<br/>
<strong>Where</strong>: Banff Avenue </p>
<p>Welcome to the Wild West! Skijoring was featured in the original 1917 Banff Winter Carnival and contributed to Banff’s reputation as one of the world’s premier winter sport destinations. Don't miss this thrilling demonstration, which follows a skiing cowboy drawn by a galloping horse. </p>
