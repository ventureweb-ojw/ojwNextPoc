<div><p>The sun can't shine every day in Banff National Park, but don't let that slow you down!</p>
<p>Here’s our complete guide to the best indoor activities in Banff and Lake Louise. </p>
<h4>1. Check out a museum  </h4>
<p>You’ve soaked in the panoramic mountain views, now learn about the iconic people who shaped the Bow Valley at <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Whyte Museum of the Canadian Rockies" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2459" href="http://www.whyte.org" target="_blank">The Whyte Museum Of The Canadian Rockies</a> and <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="The Historic Luxton Home" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2793" href="http://www.luxtonfoundation.org" target="_blank">The Historic Luxton Home</a>. Explore the origins of Banff National Park and the Canadian national park system at <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="3.9000" data-gtm_label="Cave &amp; Basin National Historic Site" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2496" href="http://pc.gc.ca/en/lhn-nhs/ab/caveandbasin" target="_blank">The Cave and Basin</a> or <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="3.9000" data-gtm_label="Banff Park Museum National Historic Site of Canada" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2614" href="http://parkscanada.gc.ca/banffparkmuseum" target="_blank">Banff Park Museum</a>. Discover the rich history of First Nations at <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Buffalo Nations Luxton Museum" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2442" href="http://www.buffalonationsmuseum.com" target="_blank">The Buffalo Nations Luxton Museum</a>. *Please check museum websites for the most up to date information on their hours and dates of operation</p>

<h4>2. Hit the Bowling alley </h4>
<p>Slip on your bowling shoes and check out the bowling alleys of Banff! <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Banff Springs" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2473" href="http://www.fairmont.com/banff-springs/activities-services/bowling-entertainment/" target="_blank">The Fairmont Banff Springs</a> hotel has bowling lanes available on-site as well as a new high-definition golf simulator. If you’re looking for the classic combo of pizza and bowling, you’ll need to check out <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="High Rollers" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2752" href="http://www.highrollersbanff.com" target="_blank">High Rollers</a>. With over 50 beers on tap, everyone will have a great time on the lane.   </p>

<h4>3. Make an escape</h4>
<p>Looking for a challenge? Banff is home to the Bow Valley's only Escape Room at <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="30.0000" data-gtm_label="EureKa Banff" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Indoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2742" href="http://www.eurekabanff.ca/" target="_blank">Eureka Banff Escape Room Adventures</a>. Eureka is a fully immersive interactive puzzle room where you will need to work with your team to complete the set objective in under one hour to win. The two rooms are themed and designed completely differently, with their own objective.</p>

<h4>4. Take in an art gallery    </h4>
<p>Banff and Lake Louise have several galleries with impressive art collections and exhibits that will not disappoint. <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="20.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Live Theatre" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2788" href="http://www.carter-ryan.com/" target="_blank">Carter-Ryan Gallery</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Canada House Gallery" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts &amp; Galleries" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2326" href="http://www.canadahouse.com/">Canada House Gallery</a>, and <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Willock &amp; Sax Gallery" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts &amp; Galleries" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2466" href="http://www.willockandsaxgallery.com/">Willock and Sax Gallery</a> are just some of the spots to see beautiful paintings, sculptures, and a myriad of other original works. For a full list of galleries, <a href="https://www.banfflakelouise.com/business-directory/retail/arts-galleries">check out our gallery listings</a>.</p>

<h4>5. Go caving     </h4>
<p>While not your traditional “indoor activity”, don’t miss your chance to head underground and explore the subterranean world beneath Grotto Mountain.
