<div><p>Banff National Park is abuzz in July, from pavement to footpath. The scenery is stunning, the temps pleasant, and the vibe alive with festive events and a killer culinary scene, making it a great time of year to visit. Here are five tips to get you started in discovering the best of Banff National Park.</p>
<p><strong>Local Tip:</strong> Thanks to a number of public transit options it has never been easier to get around Banff National Park. You can now travel from Banff to Canmore, the Lake Minnewanka Loop, Johnston Canyon, Bow Valley Parkway, and Lake Louise with <a href="https://roamtransit.com/">Roam Transit</a>. If you are traveling to Lake Louise, there are <a href="https://www.banfflakelouise.com/explorethepark">various shuttle options</a> to get you to the Lake Louise lakeshore, Moraine Lake, and <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="100.0000" data-gtm_label="The Lake Louise Ski Resort" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2603" href="https://www.skilouise.com/explore-summer/sightseeing-gondola/" target="_blank">The Lake Louise Ski Resort</a>. </p>
<h4>1. Take to a Mountain Trail</h4>
<p>While the most popular trails can see a lot of foot traffic, getting <a href="/blog/7-beaten-path-adventures-banff-national-park" target="_blank">off the beaten path</a> is easy and brings your own slice of tranquility. Make a day of it and <a href="/blog/perfecting-classic-mountain-picnic-banff-national-park" target="_blank">plan a mountain picnic</a>! Adding a few extra luxuries to your daypack will create a truly unique experience – we’re thinking summit charcuterie or alpine meadow bubblies. <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Nesters Market" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2532" href="http://www.nestersmarket.com/banff/" target="_blank">Nester’s Market</a> and <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Canada Safeway" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2327" href="http://west.iga.ca/stores/iga-banff/" target="_blank">IGA</a> in Banff or <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="The Village Market" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2174" href="https://www.banfflakelouise.com/shopping/village-market">The Village Market</a> in Lake Louise will supply all of your picnic essentials. Just remember to pack out what you pack in.</p>

<h4>2. Soak in World-Class Sights</h4>
<p>The local summits are just a gondola (or chairlift) ride away. Enjoy the daily <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://www.banffjaspercollection.com/attractions/banff-gondola/events/" target="_blank">Sunset Festival</a> at the Banff Gondola starting July 9th. Indulge in local craft beverages, taste a Canadian-inspired BBQ menu, and soak in the spectacular mountain sunsets with live music and entertainment. You can also visit <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="100.0000" data-gtm_label="The Lake Louise Ski Resort" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2603" href="https://www.skilouise.com/explore-summer/sightseeing-gondola/" target="_blank">The Lake Louise Ski Resort</a> for world-class vistas and a chance to spot a grizzly bear in its natural habitat or experience <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="29.9500" data-gtm_label="Banff Norquay Sightseeing Chairlift" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2613" href="https://banffnorquay.com/summer/sightseeing/" target="_blank">Mt. Norquay’s Sightseeing Chairlift</a> which provides a stunning bird’s eye view of the town of Banff.</p>

<h4>3. Amp Up the Adventure</h4>
<p>Looking for an alternative adventure or extra dose of adrenaline? Scale a mountain face with an ACMG guide on <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="250.0000" data-gtm_label="Mt Norquay's Via Ferrata" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2609" href="https://bll.fyi/2fyw" target="_blank">Mt. Norquay’s Via Ferrata</a>. Or, discover a different form of mountain exploration via <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Trail Riders" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2205" href="https://bll.fyi/pyft" target="_blank">horseback ride</a>, which can bring you to parts of the backcountry you never dreamed possible. Cool off with a <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="120.0000" data-gtm_label="Discover Banff Tours" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2380" href="https://www.banfftours.com/things-to-do/banff-whitewater-rafting/" target="_blank">whitewater rafting trip</a>, with all classes of rapids accessible from right within the heart of Banff to a few short hours’ drive away. If you want to delve beneath the Rockies, try a <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="100.0000" data-gtm_label="Canmore Cave Tours" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2334" href="http://www.canmorecavetours.com" target="_blank">cave tour</a> where you will discover the negative vertical world below.</p>

<h4>4. Engage in a Local Event</h4>
<p>While experiences in the mountains are a great way to unwind, they pair great with the excitement to be found in Banff National Park’s two vibrant towns. Pick up and picnic from any of the world-renowned restaurants in Banff and enjoy your takeout in the open green spaces of Central Park. To enhance your afternoon, picnic games are available to borrow in Central Park from 11:00 am - 4:00 pm every Saturday and Sunday throughout the summer.</p>
<p>Every Wednesday, you can also check out the <a data-gtm_category="Services" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Farmers Market" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Events &amp; Entertainment" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2001" href="https://www.thebanfffarmersmarket.com/" target="_blank">Banff Farmers Market</a>: a unique Farmers' Market offering guests a wide selection of quality locally made products. The market provides an excellent selection of over 80 local and regional vendors selling fruits, vegetables, artwork, jewelry, apparel, beauty products, coffee, and much more. Pick up something fresh and local on your next mid-week journey to Banff.</p>

<h4>5. Enjoy a Taste of Place</h4>
<p>If you haven’t gotten your fill of the mountain vistas by day’s end, continue the indulgence over dinner with a view. <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Sky Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2119" href="https://bll.fyi/w1qa" target="_blank">Sky Bistro</a> and the <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Cliff House Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2612" href="https://summer.banffnorquay.com/cliffhouse-bistro/" target="_blank">Cliffhouse Bistro</a> offer fine mountaintop dining, and an abundance of <a href="/blog/banff-national-park-rooftop-patios-where-you-should-chill-summer">rooftop and viewpoint patios</a> are scattered throughout the town of Banff.</p>
<p>As if the views weren’t enough, several <a href="/dining-nightlife">local restaurants</a> focus on locally sourced and home-crafted menus highlighting the flavours of the region. Enjoy a top-grade steak from the prairies of Southern Alberta at <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Chuck's Steakhouse Ltd." data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2750" href="http://www.chuckssteakhouse.ca/" target="_blank">Chuck’s Steakhouse</a> or a glass of fine merlot sourced from a B.C. vineyard all as you soak in your surroundings ‘til last light.</p>
