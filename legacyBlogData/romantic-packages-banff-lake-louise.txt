<div><p>It doesn't need to be Valentine's Day for you to find romance in Banff and Lake Louise! </p>
<p>Plan your future trip and take advantage of the many accommodation packages that invite you to unwind and enjoy your escape together. Whether you're seeking a relaxing retreat or a blissful base for adventures, you and your loved one are sure to find an experience that suits your idea of romance.</p>

<p><a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Moose Hotel &amp; Suites" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2757" href="https://www.bestofbanff.com/" target="_blank">Banff Lodging Company</a> offers a range of accommodation options complemented by a range of specials to suit your special getaway. Try the Moose <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Moose Hotel &amp; Suites" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2757" href="https://moosehotelandsuites.com/specials/" target="_blank">Forget-Me-Not Package</a>, which features spa credit and ‘wine down’ time with a glass of red or white wine, or celebrate the season with a <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Moose Hotel &amp; Suites" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2757" href="https://www.bestofbanff.com/specials/" target="_blank">Winter Warmer Package</a>. Each of these specials features accommodation in one of their condo-style properties, a bottle of wine, and a s’mores package to add a sweet touch to your stay. We think you’ll agree: it’s easier to appreciate the snow from the comfort of your cozy condo.</p>

<h4>2. Disconnect to reconnect </h4>
<p>Step back in time and enjoy a romantic evening in one of <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="250.0000" data-gtm_label="Baker Creek Mountain Resorts" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="1981" href="http://www.bakercreek.com" target="_blank">Baker Creek Mountain Resort’s</a> Heritage Trapper Cabins. When booking their “Lights Out Package” you can enjoy a digital detox featuring no cell service, WiFi, or electricity. Settle into your cozy cabin lit by storm lanterns and candles, and disengage from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Spend the evening snuggling by the wood-burning fireplace or step outside for a clear view of the stars and mountains surrounding you. Disconnect from the rest of the world and rediscover your connection to one another!</p>
