<div><p>As you walk to your cabin, you realize it is everything you have been dreaming of. </p>
<p>The wooden walls promise you a snug mountain escape, the light glows through the curtains and the deep blue of a mountain evening lingers all around. Walking through the door, you’re greeted by a picture-perfect interior, the warmth of a crackling fire and the overwhelming feeling that you are at home.</p>
<p>Your tasks for the next few days are straightforward: embrace a hearty day of adventure and then return to your cozy cabin and snuggle down by the fire. With a variety of cozy cabin-style accommodations in Banff National Park, you’ll finally find a place where you can escape from the rush of a modern world and harken back to a more simple time. </p>
<p>So where do you begin?</p>
<h4>FRONTCOUNTRY LODGES</h4>
<p>Frontcountry cabins are holiday chalet-style. Simply drive your vehicle to the front door and use your cabin as a cozy basecamp for local adventures. Frontcountry cabins offer a perfect balance of easy access, while also providing the feeling of being off the beaten path. These lodges are great for families, with many offering more space, access to a kitchenette, and plenty of adventures at your doorstep. </p>
<p><strong>Baker Creek Mountain Resort</strong> - 15 minutes from Lake Louise (or 45 minutes from Banff), <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="250.0000" data-gtm_label="Baker Creek Mountain Resorts" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="1981" href="http://www.bakercreek.com" target="_blank">Baker Creek Mountain Resort</a> offers both single cabin and lodge suite accommodations. For an extra cozy stay, try their "Lights Out Package" in one of their Heritage Trapper Cabins.</p>

<h4>BACKCOUNTRY LODGES</h4>
<p>Strap on a pair of snowshoes or hiking boots and make the journey to your backcountry lodge. Although the trip may provide a little more challenging and you can expect to spend the day on the trail, it's all part of a mountain adventure filled with unparalleled solitude.  </p>
<p><strong>Skoki Lodge</strong> - 11 kilometres on skis will bring you to historic <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Skoki Lodge" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Backcountry Lodges" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2057" href="http://www.skoki.com" target="_blank">Skoki Lodge,</a> built in 1931 as a destination for backcountry skiers. </p>
<p><strong>Sundance Lodge </strong> - 10 kilometres on horseback or skis will bring you to the door of <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Sundance Lodge" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Backcountry Lodges" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2600" href="https://bll.fyi/pyft" target="_blank">Sundance Lodge</a>. Enjoy fresh baked goods in a solar-powered lodge that oozes cabin charm.</p>
