<div><p>It's no secret that Canada can be a cold country, and there's no better place to enjoy the icy adventures than in Banff and Lake Louise.</p>
<p>Taking advantage of the best activities to do with ice, here's a list of the top 5 must-try ice adventures! </p>
<h4>Ice Skating </h4>
<p>Constantly ranked among the world's most beautiful skating rinks, Lake Louise is the quintessential Canadian spot for the best skating. Complete with an ice castle and ice rinks that are groomed and made smooth daily, Lake Louise is also a prime location for a game of hockey. Check out our <a href="/blog/8toskate">guide to Ice Skating in Banff and Lake Louise</a> for more ideas! </p>
<h4>Ice Walks </h4>
<p>Hiking in winter is made possible with snowshoes and ice cleats which are available for rent throughout Banff and Lake Louise. The <a href="/johnston-canyon">Johnston Canyon</a> ice walk is a popular winter hike that takes visitors to two sets of frozen waterfalls through a sparkling, Narnia-like canyon. Enjoy this adventure with cleat rentals or through an interpretive experience with <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="White Mountain Adventures" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2458" href="https://www.whitemountainadventures.com/johnston-canyon-icewalk" target="_blank">White Mountain Adventures</a> or <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="120.0000" data-gtm_label="Discover Banff Tours" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2380" href="https://www.banfftours.com/activities/johnston-canyon/" target="_blank">Discover Banff Tours.</a> For a unique take on the Johnston Canyon experience, try the evening Icewalk.  </p>

<h4>Ice Fishing </h4>
<p>Perhaps one of the most Canadian things to do in winter is to go ice fishing. <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Hawgwild Flyfishing Guides" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2308" href="http://www.BanffIceFishingGuides.com" target="_blank">Hawgwild Fishing</a> will give you an unforgettable day on the lake complete with classic stories and fried fish that you caught that day.   </p>
<h4>Glacier Adventure by Pursuit</h4>
<p>From May until October, climb aboard a Pursuit snowcoach as your driver takes you onto the Athabasca Glacier at the Columbia Icefield. This is one of only two triple continental divides in the world, meaning water flows to all three oceans in Canada from this icefield. <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Brewster Travel Canada" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2737" href="https://www.banffjaspercollection.com/attractions-sightseeing/columbia-icefield-glacier-adventure/" target="_blank">The Glacier Adventure</a> allows you to walk on a real glacier safely and even sip some glacier-fresh water. </p>
