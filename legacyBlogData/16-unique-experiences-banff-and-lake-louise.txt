<div><p>As a destination, Banff National Park is already positioned in a category of its own, known worldwide for its jaw-dropping vistas of alpine lakes and rugged peaks enveloped in snow and ice. But beyond these postcards views, there’s even more than meets the eye in Canada’s first national park – a range of experiences you’ll be hard-pressed to find elsewhere. </p>
<p>If you’ve got a bucket list going for Banff and Lake Louise, here are some unique experiences to add to the roster, cleverly sorted to make it easier to plan your next trip:</p>
<h4>A GLACIAL PACE</h4>
<p>1. Urban life moves at a quick clip. Slow it down with an experience like no other: walking on the ancient ice of a glacier. Take the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="80.0000" data-gtm_label="Columbia Icefield Glacier Adventure" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2359" href="https://bll.fyi/4wl6?utm_source=BLL&amp;utm_medium=PkgListings&amp;utm_campaign=BLL_Web13" target="_blank">Columbia Icefield Glacier Adventure</a> and disembark on the Athabasca Glacier where you can feel the ice beneath your feet and even sip from a glacier-fed stream. </p>
<p>2. For the super-adventurous with a climbing background, plan a guided trip onto the Wapta Icefield with <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Yamnuska Mountain Adventures" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2472" href="http://www.yamnuska.com" target="_blank">Yamnuska Mountain Adventures</a> or <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Alpine Air Adventures" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2807" href="http://alpineairadventures.com/" target="_blank">Alpine Air Adventures</a>. This massive icefield links together a variety of alpine huts and mountaineering objectives.</p>
<h4>MULTI-SPORT MECCA</h4>
<p>3. 'Mix and Match' is the name of the game here. With so many activities at your fingertips, you don’t need to choose just one for a day. Plan a morning hike and an afternoon paddle or a day of skiing followed by an evening of ice skating. <a href="/things-to-do">The options are endless!</a> </p>
<p>4. Even more brag-worthy: Get here in late spring and you can hit the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="2099.0000" data-gtm_label="SkiBig3" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2055" href="https://padlock.link/93y9" target="_blank">ski slopes</a> in the morning and then head down for rock-climbing at lower elevations in the afternoon.  </p>

<h4>ELEVATED ENTERTAINMENT</h4>
<p>5. <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Centre For Arts And Creativity" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2601" href="http://www.banffcentre.ca" target="_blank">Banff Centre</a> has an ongoing series of concerts from world-class artists. What’s even more unique, though, is the Shaw Amphitheatre, where you can take in spectacular live performances outdoors, with mountain views dominating the stage background. <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Centre For Arts And Creativity" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2601" href="https://www.banffcentre.ca/events/calendar" target="_blank">Check their calendar to see what’s coming up</a>. </p>
<p>6. Watch the mind-boggling process of ice carving at <a href="/snowdays/ice-magic">Ice Magic</a>, an annual festival that attracts the best ice carvers from around the world. The action goes down on the shores of Lake Louise – the perfect backdrop to this incredible display of artistry. </p>

<h4>GRAVITY-DEFYING EXPLOITS</h4>
<p>7. Climb a mountain without the know-how of a seasoned climber. <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="250.0000" data-gtm_label="Mt Norquay's Via Ferrata" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2609" href="https://bll.fyi/2fyw" target="_blank">Mount Norquay</a> offers <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="250.0000" data-gtm_label="Mt Norquay's Via Ferrata" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2609" href="https://bll.fyi/2fyw" target="_blank">Via Feratta</a> – guided climbing with the safety of a harness, ropes, cables and ladders – to get you up into the alpine. Choose from 2.5, 4, 5 or 6-hour routes. </p>
<p>8. One glass floor. One 918-foot drop. Head on up the Icefields Parkway to take in the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="32.0000" data-gtm_label="Glacier Skywalk" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2540" href="https://padlock.link/rlfx" target="_blank">Glacier Skywalk</a>, a one-kilometre walkway suspended over the Sunwapta Valley. </p>

<h4>FEAST YOUR EYES</h4>
<p>9. Fondue with a view? It’s as amazing as it sounds. Join <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="74.0000" data-gtm_label="Great Divide Nature Interpretation" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2302" href="https://www.greatdivide.ca/fondue/" target="_blank">Great Divide Interpretation</a> for this one-of-a-kind experience of hiking to enjoy some expansive views and a chocolate fondue.</p>
<p>10. It’s tough to find a place more beautiful than Banff National Park that is also home to an extensive list of top-notch restaurants and award-winning cuisine. Make the most of exceptional dining experiences with sky-high views at these <a href="/blog/7-most-scenic-restaurants-banff-and-lake-louise">7 Scenic Restaurants in Banff and Lake Louise</a>.</p>

<h4>THE (AUTHENTIC) WILD</h4>
<p>11. A thousand kilometres of trails wind their way through Banff National Park’s wild backcountry areas. Get off the beaten track to experience life on the trail, where you can link together backcountry campgrounds, and hike for days. Check out <a href="/blog/9-bucket-list-hikes-banff-national-park">9 Bucket List Hikes in Banff National Park</a> or <a href="/blog/backpacking-banff-fall-trip-skoki-loop">Backpacking Banff: A Fall Trip On The Skoki Loop</a> for more backcountry ideas. </p>
<p>12. Drive the Bow Valley Parkway (keep in mind <a data-gtm_category="Services" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Parks Canada" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Community &amp; Government" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2548" href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/en/pn-np/ab/banff/visit/installations-facilities/fermetures-closures" target="_blank">seasonal closures</a>) or the Lake Minnewanka Loop for a solid chance of seeing some local inhabitants, such as elk, bears and bighorn sheep. Check out <a href="/blog/wildlife-banff-national-park">Wildlife of Banff National Park</a> for more inspiration. </p>
<p>Consider joining a local company, such as <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="120.0000" data-gtm_label="Discover Banff Tours" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2380" href="https://padlock.link/ih77" target="_blank">Discover Banff Tours </a>or <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Adventures" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="1985" href="https://www.banffadventures.com" target="_blank">Banff Adventures</a>, that offers wildlife watching tours. Remember: do not stop your vehicle. Simply slow down, and always keep a good viewing distance. </p>

Get those lofty mountaineers’ views with the help of the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://bll.fyi/lhpm" target="_blank">Banff Gondola</a>, the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="32.9500" data-gtm_label="The Lake Louise Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2238" href="http://bll.fyi/pb82" target="_blank">Lake Louise Summer Gondola</a>, the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="65.0000" data-gtm_label="Mt. Norquay" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2098" href="https://bll.fyi/9jrv" target="_blank">Sightseeing Chair at Mount Norquay</a> or gondola access to <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Sunshine Sightseeing Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2830" href="https://bll.fyi/8v2k" target="_blank">Sunshine Meadows</a>. </p>

<h4>QUINTESSENTIAL MOUNTAIN LIFE</h4>
<p>15. True disconnection – from everyday life, the internet or a cell signal – has become a rare experience. Hike, ski or snowshoe into one of Banff National Park’s <a href="/blog/have-cozy-cabin-holiday-banff-national-park">backcountry lodges</a> to enjoy life as it was in a bygone era – complete with a crackling fireplace, oil lamps, reading nooks, cozy beds, gourmet cuisine and little connection to the outside world. </p>
<p>16. Get a taste of backcountry lodge life without the long trek thanks to the <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Lake Agnes Tea House" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Coffee Houses &amp; Tea Rooms" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2236" href="http://www.lakeagnesteahouse.com" target="_blank">Lake Agnes teahouse</a> at Lake Louise. A moderate hike brings you to the doorstep of one of the best traditions in the area and a lovely menu of teas, coffee, pie, and more. </p>
