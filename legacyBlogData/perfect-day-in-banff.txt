<div><p>Located in the heart of the Canadian Rockies, Banff is a picture-perfect mountain town surrounded by majestic mountain peaks, turquoise lakes, and home to some of Canada’s most iconic animals. If you only have one day to spend in this mountain paradise, here is a list of must-see activities. </p>
<h4>Soak up the serenity of the mountains</h4>
<p>Rise early and take to the trails to experience the most peaceful time of day. Follow the river path to the spectacular Bow Falls or make the short hike up Tunnel Mountain for magnificent views over the town.</p>
<p>The morning is also the best time to soar above the town on a <a href="/gondolas">scenic gondola or chairlift ride</a>. The <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://bll.fyi/lhpm" target="_blank">Banff Gondola</a> and <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="29.9500" data-gtm_label="Banff Norquay Sightseeing Chairlift" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2613" href="https://summer.banffnorquay.com/" target="_blank">Mt. Norquay Sightseeing Chairlift</a> both offer spectacular panoramic views of the town of Banff and surrounding mountains. </p>
<p>As you experience the majesty of the mountains, keep an eye out for <a href="/blog/wildlife-banff-national-park">iconic wildlife</a> such as deer and elk, or spot some of the 260 bird species found in Banff National Park.</p>

<h4>Fuel up for adventure</h4>
<p>Now that you have worked up an appetite, it’s time to indulge in some classic Canadian cuisine. If you’re looking for breakfast with a view, head to the <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Juniper Hotel Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2753" href="https://bll.fyi/kvse" target="_blank">Juniper Bistro</a> for panoramic views and a locally-inspired menu. If it's comfort food you're after, <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Coyotes Deli &amp; Grill" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2365" href="http://coyotesbanff.com" target="_blank">Coyotes Southwestern Grill</a> has you covered, and for the authentic Canadian brunch experience head to <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Maple Leaf - Grille &amp; Lounge" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2255" href="http://www.banffmapleleaf.com" target="_blank">The Maple Leaf</a> in the heart of downtown Banff. For more recommendations, check out <a href="/blog/best-brunch-banff-national-park">Best of Brunch in Banff National Park</a>.</p>

<h4>Discover your adventurous side</h4>
<p>You’re acquainted with the town and now it’s time to get your blood pumping with an afternoon adventure. If gentle exploration is your thing, try a <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Trail Riders" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2205" href="https://bll.fyi/pyft" target="_blank">horseback adventure</a> or <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Rocky Mountain Raft Tours" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2030" href="http://www.banffrafttours.com" target="_blank">scenic float tour</a>. For adrenaline junkies, choose from the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="250.0000" data-gtm_label="Mt Norquay's Via Ferrata" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2609" href="https://bll.fyi/2fyw" target="_blank">Via Ferrata</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="120.0000" data-gtm_label="Discover Banff Tours" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2380" href="https://www.banfftours.com/things-to-do/banff-whitewater-rafting/" target="_blank">whitewater rafting</a>, or <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="100.0000" data-gtm_label="Canmore Cave Tours" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2334" href="http://www.canmorecavetours.com" target="_blank">cave tours</a>.</p>

<h4>Indulge in the best of regional cuisine</h4>

If you're looking to enjoy dinner with a view then head up the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://bll.fyi/lhpm" target="_blank">Banff Gondola</a> to <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Sky Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2119" href="https://bll.fyi/w1qa" target="_blank">Sky Bistro</a> or enjoy breathtaking views over the valley at the <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Juniper Hotel Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2753" href="https://bll.fyi/kvse" target="_blank">Juniper Bistro</a>.</p>

<h4>Stargaze the night away</h4>
<p>Make the most of the dark skies over Banff National Park and gaze at thousands of stars overhead.
