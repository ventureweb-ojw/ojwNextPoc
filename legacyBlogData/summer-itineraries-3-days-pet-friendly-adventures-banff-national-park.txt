<div><p paraeid="{8a801302-8dc1-4667-9b6c-b5733ebb6e67}{178}" paraid="1">You: “There’s no such thing as a perfect vacat…” </p>
<p paraeid="{8a801302-8dc1-4667-9b6c-b5733ebb6e67}{194}" paraid="4">Banff and Lake Louise: “You can bring your pet!” </p>
<p paraeid="{8a801302-8dc1-4667-9b6c-b5733ebb6e67}{204}" paraid="6">Now that we’ve established that the perfect vacation exists (think: mountains, glaciers, and delicious food all side-by-side with your furry friend), here’s how you can make dreams come true.  </p>
<h4 paraeid="{8a801302-8dc1-4667-9b6c-b5733ebb6e67}{214}" paraid="8">Day 1: Spoil Your Dog and Cat With Room Service  </h4>
<p paraeid="{8a801302-8dc1-4667-9b6c-b5733ebb6e67}{224}" paraid="10">Who are we kidding? You’re spoiling yourself too! Now that you brought your pet, you can extend your stay instead of rushing home.  </p>
<p paraeid="{8a801302-8dc1-4667-9b6c-b5733ebb6e67}{234}" paraid="12">So pick a hotel with your favourite restaurants, spas, and views, because you need to love it just as much as them, and their only booking preference is that they share a room with you. </p>
<p paraeid="{8a801302-8dc1-4667-9b6c-b5733ebb6e67}{244}" paraid="14">Check-in, unwind and let the vacation begin. Then spend your first morning lounging together in a decadent robe atop a plush bed while eating fluffy waffles topped with fresh berries, whipped cream (for sharing) and maybe even a caesar or mimosa. </p>
<p paraeid="{8a801302-8dc1-4667-9b6c-b5733ebb6e67}{254}" paraid="16">We know you love your pet, but sometimes you can’t do every activity with them, and not all hotels allow you to leave them unattended in your room.  </p>
<p paraeid="{d60085cc-b254-43f8-b2f6-c7838da77d00}{9}" paraid="18">If you plan a meal out, a dip at the hot springs, or a horseback ride, check them into <a data-gtm_category="Services" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Veronica's Dog Grooming" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Community &amp; Government" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2451" href="https://www.762dogs.com/" target="_blank">Veronica’s Dog Grooming &amp; Day Care</a>. You can drop your dog off for the day, the night, or even just for a spa appointment of their own.  </p>

<h4 paraeid="{d60085cc-b254-43f8-b2f6-c7838da77d00}{141}" paraid="42">Day 2: Shop for Souvenirs Together </h4>
<p paraeid="{d60085cc-b254-43f8-b2f6-c7838da77d00}{147}" paraid="43">Aside from the iconic photo atop Tunnel Mountain or on the shores of Lake Louise with your pooch, your dog deserves to bring home some souvenirs from <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Doghouse" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sporting goods &amp; Apparel" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2836" href="https://www.facebook.com/banffdoghouse/" target="_blank">Banff Doghouse</a>.  </p>
<p paraeid="{d60085cc-b254-43f8-b2f6-c7838da77d00}{162}" paraid="45">Treat them to a delicious hand-made cookie to mark the occasion. Then, trim them from head to tail in swag like Banff-themed bandanas so that everyone back home knows that you didn’t leave your pet behind. </p>
<p paraeid="{d60085cc-b254-43f8-b2f6-c7838da77d00}{178}" paraid="47">If you forget any of the necessities, you can pick up food, beds, bowls, leads, and more. </p>
<p paraeid="{d60085cc-b254-43f8-b2f6-c7838da77d00}{188}" paraid="49">For dinner, head over to <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Bear Street Tavern" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2155" href="http://www.bearstreettavern.ca" target="_blank">Bear St. Tavern’s</a> patio where you can tie your dog up while you sip on a locally brewed beer and eat your weight in pizza drizzled with their signature honey and chilli oil.  </p>

<h4 paraeid="{d60085cc-b254-43f8-b2f6-c7838da77d00}{206}" paraid="53">Day 3: Show Your Furry Friend the Sights  </h4>
<p paraeid="{d60085cc-b254-43f8-b2f6-c7838da77d00}{212}" paraid="54">Many of the trails in the Rockies are pet-friendly, and you can check with <a data-gtm_category="Services" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Parks Canada" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Community &amp; Government" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2548" href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/en/voyage-travel/regles-rules/chien-dog" target="_blank">Parks Canada </a>before you head out to be sure that you’ve chosen the right one.  </p>
<p paraeid="{d60085cc-b254-43f8-b2f6-c7838da77d00}{227}" paraid="56">Some of our go-to trails include: </p>
<ul role="list">
<li aria-setsize="-1" data-aria-level="1" data-aria-posinset="1" data-font="" data-leveltext="-" data-listid="2" role="listitem">
<p paraeid="{d60085cc-b254-43f8-b2f6-c7838da77d00}{237}" paraid="58">The pedestrian bridge from downtown Banff to the Bow Falls  </p>
</li>
<li aria-setsize="-1" data-aria-level="1" data-aria-posinset="2" data-font="" data-leveltext="-" data-listid="2" role="listitem">
<p paraeid="{d60085cc-b254-43f8-b2f6-c7838da77d00}{242}" paraid="59"><a data-gtm_category="Services" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Parks Canada" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Community &amp; Government" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2548" href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/en/pn-np/ab/banff/activ/randonee-hiking/lakelouise#consolation" target="_blank">Consolation Lakes</a> in Lake Louise </p>

<li aria-setsize="-1" data-aria-level="1" data-aria-posinset="3" data-font="" data-leveltext="-" data-listid="2" role="listitem">

</li>
</ul>
<p paraeid="{d60085cc-b254-43f8-b2f6-c7838da77d00}{252}" paraid="61">You may have an adventure pup or cat on your hands but be sure to match the activity to their skill level. Like humans, hopping on a big hike on a hot summer day can be exhausting if they’ve never done it before. Bring plenty of water along for you both.  </p>
<p paraeid="{9dd4a594-a702-42b0-beb4-644a3bcf02a3}{7}" paraid="63">And, unless you’re visiting the <a data-gtm_category="Services" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Town Hall" data-gtm_no_track="Y" data-gtm_subcategory="Community &amp; Government" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2605" href="https://banff.ca/1030/Off-Leash-Dog-Park" target="_blank">off-leash park</a>, ensure they are on-leash at all times. <p paraeid="{9dd4a594-a702-42b0-beb4-644a3bcf02a3}{17}" paraid="65">Now that you’re sufficiently tuckered out, it’s time to head home—or stay another night, we wouldn’t judge you if you needed a cat nap after all that activity.  </p>
