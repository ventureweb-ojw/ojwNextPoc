<div><p dir="ltr">Just because the sun has set doesn’t mean your adventure needs to be over! Banff and Lake Louise have an endless amount of excuses to keep you up well past your bedtime. Here’s our list of 7 great after-dark activities:</p>
<h4 dir="ltr">1. Experience the nightlife </h4>
<p dir="ltr">As the day winds down, the evening scene in Banff and Lake Louise comes to life. <a href="/blog/your-guide-amazing-cocktails-banff-national-park">Grab a cocktail</a> at one of the many great lounges, sample the best of the après scene or dance the night away at one of our great nightclubs. Check out our <a href="/dining-nightlife">Dining and Nightlife</a> page for plenty of inspiration! </p>

<h4 dir="ltr">2. Lace up your skates </h4>
<p dir="ltr">Getting ready for the next Olympics? Well, you’re in luck as there are several illuminated skating rinks in Banff and Lake Louise. If you’re downtown, swing by the centrally located rink at the Banff High School (at the corner of Banff Ave and Wolf St). If you’re adventuring near the <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2474" href="http://www.fairmont.com/lake-louise/" target="_blank">Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise,</a> bring your skates to the lakeshore and have a blast skating on natural ice rinks of Lake Louise. For more inspiration, read our <a href="/blog/8toskate">8 memorable places to skate blog</a>. If you need ice skates, rentals are available from <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Snowtips-Bactrax Ski &amp; Bike Rental" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sporting goods &amp; Apparel" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2063" href="http://www.snowtips-bactrax.com" target="_blank">Snowtips-Bactrax</a> and <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Adventures" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="1985" href="http://www.banffadventures.com" target="_blank">Banff Adventures</a> in Banff and the <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2474" href="http://www.chateaumountainsports.com" target="_blank">Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise</a> and <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Wilson Mountain Sports" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sporting goods &amp; Apparel" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2467" href="https://www.wmsll.com" target="_blank">Wilson Mountain Sports</a> in Lake Louise.  </p>

<h4 dir="ltr">3. Explore a frozen canyon   </h4>
<p dir="ltr">Strap on your cleats, turn on your headlamp and watch the crystallized walls of <a href="/johnston-canyon">Johnston Canyon</a> come to life on an evening ice tour with <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="120.0000" data-gtm_label="Discover Banff Tours" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2380" href="https://www.banfftours.com/activities/johnston-canyon-evening/" target="_blank">Discover Banff Tours</a> and <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Adventures" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="1985" href="https://www.banffadventures.com/Things-To-Do/Winter-Activities/Ice-Walks/Johnston-Canyon-Evening-Icewalk" target="_blank">Banff Adventures</a>. One of the most popular summer destinations in Banff National Park, Johnston Canyon becomes transformed as surging waterfalls turn to frozen giants during the winter months. Add a cup of hot chocolate and you’re in for a night to remember!</p>

<h4 dir="ltr">4. Soak in the stars</h4>
<p dir="ltr">You’ve seen the stunning aurora photos, now it’s time to capture one for your Instagram! Astrophotography enthusiasts will appreciate the endless dark skies surrounding Banff and Lake Louise. Consider heading out to Lake Minnewanka, Bow Lake to be sure you’ve left all of the light behind! For the latest on aurora conditions, sign up for alerts with <a href="http://www.aurorawatch.ca">Aurora Watch.</a> If photography isn’t your thing, just bring some tea and watch the stars dance across the mountain skies - you won't regret it.   </p>

<h4 dir="ltr">5. Go for a moonlight snowshoe</h4>
<p dir="ltr">As night falls over the iconic Lake Louise, the only sound you’ll hear is the snow crunch under your feet. Put on some snowshoes and let a <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2474" href="http://www.fairmont.com/lake-louise/promotions/mountain-heritage-program-winter/" target="_blank">Mountain Heritage Guide</a> from the<a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2474" href="http://www.fairmont.com/lake-louise/" target="_blank"> Chateau Lake Louise</a> help you explore hidden gems under the red glow of headlamps. The evening transforms this iconic shoreline into a silent paradise. </p>

<h4 dir="ltr">6. Try night skiing at Mt Norquay  </h4>
<p dir="ltr">A favourite of many Banff locals, the illuminated slopes of <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="65.0000" data-gtm_label="Mt. Norquay" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2098" href="https://bll.fyi/9jrv" target="_blank">Mt Norquay</a> will give you a chance to ski under the stars. Hop on the Cascade chair and enjoy a few laps to finish off the week.  Skiing runs for several Fridays during the winter ski seasons - dates to be announced. Local tip: Don’t forget to head up to <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="65.0000" data-gtm_label="Mt. Norquay" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2098" href="http://winter.banffnorquay.com/your-mountain/on-hill-dining/" target="_blank">The Lone Pine</a> after your runs for a pint and a snack!</p>
<h4 dir="ltr">7. Enjoy a show at Banff Centre</h4>
<p dir="ltr">With an events calendar as tall as the mountains surrounding it, <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Centre For Arts And Creativity" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2601" href="https://www.banffcentre.ca/events" target="_blank">Banff Centre for Arts &amp; Creativity will have a show for you.</a> Enjoy the best of dance, music, theatre and more at this iconic Canadian arts institution. Before or after the show, be sure to grab a table at <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Three Ravens Restaurant &amp; Wine Bar - The Banff Centre" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2176" href="https://www.banffcentre.ca/three-ravens-restaurant-wine-bar" target="_blank">Three Ravens restaurant and bar, </a>where you can watch the moonlight wash across the valley. </p>
