<div><p>Flights are booked, but now what? We’ve got a full seven-day itinerary mapped out for you to help you plan your perfect trip to Banff National Park. All you have to do is say “yes” to a trip of a lifetime with extraordinary views, delicious meals, and adventures for every style.</p>
<h4>DAY ONE</h4>
<h5><strong>Journey Back in Time and Explore the History of Canada’s First National Park</strong></h5>
<p>Take a short walk from town to <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="3.9000" data-gtm_label="Cave &amp; Basin National Historic Site" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2496" href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/cave" target="_blank">Cave and Basin National Historic Site</a> where you can explore the interpretive exhibits, learn about the natural and cultural history of the area, and wander the boardwalks surrounding the springs.</p>
<h5><strong>View Iconic Canadian Animals in their Natural Environment</strong></h5>
<p>Once you’ve brushed up on your history and learned which animals to expect through a tour of <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="3.9000" data-gtm_label="Banff Park Museum National Historic Site of Canada" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2614" href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/en/lhn-nhs/ab/banff" target="_blank">Banff Park Museum</a>, you’re ready to search for some that are inhabiting the park today. Take a <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="120.0000" data-gtm_label="Discover Banff Tours" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2380" href="https://www.banfftours.com/things-to-do/banff-wildlife-tours/" target="_blank">wildlife tour</a> for a northern version of the safari. Winding mountainous roads may surprise you with a visit from our wild neighbours.</p>
<h5><strong>Farm-Fresh Food</strong></h5>
<p>Exploring the valleys is hungry work and you’ve yet to see all that these mountains have to offer. Hop on the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://bll.fyi/lhpm?pd00=250901543.1562630337" target="_blank">Banff Gondola</a> and dine at 7,400 ft. With sweeping views brought to you by floor-to-ceiling glass windows, you’ll be hard pressed to find somewhere more phenomenal to enjoy your dinner. <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Sky Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2119" href="https://bll.fyi/w1qa?pd00=250901543.1562630337" target="_blank">Sky Bistro’s</a> regionally sourced menu focuses on bringing local farmers to your table, serving you the best of what Alberta and B.C. have to offer.  </p>
<h5><strong>Enjoy an Evening Exploration of the Galaxies</strong></h5>
<p>Now it’s time to sit back, relax, and search the skies above for the stars. On the sightseeing deck of the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://bll.fyi/lhpm?pd00=250901543.1562630337" target="_blank">Banff Gondola</a>, you’ll feel like you’ve left this world and entered a new one of dark skies speckled with stars.</p>

<h5><strong>Watch the Sunrise from the Top of Tunnel Mountain</strong></h5>
<p>Rise and shine! Make your way to the top of <a data-gtm_category="Services" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Parks Canada" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Community &amp; Government" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2548" href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/en/pn-np/ab/banff/activ/randonee-hiking/banff" target="_blank">Tunnel Mountain</a> for sunrise. You’ll summit in approximately an hour for breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and the town of Banff below. Check out some <a href="/blog/locals-favourite-hikes-banff-national-park">Locals’ Favourite Hikes</a> for more adventure inspiration.</p>
<h5><strong>Prepare for a Day of Adventure with the Best Canadian Cuisine</strong></h5>
<p>Skipping your morning coffee to make sunrise was well worth the sacrifice, but now that you’re on your way down the mountain, your next adventure will have to wait until you’ve had your fill. Stop in for a tasty bite at <a href="https://www.banfflakelouise.com/blog/best-brunch-banff-national-park">one of these breakfast restaurants with a view</a> to refuel before the day continues.</p>
<h5><strong>Experience Ultimate Panoramic Views at Mt. Norquay</strong></h5>
<p>For another perspective of the mountains, it’s time to head up to <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="65.0000" data-gtm_label="Mt. Norquay" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2098" href="https://bll.fyi/9jrv?pd00=250901543.1562630337" target="_blank">Mt. Norquay</a>. The drive up is super scenic, so take the free shuttle and keep your eyes on the view. A quick whisk up the sightseeing chairlift and you’ve reached the sightseeing platform with views stretching in every direction. This is the perfect spot to stop for lunch with the <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Cliff House Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2612" href="https://bll.fyi/ln3y" target="_blank">Cliffhouse Bistro</a> offering a contemporary menu with a mountain casual feel. Want to get a little higher? Book on the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="250.0000" data-gtm_label="Mt Norquay's Via Ferrata" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2609" href="https://bll.fyi/2fyw?pd00=250901543.1562630337" target="_blank">Via Ferrata</a> tour to climb to the summit of Mt. Norquay and stand where few have stood before.</p>
<h5><strong>Awaken your Senses with an After-Dark Lantern Tour</strong></h5>
<p>If you missed the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="3.9000" data-gtm_label="Cave &amp; Basin National Historic Site" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2496" href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/cave" target="_blank">Cave and Basin</a> on your first day, head over for an after-dark tour lit by lanterns.</p>

<h5><strong>Take your Sightseeing Views to Greater Heights</strong></h5>
<p>We have our fair share of gondolas in Banff National Park, and there’s a good reason why: they all bring you to different and equally incredible landscapes. The <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Sunshine Sightseeing Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2830" href="https://bll.fyi/8v2k?pd00=250901543.1562630337" target="_blank">Banff Sunshine Gondola</a> gives you easy access to a remote valley with lakes, wildflowers, and kilometres of walking trails as you straddle the border between Alberta and B.C.</p>
<h5><strong>Navigate the Waterways of Vermilion Lakes</strong></h5>
<p>Those alpine lakes you saw at <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Sunshine Sightseeing Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2830" href="https://bll.fyi/8v2k?pd00=250901543.1562630337" target="_blank">Banff Sunshine</a> will have you wishing you could explore them by canoe, SUP, or kayak. Back in Banff, you can rent all three at the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="92.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Canoe Club" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2528" href="https://bll.fyi/yb11?pd00=250901543.1562630337" target="_blank">Banff Canoe Club</a> and head over to Vermilion Lakes. As you paddle along, keep a close eye out for beavers, birds, and even the odd elk along the shoreline.</p>
<h5><strong>Enjoy a Local Food Experience</strong></h5>
<p>Along your travels so far, you’ve met a local or two and they’ve likely shared their favourite parts about Banff and Lake Louise with you. One thing that’s sure to be on everyone’s list is the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Mountain Market" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Entertainment" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2850" href="https://www.facebook.com/banffmountainmarket/" target="_blank">farmer’s market</a>. On Wednesday’s throughout the summer, pop over for lunch, fresh veggies, local handmade wares, and live music.</p>
<h5><strong>Explore the Beauty of the Lake Minnewanka Loop</strong></h5>
<p>You’ve hiked past lakes, paddled through them, but you haven’t seen all that these crystal waters have to offer. A short drive, or bus ride, from the town of Banff will take you along the <a href="https://www.banfflakelouise.com/blog/explore-lake-minnewanka-loop">Lake Minnewanka Loop</a>, where you’ll see the lake itself along with Two Jack Lake.</p>

<h5><strong>Choose Your Own Adventure</strong></h5>
<p>You’ve seen the Rocky Mountains that Alberta is famous for, but you’ve also heard tales of the West and have been craving a try at the cowboy lifestyle. Saddle up and take a <a href="https://www.banfflakelouise.com/horseback-riding">horseback tour or horse-drawn wagon tour</a> and see how these mountains were historically explored.</p>
<p>Taking a trip to the past is one thing but being a part of history is another. Take a guided tour with <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="100.0000" data-gtm_label="Canmore Cave Tours" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2334" href="http://www.canmorecavetours.com/" target="_blank">Canmore Cave Tours</a> to explore a wild cave system that stretches below the Rockies. The temperature hovers around 5°C all year, so it makes for a great escape from both the heat and the cold. </p>
<p>If you’d rather feel the rushing water beneath you, a rafting tour may be better suited. From gentle floats to <a href="https://www.banfflakelouise.com/rafting">whitewater rafting</a>, you’ve got plenty of tours to choose from.</p>
<h5><strong>Take the Scenic Route on the Bow Valley Parkway</strong></h5>
<p>A morning of adventure might have you looking for a little reprieve. Take a leisurely drive down the <a href="https://www.banfflakelouise.com/blog/8-must-visit-stops-bow-valley-parkway">Bow Valley Parkway</a>. You’ll pass <a href="https://www.banfflakelouise.com/johnston-canyon">Johnston Canyon</a>, a place popular for its hiking trails, and Morant’s Curve, a place made famous by National Geographic photographer Nicholas Morant for its mountain backdrop and well-timed train passings. If you travel like a local, you’ll want to visit late in the day or early in the morning to give yourself the best chance to spot wildlife along this scenic stretch.</p>
<h5><strong>Settle in for the Night in a Cozy Cabin</strong></h5>
<p>You’ll also find exceptional cuisine along this road. <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="250.0000" data-gtm_label="Baker Creek Mountain Resorts" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="1981" href="http://www.bakercreek.com/" target="_blank">Baker Creek Mountain Resort</a> and <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Storm Mountain Lodge &amp; Cabins" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2599" href="http://www.stormmountainlodge.com/" target="_blank">Storm Mountain Lodge</a> not only offer seasonally inspired menus, but also offer a true respite: log cabins, crackling fires, and a goodnight’s sleep to the sound of the forest’s soothing calmness. For more cozy cabin inspiration, check out <a href="https://www.banfflakelouise.com/blog/have-cozy-cabin-holiday-banff-national-park">Your Guide To A Cozy Cabin Holiday</a>.</p>

<h5><strong>Discover the World-Famous Lake Louise</strong></h5>
<p>It’s turquoise blue hues that cover magazines and Instagram feeds are unparalleled in real life. Start the morning off with a quick walk along the lakeshore or opt for a longer <a href="https://www.banfflakelouise.com/blog/tea-house-hikes-banff-national-park">hike to one of the two tea houses</a> that are only accessed during the summer. </p>
<p>Local Tip:<strong> </strong>The wait time for shuttles to Lake Louise and Moraine Lake can be up to 2 hours in popular summer months. Do as the local's do and <a href="/explorethepark">take transit</a> directly from Banff to skip the queue, get dropped off right at the lakeshore, and spend more time exploring!</p>
<h5><strong>Spot Wild Grizzly Bears from Above</strong></h5>
<p>When you’re ready to give your hiking boots a break, hop aboard the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="32.9500" data-gtm_label="The Lake Louise Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2238" href="http://bll.fyi/pb82?pd00=250901543.1562630337" target="_blank">Lake Louise Gondola</a>. Keep a keen eye below, this territory is famous for wild grizzly bear sightings.</p>
<h5><strong>Savour a Must-Do Dining Experience</strong></h5>
<p>Now it’s really time to kick your boots up. Lake Louise offers spectacular dining options for every taste, from <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Deer Lodge" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2369" href="http://bll.fyi/y65l?pd00=250901543.1562630337" target="_blank">Deer Lodge</a> and <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2474" href="http://www.fairmont.com/lake-louise/" target="_blank">Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise</a>, to the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="32.9500" data-gtm_label="The Lake Louise Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2238" href="http://bll.fyi/pb82?pd00=250901543.1562630337" target="_blank">Whitehorn Bistro</a> at the top of the gondola.  </p>

<h5><strong>Get Ready for a Day of Adventure on the Stunning Icefields Parkway</strong></h5>
<p>Rated one of the best drives in the world, the <a href="https://www.banfflakelouise.com/explorethepark/sightseeing">Icefields Parkway</a> winds you through mountains and glaciers alike. Allow yourself extra time along this drive to stop into some of our more hidden gems like <a href="https://www.banfflakelouise.com/explorethepark/sightseeing">Bow Lake and Mistaya Canyon</a>. </p>
<h5><strong>Ride onto the Athabasca Glacier in an All-Terrain Ice Explorer</strong></h5>
<p>Once you’ve made it as far as the Athabasca Glacier, it will be time to pull over for lunch and a sightseeing tour of the glacier itself. Step onto the seriously cool all-terrain <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="80.0000" data-gtm_label="Columbia Icefield Glacier Adventure" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2359" href="https://bll.fyi/4wl6?utm_source=BLL&amp;utm_medium=PkgListings&amp;utm_campaign=BLL_Web13&amp;pd00=250901543.1562630337" target="_blank">Ice Explorer</a>, just make sure to bring a sweater, it can be cold standing on top of a glacier.</p>
<h5><strong>Step out on a Cliff-Edge Walkway</strong></h5>
<p>From here, your tour will take you to the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="80.0000" data-gtm_label="Columbia Icefield Glacier Adventure" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2359" href="https://bll.fyi/4wl6?utm_source=BLL&amp;utm_medium=PkgListings&amp;utm_campaign=BLL_Web13&amp;pd00=250901543.1562630337" target="_blank">Glacier Skywalk</a>. A glass-floored cliff-side bridge that suspends 918 feet over the Sunwapta Valley. Enjoy, quite literally, a 360-degree views of the valley with waterfalls, fossil, and wildlife abound.</p>

<h5>Relax and Restore your Energy</h5>
<p>Visit one of our <a href="https://www.banfflakelouise.com/spa-wellness">yoga studios</a> for a quick stretch and reset before your plane ride home. Or, stop by the <a data-gtm_category="Spa &amp; Wellness" data-gtm_event_value="7.3000" data-gtm_label="Banff Upper Hot Springs" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Day Spas" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2330" href="https://www.hotsprings.ca/banff" target="_blank">Banff Upper Hot Springs</a> to leave you completely refreshed.</p>
<h5><strong>Soak Up the Feeling of the Mountains</strong></h5>
<p>Feel cleansed with a walk along the mountain trails listening to the sounds of nature and taking a moment to fully appreciate yourself and all of your experiences. Follow the trails along the Bow River to the Bow Falls, or in the opposite direction to the Fenland Loop along the path you paddled just days before.</p>
<h5><strong>One Last Bite</strong></h5>
<p>Your final dinner before departing should wrap up everything special about your trip. Head up to the <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Three Ravens Restaurant &amp; Wine Bar - The Banff Centre" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2176" href="https://www.banffcentre.ca/three-ravens-restaurant-wine-bar" target="_blank">Three Ravens at Banff Centre</a> for a meal with a view of all of Banff. Cap your night with a walk through the Walter Phillips Gallery or see what art you can find on a stroll around the campus.</p>
