<p>Whether you’re a kid at heart or are visiting Banff and Lake Louise with kids of your own, sipping on a steaming cup of hot chocolate is a must this Christmas season! </p>
<p>And we’re not alone in the sentiment either. Stop by these cafes and restaurants to try their unique spins on the famous winter-loving drink. Make sure to share your foodie snaps with <a href="/mybanffchristmas">#MyBanffChristmas</a>.</p>
<h4>Wild Flour &amp; Little Wild </h4>
<p>If you’re strolling Banff Ave or Bear Street, there’s no excuse not to cozy up at <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Little Wild - Coffee by Wild Flour" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Coffee Houses &amp; Tea Rooms" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2740" href="http://www.littlewildcoffee.ca/" target="_blank">Little Wild</a> or <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Wild Flour Banff's Artisan Bakery Cafe" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Coffee Houses &amp; Tea Rooms" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2797" href="http://www.wildflourbakery.ca" target="_blank">Wild Flour</a> - where everything is made from scratch. Their holiday classic is Maeghan’s Cocoa Chai, made with Black tea and spices, combined with Wild Flour Bakery’s homemade chocolate ganache.</p>
<p>Don’t forget to grab a freshly baked gingerbread cookie to dunk in your creamy hot chocolate.  </p>

<h4>STOCK Food &amp; Drink at the Banf Springs</h4>
<p>Love hazelnut? Try <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="STOCK [Food &amp; Drink]" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bakery &amp; Deli" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2481" href="http://www.fairmont.com/banff-springs/dining/stock-food-drink/?genid=stock_bsh_bllt&amp;utm_source=stock&amp;utm_medium=paid_referrer&amp;utm_content=bsh&amp;utm_campaign=bllt" target="_blank">STOCK’s</a> Hazelnut Bliss Hot Chocolate: STOCK Food &amp; Drink house-made hot chocolate with Frangelico and hazelnut syrup, topped with whip cream and Ferrero Rocher crumble.</p>
<h4>Park Distillery </h4>
<p>Mix one-of-a-kind spirits with your kid at heart! <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Park Distillery Restaurant &amp; Bar" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2671" href="http://parkdistillery.com" target="_blank">Park Distillery’s</a> Back Country Warmer hot chocolate lets you choose between three of Park Distillery’s house-made spirits - chilli, vanilla or espresso vodka to add to creamy hot chocolate.</p>

<h4>The Juniper Bistro </h4>
<p><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Juniper Hotel Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2753" href="https://thejuniper.com/dining/" target="_blank">The Juniper Bistro</a> has us hooked on their extensive list of specialty hot chocolates. They have seven different drinks for you to try this year. </p>
<ul>
<li>Orange &amp; Spice: Hot chocolate with orange syrup, whip, orange zest.</li>
<li>Juniper Hot Chocolate: Chocolate sauce and frothed milk with whip and chocolate sprinkle.</li>
<li>Peppermint Hot Chocolate: Peppermint syrup with whipped cream, candy cane crumble and mint leaf.</li>
<li>Coconut Cloud: Vegan hot chocolate.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Boozy Hot Chocolates</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Snow Angel: Grand Marnier, Baileys and Vanilla Galliano with whip, toasted marshmallow and white chocolate.</li>
<li>Maple Nut: Park Maple Rye Whiskey, Frangelico and Amaretto with whip and toasted marshmallow.</li>
<li>The Winter Shaft: Vodka, Baileys, Kahlua and espresso with whip and espresso bean.</li>
</ul>

A creamy white hot chocolate infused with raspberry syrup, topped with decadent whipped cream and raspberry drizzle.</p>

<h4>Sky Bistro and Castle Mountain Coffee Co. at Banff Gondola  </h4>
<p>Baby, it’s chilly outside, and you can’t blame us for heading into the observatory at the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://bll.fyi/lhpm" target="_blank">Banff Gondola</a> for a hot beverage. Their take on the classic warm drink is a Peppermint and Lavender Hot Chocolate, or make it Optional Bailey’s add-on available at Sky Bistro.</p>
<p>Once you’ve warmed up enough, explore the observation deck for even better views!</p>

<h4>The Balkan Restaurant </h4>
<p>The perfect nightcap is <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Balkan the Greek Restaurant" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="1982" href="http://www.banffbalkan.ca" target="_blank">The Balkan’s</a> boozy Hot Chocolate Rasta: Kahlua, Malibu Coconut Rum and hot chocolate topped with hand-shaken cream. For an after-dinner drink and dessert, we recommend ordering it with a side of their famous Baklava!</p>
<h4>Bear Street Tavern</h4>
<p>Get a classic Banff pizza and your fill of warm holiday cheer with the <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Bear Street Tavern" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2155" href="http://www.bearstreettavern.ca" target="_blank">Bear Street </a>Tavern’s Spicy Bear Hot Chocolate. They mix Park Distillery Chili Vodka, Forty Creek Cream and rich hot chocolate, finished with whipped cream and cinnamon. 2 oz. Make a night of it and get one of the Tavern’s famous pizzas while you’re there.</p>

<h4>The Larkspur Lounge at the Rimrock Resort</h4>
<p>On your way to the Banff Hot Springs or after hiking down from the top of Sulphur Mountain, stop at the beautiful <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Larkspur" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2556" href="http://www.rimrockresort.com/larkspur.html" target="_blank">Larkspur Lounge</a> in the Rimrock Resort and take in the view of the bow valley with their Decadent Hot Chocolate: decadent chocolate, cream, dripping chocolate sauce, marshmallows, and chocolate fudge brownie pieces.</p>
<p>Or try their adult version with a boozy add-on in their Spiced Orange Hot Chocolate: Triple Sec, Canton Ginger Liqueur, Half and Half Cream, topped with whipped cream and candied orange flakes.</p>
