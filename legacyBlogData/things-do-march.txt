<div><p>As the days grow longer and warmer, Banff and Lake Louise bid farewell to winter and welcome a new set of adventures with the onset of spring. Whether you’re a foodie, powderhound or all-season adventurer, you'll find something to tickle your fancy at this time of year. </p>
<h4>Sweet, Sweet Spring Skiing </h4>
<p>March skiing is a local’s favourite secret. The snow gets a little softer, the skies a little more blue, and the après patio parties a lot more bumpin’! </p>
<p>For best results, we recommend the triple whammy and hitting up all three ski hills to compare the terrain and conditions. Norquay</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="105.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Sunshine Village" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2143" href="https://bll.fyi/1i95" target="_blank">Banff Sunshine</a>, and <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="100.0000" data-gtm_label="The Lake Louise Ski Resort" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2603" href="https://bll.fyi/r495" target="_blank">Lake Louise Ski Resort</a>, so you can pick your favourite first-hand. </p>

<h4>Adventure: A Year-round Activity </h4>
<p>Snow has thoroughly blanketed the mountains, but that doesn’t mean you have to stop exploring. Much less trafficked in the winter months, Banff and Lake Louise’s trails offer serene tranquility. We recommend looking at <a data-gtm_category="Services" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Parks Canada" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Community &amp; Government" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2548" href="http://www.pc.gc.ca/banff" target="_blank">Parks Canada’s</a> list of <a data-gtm_category="Services" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Parks Canada" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Community &amp; Government" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2548" href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/en/pn-np/ab/banff/activ/randonee-hiking/etat-sentiers-trail-conditions" target="_blank">recommended trails</a>, and assessing the conditions before heading out!</p>
<p>To stay above the snow, we recommend snowshoeing, cross-country skiing, or fat biking. If you want to try these adventures, check out <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Snowtips-Bactrax Ski &amp; Bike Rental" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sporting goods &amp; Apparel" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2063" href="http://www.snowtips-bactrax.com" target="_blank">Bactrax</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Adventures" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="1985" href="https://www.banffadventures.com" target="_blank">Banff Adventures</a> or <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Chateau Mountain Sports" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sporting goods &amp; Apparel" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2839" href="" target="_blank">Chateau Mountain Sports</a> in Banff, or <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Wilson Mountain Sports" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sporting goods &amp; Apparel" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2467" href="http://www.wmsll.com" target="_blank">Wilsons Mountain Sports</a> in Lake Louise for all of your rental needs.</p>
<p>On the path more travelled, we can't speak of adventure without mentioning <a href="/ice-skating">skating</a> on alpine lakes or <a href="/tubing-tobogganning">tubing</a> down some of the largest tube parks in Canada. </p>

<h4>Soothing Soaks</h4>
<p>Sometimes, our preferred way to enjoy winter is from inside the spa where you can watch as snowflakes fall. <a data-gtm_category="Spa &amp; Wellness" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Willow Stream Spa" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Day Spas" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2525" href="http://www.fairmont.com/EN_FA/Property/BSH/GuestServices/SPA/" target="_blank">The Willow Springs Spa</a> at the <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Banff Springs" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2473" href="http://www.fairmont.com/banff-springs" target="_blank">Fairmont Banff Springs</a> has indoor hot springs fit for any wellness enthusiast. Their indoor and outdoor pools allow you to experience the best of both worlds without having to surrender your robe. </p>
<p>If you prefer the snowflakes falling on your rosy cheeks, the <a data-gtm_category="Spa &amp; Wellness" data-gtm_event_value="7.3000" data-gtm_label="Banff Upper Hot Springs" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Day Spas" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2330" href="https://www.hotsprings.ca/banff" target="_blank">Banff  Upper Hot Springs</a> offers an outdoor soak. These hot springs replaced the original springs at the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="3.9000" data-gtm_label="Cave &amp; Basin National Historic Site" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2496" href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/cave" target="_blank">Cave and Basin National Historic Site</a> where you can no longer swim, but you can still visit the space, see the hot spring, and enjoy the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="3.9000" data-gtm_label="Cave &amp; Basin National Historic Site" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2496" href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/en/lhn-nhs/ab/caveandbasin/activ/calendrier-events?utm_source=TravelAlberta-ATIS&amp;utm_medium=TravelAlberta&amp;utm_campaign=ATIS+-+Alberta+Tourism+Information+Service&amp;utm_content=Event%2f12090%2fen-CA" target="_blank">Banff Winter Carnival</a>. </p>
