<div><p><em>Please note: The Banff Centre Mountain Film and Book Festival has gone digital for 2020. For more information visit <a href="/banff-mountain-film-and-book-festival">Banff Centre Mountain Film and Book Festival.</a></em></p>
<p>Outdoor enthusiasts don’t often like to be indoors, but there’s a special season in Banff where even the most outdoorsy folks head in for nine days of epic programming. Each year, the world-renowned Banff Centre Mountain Film and Book Festival gathers filmmakers, writers, photographers, adventurers, and thought-leaders, and this year’s edition (October 26 - November 3, 2019) promises to be as good as ever. </p>
<p>With Sharon Wood, Sasha DiGiulian, Andrzej Bargiel, Sarah Marquis and many more speakers taking the stage, events largely take place at <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Centre For Arts And Creativity" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2601" href="https://www.banffcentre.ca/banff-mountain-film-book-festival" target="_blank">Banff Centre</a>. However, this year’s festivities extend into the downtown and the greater Banff region through special gatherings and culinary experiences.</p>

<p>Here’s what you can look forward to:</p>
<h4>BANFF AVE. BLOCK PARTY</h4>
<p>October 25, 6-10 p.m.</p>
<p>The nine epic days kicks off with a free block party on Banff Avenue between Buffalo and Caribou Streets. Take in some live entertainment, outdoor product demos, interactive displays and more! </p>
<h4>DISCOVER DOWNTOWN EVENTS</h4>
<p>This year, the stoke spreads downtown all week long with local businesses and venues hosting their own festival events. These include: </p>
<p>So You Want to Ski in the Movies? at Helly Hansen (October 30, 5-7 p.m.)</p>
<p>The Art of the Outdoors at Smartwool Banff with artist Kate Zessel (November 2, 1:30-5 p.m.)</p>
<p>Hand and Foot Care Treatments at Rocky Mountain Soap Company (October 26, 2 p.m.)</p>
<p>Care for the Land at Fjallraven (November 2, 3-4 p.m.), a guided walk to learn about medicinal plants and wild edibles</p>
<p>Paul Preuss: Lord of the Abyss presentation by David Smart at the Whyte Museum of the Canadian Rockies (November 2, 7 p.m.)</p>
<p>Mark Synnott book signing of The Impossible Climb at The North Face Banff (November 3, 2 p.m.)</p>
<p>After-Parties include a “Locals versus Legends” Snow Show After-Party at High Rollers (October 30, 10 p.m.) and</p>
<p>Cocktails for a Cause, a winter kickoff with proceeds donated to Avalanche Canada and Protect Our Winters Canada.</p>

<h4>FRESH AIR OPPORTUNITIES </h4>
<p>The festival certainly gives you an excuse to relax in a theatre chair and take in some films, but if you’re up to some outdoor activities, the mountain environment has plenty of those. </p>
<p>On the second weekend of the festival, join Oboz and MEC for Sunrise Hikes that showcase Banff’s beauty. Hikers will also enjoy a Mountain House breakfast, Kicking Horse Coffee, and a chance for some fabulous draw prizes from participating Festival partners. Destinations: Tunnel Mountain (November 2) and Hoodoos/Bow River Trail (November 3).</p>
<p>Meet at 7 a.m. for registration and prize draw at the Kinnear Centre 2nd Floor Patio (departure at 7:30 a.m.).</p>
<p>Looking to head out on your own? <a href="/blog/10-early-season-hikes-banff-national-park">Early season hikes</a> are equally good in the late season when snow may be accumulating higher up. Find your next hike <a href="/blog/10-early-season-hikes-banff-national-park">here</a>.  </p>
<h4>FESTIVAL FOOD OFFERINGS</h4>
<p>Autumn is a great time to discover the rich culinary scene in Banff and Lake Louise, and the festival week is no exception. Enjoy Pulled Pork with Apple BBQ Sauce on a Housemade Biscuit at <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Baker Creek Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2902" href="http://www.bakercreek.com" target="_blank">Baker Creek Mountain Resort</a>, an Après-Film Charcuterie Board with Wine at <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Juniper Hotel Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2753" href="https://thejuniper.com/dining/" target="_blank">The Juniper Hotel &amp; Bistro</a>, or the Inspired by Adventure three-course set menu at <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="La Terrazza" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2152" href="http://www.banffparklodge.com/dining/la-terrazza" target="_blank">La Terrazza</a>. </p>
