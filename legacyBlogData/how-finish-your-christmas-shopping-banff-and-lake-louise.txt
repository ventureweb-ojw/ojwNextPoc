<h4>For The “Hygge” Fan On Your List…   </h4>
<p>Hygge - generally defined as “the Danish art of cozy” (New to the idea? <a href="/blog/5-ways-feel-hygge-banff-and-lake-louise">Check out our blog</a>.) - is all about fighting off the chill of winter with a good book, warm socks and a fireplace! Bookworms won’t want to miss <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Whyte Museum of the Canadian Rockies" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2459" href="http://www.whyte.org" target="_blank">The Whyte Museum of The Canadian Rockies</a> to find a book inspired by the mountains! Next, you’ll want to pick up the perfect bag of tea from<a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Tea Co." data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2399" href="http://www.banffteaco.com" target="_blank"> Banff Tea Co</a> or <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Jolene’s Tea House" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2978" href="https://natureltea.ca/" target="_blank">Jolene's Tea House</a>. Once you’ve finished that, swing by <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Rocky Mountain Flannel Company" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sporting goods &amp; Apparel" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2821" href="http://www.rockymountainflannelcompany.com" target="_blank">Rocky Mountain Flannel</a> and find your perfect “hygge” outfit! </p>
