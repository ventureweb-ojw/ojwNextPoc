<div><p paraeid="{0ce6efc0-f942-472b-b903-bc29225284ec}{179}" paraid="961218364">With snow-dusted mountains, turquoise rivers, and rich fall colours, is there a more perfect backdrop for a Pride celebration than Banff National Park in the fall? Banff's annual Pride celebrations are happening October 3 – 11, 2021, throughout the park, with venues and events of all types. Check out all the ways you can show off your Pride in Banff this year. </p>

<h4 paraeid="{0ce6efc0-f942-472b-b903-bc29225284ec}{213}" paraid="439893731">Rainbow Walk and Flag Raising </h4>
<p paraeid="{0ce6efc0-f942-472b-b903-bc29225284ec}{219}" paraid="622662849">Bring your tie-dye, rainbows, and Pride swag for a beautiful start to Banff's Pride celebrations. Gather for a rainbow walk through the Banff townsite to a flag raising at Banff Community High School. Meet at the rainbow crosswalk next to the pedestrian bridge at 11:30 am for the start of the walk. You'll walk over to the high school for the annual, official Flag Raising by the Rainbow Alliance at 11:45 am. </p>
<p paraeid="{0ce6efc0-f942-472b-b903-bc29225284ec}{251}" paraid="931592191">Date: Monday, October 4 </p>
<p paraeid="{ca78413f-d66a-45e9-87f1-79f5e73caf28}{4}" paraid="1786514972">Time: 11:00 am to 12:30 pm </p>
<p paraeid="{ca78413f-d66a-45e9-87f1-79f5e73caf28}{18}" paraid="89289260">This event is open to all ages. </p>

<h4 paraeid="{ca78413f-d66a-45e9-87f1-79f5e73caf28}{28}" paraid="47881860">Pride Kick-Off Party with Brooke Lynn Hytes </h4>
<p paraeid="{ca78413f-d66a-45e9-87f1-79f5e73caf28}{40}" paraid="846758164">Get your dancing shoes on and kick off Banff Pride Week with music and dancing at the historic <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Rundle Bar" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2480" href="http://www.fairmont.com/banff-springs/dining/rundlelounge/?genid=rundlelounge_bsh_blltdining&amp;utm_source=rundlelounge&amp;utm_medium=paid_referrer&amp;utm_content=bsh&amp;utm_campaign=blltdining" target="_blank">Rundle Bar</a> at the Fairmont Banff Springs. Canada's Drag Race host and star of season 11 of RuPaul's Drag Race, Brooke Lynn Hytes, will be your host for the evening as you kick off Banff Pride in style. The DJ will keep the dance floor full all night long, and there will be performances by Alberta Queens Gogo Fetch, Jesse Hannibal, Chelsea Horrendous and Transgender Comedian Cindy Rivers. </p>
<p paraeid="{ca78413f-d66a-45e9-87f1-79f5e73caf28}{72}" paraid="1518125910">Tickets and more information about the event can be found on the Banff Pride <a href="https://www.banffpride.ca/">website</a>.  </p>
<p paraeid="{ca78413f-d66a-45e9-87f1-79f5e73caf28}{78}" paraid="484344826">Date &amp; Time: Saturday, October 2, 9:00 pm to Sunday, October 3, 2:00 am </p>
<p paraeid="{ca78413f-d66a-45e9-87f1-79f5e73caf28}{106}" paraid="1568783326">Location: Rundle Bar (405 Spray Ave) </p>

<h4 paraeid="{ca78413f-d66a-45e9-87f1-79f5e73caf28}{130}" paraid="1874027826">Chalk-ful of Pride </h4>
<p paraeid="{ca78413f-d66a-45e9-87f1-79f5e73caf28}{136}" paraid="272261745">Show off your artistic side and decorate one of Banff's busiest sidewalks with your Pride. <h4 paraeid="{17e6e539-67a1-471b-867c-29c5aa84143e}{45}" paraid="711700340">Mountain Top Drag Show </h4>
<p paraeid="{17e6e539-67a1-471b-867c-29c5aa84143e}{51}" paraid="1284852642">With Banff below and mountain tops around you, get ready for one of the most beautiful drag shows you will ever witness. At the top of the<a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://bll.fyi/lhpm" target="_blank"> Banff Gondola</a>, watch local drag performers put on a show you're likely never to forget. On Saturday, Hire Heels YYC will have a 3-performer show featuring local performers. On Sunday, Cabaret Calgary will have a performance featuring local Indigiqueer and 2-spirit performers, games, and giveaways! Seating is first-come, first-serve, and standing room will also be available. </p>
<p paraeid="{17e6e539-67a1-471b-867c-29c5aa84143e}{117}" paraid="1019600225">Date &amp; Time: October 9 &amp; 10 6:00 to 8:30 pm </p>
