<div><p paraeid="{0f7a89a2-21ae-4f86-bdc8-d1b45d7f90bd}{172}" paraid="1091352760">Let’s set the record straight. January in Banff and Lake Louise can be frosty, but it’s also so uniquely beautiful that it merits wearing an extra few layers to experience it.  </p>
<p paraeid="{0f7a89a2-21ae-4f86-bdc8-d1b45d7f90bd}{182}" paraid="954806600">It’s the time of year when the stars shine the brightest, and there’s a good base of powder and ice for all of your favourite winter activities. </p>
<p paraeid="{0f7a89a2-21ae-4f86-bdc8-d1b45d7f90bd}{192}" paraid="1488527594">We’ll repeat that last part -- it’s one of the best months for winter activities like skiing, snowboarding, skating, cross-country skiing, and more -- and there’s no better place to do so than the Canadian Rockies! </p>
<p paraeid="{0f7a89a2-21ae-4f86-bdc8-d1b45d7f90bd}{202}" paraid="1382893801">Here’s what you can expect to see, do, and experience in Banff and Lake Louise in January.  </p>

<h4 paraeid="{0f7a89a2-21ae-4f86-bdc8-d1b45d7f90bd}{212}" paraid="858244213">The Weather  </h4>
<p paraeid="{0f7a89a2-21ae-4f86-bdc8-d1b45d7f90bd}{222}" paraid="952240504">It’s cold. But it’s also manageable.  </p>
<p paraeid="{0f7a89a2-21ae-4f86-bdc8-d1b45d7f90bd}{232}" paraid="298654697">If that seems intimidating, let us assure you that it’s well worth it. This is the time of year when the skies are mostly clear, making for great views of the stars and possibly a rare appearance from the Aurora Borealis. </p>
<p paraeid="{0f7a89a2-21ae-4f86-bdc8-d1b45d7f90bd}{248}" paraid="474736534">Banff Daytime -4°C (24.8°F)  </p>

<p paraeid="{7fa80cf2-b67a-4721-92f5-15a0f4dde7a6}{9}" paraid="1283609720">Lake Louise Daytime -6°C (21.2°F) </p>
<p paraeid="{7fa80cf2-b67a-4721-92f5-15a0f4dde7a6}{17}" paraid="1055873314">Lake Louise Overnight -20°C (-4°F)  </p>

<h4 paraeid="{7fa80cf2-b67a-4721-92f5-15a0f4dde7a6}{29}" paraid="545263735">What to Pack  </h4>
<p paraeid="{7fa80cf2-b67a-4721-92f5-15a0f4dde7a6}{39}" paraid="26705336">Layers are a traveller’s best friend. Before you head out for the day, throw on a winter coat, toque, mitts, and a scarf.  </p>
<p paraeid="{7fa80cf2-b67a-4721-92f5-15a0f4dde7a6}{49}" paraid="1266762668">Wear (and pack extra) layers like long underwear, socks, sweaters, and activewear pants if you’re heading to the ski hill. As you warm up, you may want to remove layers to avoid sweating through them, which can make you colder. A pair of waterproof pants or snow pants will go a long way in keeping you warm and dry.  </p>
<p paraeid="{7fa80cf2-b67a-4721-92f5-15a0f4dde7a6}{63}" paraid="1732940578">Wear thicker layers if you’re doing something a little more sedentary like dog sledding or tubing since you won’t have the added benefit of exercise to keep your temperature up.  </p>
<p paraeid="{7fa80cf2-b67a-4721-92f5-15a0f4dde7a6}{75}" paraid="1900211028">Waterproof and slip-resistant boots are always suggested, no matter the activity. </p>
<p paraeid="{7fa80cf2-b67a-4721-92f5-15a0f4dde7a6}{85}" paraid="1782947748">These items are available for purchase in Banff and Lake Louise if you don’t bring them from home. Check out our <a href="/shopping">local outfitters</a>.</p>

<h4 paraeid="{7fa80cf2-b67a-4721-92f5-15a0f4dde7a6}{95}" paraid="2134940521">What to See and Do </h4>
<p paraeid="{7fa80cf2-b67a-4721-92f5-15a0f4dde7a6}{105}" paraid="1984261519">We like an active holiday, but sometimes it doesn’t hurt to sit back and let someone else do the work, and by someone, we mean our furry four-legged friends. <a href="/dog-sledding">Dogsledding</a> is a spectacular way to see the mountains and try a traditional mode of transportation developed by Indigenous peoples. You’ll have time to meet and greet the dogs and learn from their mushers before giving it a try yourself.  </p>
<p paraeid="{7fa80cf2-b67a-4721-92f5-15a0f4dde7a6}{115}" paraid="90153945">By now, the lakes have frozen over and are ready for skaters. Parks Canada doesn’t monitor natural lakes for ice thickness, so we recommend safer options. You’ll find human-made rinks in <a href="/ice-skating">Banff</a> and a natural rink on <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2474" href="https://www.fairmont.com/lake-louise/activities/ice-skating/" target="_blank">Lake Louise</a>, which is checked by <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2474" href="https://www.chateau-lake-louise.com/" target="_blank">Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise</a> staff.  </p>
<p paraeid="{7fa80cf2-b67a-4721-92f5-15a0f4dde7a6}{125}" paraid="557161477">While you’re all bundled up, head over to <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="100.0000" data-gtm_label="The Lake Louise Ski Resort" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2603" href="https://www.skilouise.com/tickets-passes/winter-tickets/sunny-tube-park-tickets/" target="_blank">Lake Louise Ski Resort</a> or <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="65.0000" data-gtm_label="Mt. Norquay" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2098" href="https://banffnorquay.com/winter-2/tubing/" target="_blank">Mt. Norquay</a> to whiz down, twirl around, and race to the bottom of their tube parks.  </p>

<h4 paraeid="{7fa80cf2-b67a-4721-92f5-15a0f4dde7a6}{135}" paraid="2085981984">January Events  </h4>
<h5 paraeid="{7fa80cf2-b67a-4721-92f5-15a0f4dde7a6}{145}" paraid="269268675">SnowDays  </h5>

<p paraeid="{7fa80cf2-b67a-4721-92f5-15a0f4dde7a6}{165}" paraid="729585380">In Banff, you’ll find larger-than-life outdoor snow sculptures that take over the town. Along with <a href="/snowdays/skijoring">Skijorning</a> and the <a href="/snowdays/play-zone">SnowDays Play Zone</a>, there is plenty to keep you entertained! </p>

<h5 paraeid="{7fa80cf2-b67a-4721-92f5-15a0f4dde7a6}{179}" paraid="2088570152">Tribute Craft Spirits Festival  </h5>

<h5 paraeid="{7fa80cf2-b67a-4721-92f5-15a0f4dde7a6}{219}" paraid="629036972">Nightrise  </h5>
<p paraeid="{7fa80cf2-b67a-4721-92f5-15a0f4dde7a6}{229}" paraid="1362438402">After dark, the skies light up with more than just stars as the Banff Gondola hosts <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://www.banffjaspercollection.com/attractions/banff-gondola/events/nightrise/" target="_blank">Nightrise</a> in partnership with the Stoney Nakoda Nation. A combination of light, projections, and music cover Sulphur Mountain, telling the story of the First Peoples in this area. </p>
