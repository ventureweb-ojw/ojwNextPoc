<div><p paraeid="{54ed803c-a2b8-4574-928b-3ff10897a080}{231}" paraid="852022705">Driving into Banff, you'll be greeted by twinkling white Christmas lights adorning the trees of Banff Avenue. They offer a warm welcome and signal that the spirit of the holiday season is thriving in this mountain town. Let the lights guide your way down Banff's main street and across the historic Bow River Bridge up to the <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Banff Springs" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2473" href="http://www.fairmont.com/banff-springs" target="_blank">Fairmont Banff Springs</a>, your family's holiday home away from home for the weekend. </p>
<p paraeid="{528a10de-4381-43fd-a4f6-51a4dba32238}{4}" paraid="2124020535">Here in the Canadian Rockies, Christmas spirit meets mountain magic. It's equally enchanting for the young and the young at heart. This is a place where imagination reigns free, and through the eyes of little ones, the holiday season truly comes to life.  </p>

<h4 paraeid="{528a10de-4381-43fd-a4f6-51a4dba32238}{4}" paraid="2124020535">Day 1: Uncover the Magic of Christmas </h4>
<p paraeid="{528a10de-4381-43fd-a4f6-51a4dba32238}{50}" paraid="1592613011">Embrace the enthusiasm of a snow day by kickstarting your holiday getaway at the hotel's Winter Wonderland zone. When the instinctive draw to play in the snow is satiated, and boisterous energy has been spent, head into downtown Banff and check out the <a href="/christmas/hot-chocolate-trail">Hot Chocolate Trail</a>. You may well find yourselves sampling a different concoction each day and starting your own family Christmas tradition.  </p>

<p paraeid="{528a10de-4381-43fd-a4f6-51a4dba32238}{108}" paraid="870980486">Stop into the shops and galleries of Banff Avenue and nearby Bear Street to find mountain-inspired gifts from local artisans that each tell their own story. Christmas shopping is a joy rather than a task here, and with a visit to the must-see <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="The Spirit of Christmas" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Souvenir" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2168" href="http://www.spiritofchristmas.ca" target="_blank">Spirit of Christmas</a> store, it's a family-friendly event.  </p>
<p paraeid="{528a10de-4381-43fd-a4f6-51a4dba32238}{140}" paraid="1327188091">Before darkness falls over Banff, make your way to the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="3.9000" data-gtm_label="Cave &amp; Basin National Historic Site" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2496" href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/cave" target="_blank">Cave and Basin</a>  – the birthplace of Canada's first national park. Arrive by late afternoon to ensure there's time to visit the lantern-lit cave and hear the legends of the past. Children can run free on the snowy boardwalks, wrinkling their noses at the natural sulphur scent that rises from hot spring ponds.  </p>
<p paraeid="{528a10de-4381-43fd-a4f6-51a4dba32238}{178}" paraid="903941412">Returning to the "castle in the Rockies" that is the <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Banff Springs" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2473" href="http://www.fairmont.com/banff-springs" target="_blank">Fairmont Banff Springs</a>, roam the beautifully decorated stone halls for an impromptu game of hide and seek. Don't be surprised if the kids find their way to the North Pole – a magical place where gingerbread house decorating gives way to the shared giggles of a classic holiday movie.  </p>

<h4 paraeid="{528a10de-4381-43fd-a4f6-51a4dba32238}{178}" paraid="903941412">Day 2: Explore the Wonders of Winter </h4>
<p paraeid="{528a10de-4381-43fd-a4f6-51a4dba32238}{230}" paraid="830831232">Banff National Park is a playground for winter adventurers. Bundle up for a day of family magic in the great outdoors and head west along the picture-perfect Bow Valley Parkway to <a href="/johnston-canyon">Johnston Canyon</a>. Join a guided Icewalk with <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="120.0000" data-gtm_label="Discover Banff Tours" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2380" href="https://www.banfftours.com/things-to-do/banff-icewalks/" target="_blank">Discover Banff Tours</a> or <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="White Mountain Adventures" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2458" href="https://www.whitemountainadventures.com/johnston-canyon-icewalk" target="_blank">White Mountain Adventures</a>, designed to let your family explore a frozen canyon, unlike anything you've seen before. Even the kids are likely to fall speechless as they marvel at towering pillars of ice from frozen waterfalls.  </p>
<p paraeid="{511e0c1b-eef6-46c9-804e-2c7194d50f09}{11}" paraid="800263886">In the hamlet of Lake Louise, pick up skate rentals at <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Wilson Mountain Sports" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sporting goods &amp; Apparel" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2467" href="https://www.wmsll.com" target="_blank">Wilson Mountain Sports</a> and head to what is undeniably one of the world's most stunning natural skating arenas. The frozen surface of Lake Louise is transformed into a <a href="https://www.fairmont.com/lake-louise/activities/ice-skating/">skating rink</a> that beckons to the whole family.  </p>

<p paraeid="{511e0c1b-eef6-46c9-804e-2c7194d50f09}{45}" paraid="1523937806">Then it's back to Banff for dinner before an evening at the immersive event <a href="/christmas/christmas-spirit">In Search of Christmas Spirit</a>. Watch the kids' eyes light up as they grab a lantern and follow life-size animal puppets along the snow-lined paths of Banff's Cascade Gardens. Around each corner is a new surprise that's part of this interactive storytelling experience beneath the night sky.  </p>

<h4 paraeid="{511e0c1b-eef6-46c9-804e-2c7194d50f09}{45}" paraid="1523937806">Day 3: Snowflakes, Sleigh Rides and Santa Claus </h4>
<p paraeid="{b72f50d9-6524-48a9-9fae-2f8be8da4a70}{115}" paraid="85381732">Wake up to falling snow and delight your family with a trip to the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="65.0000" data-gtm_label="Mt. Norquay" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2098" href="https://bll.fyi/9jrv" target="_blank">Mount Norquay</a> or <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="100.0000" data-gtm_label="The Lake Louise Ski Resort" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2603" href="https://bll.fyi/r495" target="_blank">Lake Louise</a> snow <a href="/tubing-tobogganning">tube parks</a>. The slopes of these ski resorts are also home to sledding zones complete with mechanized "magic carpets" for a leisurely ride up and well-groomed tube runs down that often have adults squealing with joy as much as the kids.  </p>

<p paraeid="{b72f50d9-6524-48a9-9fae-2f8be8da4a70}{159}" paraid="1741094566">Take a well-deserved rest while enjoying a cozy sleigh ride with <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Trail Riders" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2205" href="http://www.horseback.com" target="_blank">Banff Trail Riders</a>. Kids can say hello to the friendly horses before bundling up in the sleigh to venture into the wilderness at the edge of Banff. Then it's time for a ride up the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://bll.fyi/lhpm" target="_blank">Banff Gondola</a> to take in the views from the summit of Sulphur Mountain. Your family will emerge from the Gondola cabin elated with the ride and find yourselves surrounded by Santa's elves at <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://www.banffjaspercollection.com/attractions/banff-gondola/events/" target="_blank">Mountaintop Christmas</a>. It's an event that will capture everyone's imaginations with arts and crafts, cookie decorating and a visit with Santa himself.  </p>
