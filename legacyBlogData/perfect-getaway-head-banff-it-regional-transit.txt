<div><p>It’s 2:45 p.m. on a Friday. </p>
<p>You look out your office window and there they are - the rugged front ranges of the Canadian Rockies - stretching across the horizon as far as you can see. </p>
<p>This is the perfect weekend to visit the mountains. </p>
<p>Thanks to <a href="https://www.onitregionaltransit.ca/banff-canmore-service/">On-It Regional Transit's Banff-Calgary service,</a> a trip to the mountains has never been easier. </p>
<h4>Start Your Day </h4>
<p>Take the LRT to Crowfoot Station in Calgary where you’ll be able to catch <a href="https://www.onitregionaltransit.ca/banff-canmore-service/">the On-It bus to Banff.</a> There are ten departures from Calgary and ten departures from Banff per day on Saturdays, Sundays, and long weekends - May 19 to September 3. One-way fares are $10 for ages 6+ and free for children between 2 - 5 years old. Tickets can be purchased online <a href="https://www.onitregionaltransit.ca/banff-canmore-service/">here.</a> </p>
<p>Once you’re onboard, enjoy the scenery, catch-up on your favourite podcast, or get inspiration for your trip with some <a href="/blog">stories from Banff National Park.</a>    </p>

<h4>Arrive in Banff National Park</h4>
<p>Fast-forward about two hours of a drive through scenic prairies and into the mountains, and you’re here. That was easy. </p>
<p>All On-It ticket holders receive free rides on <a href="http://roamtransit.com/">Roam Transit</a> all-day, so head downtown to start your adventure. Simply show your On-It ticket to the driver and you’re ready to roll. </p>
<p>From there you can <a href="https://www.banfflakelouise.com/explorethepark/transit-and-shuttles">take transit</a> all throughout Banff National Park, including <a data-gtm_category="Services" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Parks Canada" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Community &amp; Government" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2548" href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/en/pn-np/ab/banff/visit/parkbus/banff">Johnston Canyon,</a> <a data-gtm_category="Services" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Parks Canada" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Community &amp; Government" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2548" href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/en/pn-np/ab/banff/visit/parkbus/banff">Lake Louise,</a> <a href="http://roamtransit.com/schedules-routes/minnewankal-route-six/">Lake Minnewanka,</a> and <a href="https://www.banfflakelouise.com/explorethepark/transit-and-shuttles">more.</a> Check out <a href="/explorethepark">ExploreThePark.ca</a> for a full breakdown.  </p>
<h4>Savour A Snack</h4>
<p>A day of adventure requires fuel, but that doesn’t mean you need to settle for a Clif Bar. Banff and Lake Louise are home to an incredibly diverse local food scene. Here you’ll find hearty game meats, Alberta and BC grown produce, and meticulously crafted dishes from chefs inspired by the landscape.  </p>

<h4>Lace up for a Hike  </h4>
<p>Bring your hiking shoes and explore more than 1,600 km of maintained trails across Banff National Park. </p>
<p>Those looking to stay close to the Town of Banff will love <a data-gtm_category="Services" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Parks Canada" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Community &amp; Government" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2548" href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/en/pn-np/ab/banff/activ/randonee-hiking/banff#tunnelmtn">Tunnel Mountain</a> - a 5.2 km round-trip hike from downtown Banff, which takes around two hours and offers an excellent view of the Bow Valley.</p>
<p><strong>Some other downtown hikes include:</strong></p>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Services" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Parks Canada" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Community &amp; Government" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2548" href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/en/pn-np/ab/banff/activ/randonee-hiking/banff#sundance">Sundance Canyon Trail</a></strong> - A beautiful, paved trail that offers easy access to the Bow River and beyond. </p>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Services" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Parks Canada" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Community &amp; Government" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2548" href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/en/pn-np/ab/banff/activ/randonee-hiking/banff#fenland">The Fenlands Loop</a></strong> - Another accessible option which travels under a beautiful canopy of old-growth spruce trees. A great spot to watch for birds and other wildlife.     </p>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Services" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Parks Canada" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Community &amp; Government" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2548" href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/en/pn-np/ab/banff/activ/randonee-hiking/banff#fenland">The Hoodoo Trail</a></strong> - Walk up to the famous “Surprise Corner” (via Buffalo Street) and follow the 4.8-km trail around Tunnel Mountain. Leave yourself two to three hours to finish the trail and catch the <a href="http://roamtransit.com/schedules-routes/banff-local-route-two/">Roam Bus Route #2</a> back downtown.  </p>
<h4>Soak in some History </h4>
<p>After your hike, use your free Roam Transit transfer and <a href="http://roamtransit.com/schedules-routes/banff-local-route-one/">catch bus #1</a> up to the <a data-gtm_category="Spa &amp; Wellness" data-gtm_event_value="7.3000" data-gtm_label="Banff Upper Hot Springs" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Day Spas" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2330" href="https://www.hotsprings.ca/banff" target="_blank">Banff Upper Hot Springs</a> where you can unwind in the historic mineral pools. From downtown Banff you can also explore more of the history of Banff National Park by visiting <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Whyte Museum of the Canadian Rockies" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2459" href="http://www.whyte.org">The Whyte Museum of The Canadian Rockies,</a> <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="3.9000" data-gtm_label="Cave &amp; Basin National Historic Site" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2496" href="http://pc.gc.ca/en/lhn-nhs/ab/caveandbasin">The Cave &amp; Basin National Historic Site</a> (Catch <a href="http://roamtransit.com/schedules-routes/banff-local-route-four/">Roam Transit Bus #4</a>) or <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="3.9000" data-gtm_label="Banff Park Museum National Historic Site of Canada" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts, Culture &amp; Heritage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2614" href="http://parkscanada.gc.ca/banffparkmuseum">The Banff Park Museum. </a> </p>

<h4>Now raise a glass  </h4>
<p>If you haven’t already done so, you should probably check out some of the patios you’ve been walking past. Don’t forget - the driving is taken care of for you today.</p>
<p>Thanks in part to the crystal-clear water that flows through town, Banff is also home to an incredible cocktail scene. Check out our <a href="/blog/your-guide-amazing-cocktails-banff-national-park">Guide To Amazing Cocktails</a> for some inspiration. </p>
<p>If you’re looking for an ultra-special patio experience, don’t miss the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://bll.fyi/k1l4">Sunset Festival at The Banff Gondola.</a> Roam Transit <a href="http://roamtransit.com/schedules-routes/banff-local-route-one/">bus #1</a> will get you there in time to sample craft beer, bubbly and a variety of delicious bites. </p>
