<div><p>A long weekend is the perfect amount of time to escape the city with your family and explore Banff National Park.</p>
<p>Experience natural wonders, try new winter adventures and sample some amazing Canadian food with the ones you love. There are endless combinations of accommodations and activities, so we've outlined a few ideas to inspire you and your family.  </p>

<h4>DAY ONE </h4>
<p>Officially start your Banff National Park adventure by checking in at <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Castle Mountain Chalets" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2346" href="http://www.castlemountain.com" target="_blank">Castle Mountain Chalets</a>, where you can explore the surrounding area on snowshoes. If you don't have your own snowshoes, don't worry, you can rent them from their Activity Centre on site.</p>

<p>Log chalets with full kitchens and private balconies line the property in the shadows of the iconic Castle Mountain. Located at the junction of the <a href="/blog/8-must-visit-stops-bow-valley-parkway">Bow Valley Parkway</a> and the Trans-Canada Highway, Castle Mountain Chalets is a unique property that runs along the very railway line that founded Banff National Park and exploration in the Canadian Rockies. The property and chalets are dog-friendly and even offer furry friends a special welcome treat and clean-up bags upon arrival. Buy some of your food and beverage essentials at the Castle Mountain General Store which is licensed and located on the property.  </p>
<p><strong>Dinner at</strong> <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Lake Louise Station Restaurant" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2239" href="http://www.lakelouisestation.com/" target="_blank">The Station Restaurant</a> in Lake Louise - Enjoy local favourites and Canadian cuisine in a heritage railway station.  </p>
<p><strong>Evening at</strong> <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Castle Mountain Chalets" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2346" href="http://www.castlemountain.com" target="_blank">Castle Mountain Chalets</a> - Enjoy the outdoor fire pit as the stars illuminate the quiet winter night or cozy up to your own wood-burning fireplace in your chalet.   </p>

<h4>DAY TWO </h4>
<p>Make breakfast in the full kitchen at the Chalet, check out, and continue to Lake Louise. </p>
<h5>LAKE LOUISE ACTIVITY OPTIONS</h5>
<p><strong>Horse-drawn sleigh ride on Lake Louise</strong> - Book a <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Brewster Adventures" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2434" href="http://www.brewsteradventures.com/" target="_blank">sleigh ride</a> at the concierge desk and embark on a cozy journey cuddled in blankets as the horse-drawn sleigh glides on Lake Louise in a snow globe-like setting. </p>
<p><strong>Ice skate on Lake Louise</strong> - Rent skates from <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Wilson Mountain Sports" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sporting goods &amp; Apparel" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2467" href="https://www.wmsll.com" target="_blank">Wilson Mountain Sports</a> and travel to the Lake Louise lakeshore. Skate with the kids on what is constantly rated among the world's most beautiful outdoor skating rinks.</p>

<p><strong>Ski and/or tubing at <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="100.0000" data-gtm_label="The Lake Louise Ski Resort" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2603" href="http://bll.fyi/r495" target="_blank">Lake Louise Ski Resort</a></strong> - Lake Louise Ski Resort is Canada's third-largest ski resort and home to some of the best views of any resort in the Rockies. Beginner, moderate, and ample expert terrain with views overlooking Lake Louise make this a not-to-miss place. The resort also features a great terrain and tubing park. </p>

<h5>BANFF ACTIVITIES</h5>
<p>Head back to Banff and check-in at your hotel <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Hidden Ridge Resort" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2312" href="http://www.banffhiddenridge.com" target="_blank">Hidden Ridge Resort</a>. </p>
<p>The Hidden Ridge Resort is a pet-friendly condo-style accommodation with outdoor hot pools, a children's play area, and a sauna. Multi-level condos with games rooms make it easy for mom and dad to enjoy themselves while the kids play. Also featuring wood-burning fireplaces and complimentary <a href="https://roamtransit.com/">Roam bus</a> passes to downtown Banff, the Hidden Ridge Resort on Tunnel Mountain is an ideal location for families.  </p>
<p>Other options to consider are also <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Douglas Fir Resort and Chalets" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2383" href="http://www.douglasfir.com" target="_blank">Douglas Fir Resort and Chalets</a> and the <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="The Fox Hotel &amp; Suites" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2288" href="http://www.foxhotelandsuites.com" target="_blank">Fox Hotel and Suites</a>. Douglas Fir Resort &amp; Chalets offers multiple room categories with chalets, condos, and lodges, and to keep the kids entertained, an indoor waterpark and indoor playground. The Fox Hotel and Suites is a great option as it includes 1- and 2-bedroom suites, options with kitchenettes, complimentary continental breakfasts, and Cave &amp; Basin-themed pool facilities.</p>

<h4>DAY THREE</h4>
<p>Grab breakfast at your hotel, or head to <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Wild Flour Banff's Artisan Bakery Cafe" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Coffee Houses &amp; Tea Rooms" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2797" href="http://www.wildflourbakery.ca" target="_blank">Wild Flour Bakery</a> or <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Whitebark Cafe" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Coffee Houses &amp; Tea Rooms" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2535" href="http://www.whitebarkcafe.com/" target="_blank">Whitebark Café</a> for a bite to eat before heading off for your adventures for the day. </p>
<p>Ride the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://bll.fyi/lhpm" target="_blank">Banff Gondola</a> and have lunch at <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Sky Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2119" href="https://bll.fyi/w1qa" target="_blank">Sky Bistro</a> - The Banff Gondola is an experience not to miss for the whole family. Enjoy the 8-minute ride to the top of Sulphur Mountain where an interpretive centre with hands-on interactive displays, multi-sensory theatre, boardwalk to Samson Peak, and an outdoor observation deck await you. <a data-gtm_category="Services" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Parks Canada" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Community &amp; Government" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2548" href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/en/pn-np/ab/banff/activ/rouge-red" target="_blank">Parks Canada Red Chairs </a>are perfectly positioned in front of Mount Rundle and Cascade Mountain for an ideal family picture or selfie.  </p>
