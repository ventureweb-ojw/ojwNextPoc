<div><p>Adventures big and small in Banff National Park carry their own moments of exploration and discovery. You might expect this from the hiking trails around Banff, but the same spirit is alive and well in the heart of town. Find it on Bear Street, a favourite spot for locals and visitors alike. The studios, shops, and cafes that line the street are quintessentially Banff and should be included on every stroll through town.</p>
<p>This row of delights recently underwent a beautification and has now re-opened in a pedestrian-friendly space with your favourite classics and new options to explore!</p>
