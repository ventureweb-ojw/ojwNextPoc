<div><p>Patio season in the Canadian Rockies is a glorious time when endless views are complemented by local cuisine, Okanagan wine, Alberta craft beer, and locally inspired cocktails.</p>
<p>These Banff and Lake Louise patios are guaranteed to help you soak up summery mountain vibes and provide the perfect setting for those Instagram perfect moments.</p>
<h4>PATIOS IN BANFF</h4>
<h4>1. Waldhaus Pub, Fairmont Banff Springs</h4>
<p>German-inspired beer and cuisine meet views of the Spray River Valley at the <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Waldhaus Pub &amp; Patio" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2483" href="http://www.fairmont.com/banff-springs/dining/waldhauspub/?genid=waldhauspubandpatio_bsh_blltdining&amp;utm_source=waldhauspubandpatio&amp;utm_medium=paid_referrer&amp;utm_content=bsh&amp;utm_campaign=blltdining" target="_blank">Waldhaus Pub</a>. It's the spot to be this summer after enjoying a bike ride or walk by the Bow Falls. This pub is located just up from the falls, overlooking the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Springs Golf Course" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2475" href="http://www.fairmontgolf.com/banffsprings" target="_blank">Stanley Thompson golf course</a>. Housed in a heritage building that was once the golf course clubhouse, the Waldhaus is open for brunch on weekends and lunch and dinner seven days a week.</p>

<h4>2. El Patio</h4>
<p><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Magpie &amp; Stump and Cantina" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2252" href="http://www.magpieandstump.ca" target="_blank">El Patio</a> is one of the newest additions to Banff's patio scene. This sun-drenched rooftop taco bar in downtown Banff provides a classic Mexican experience and is the perfect place to spend sunny afternoons and warm summer nights. </p>
<p><a class="btn" data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Magpie &amp; Stump and Cantina" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2252" href="https://www.magpieandstump.ca/banff-patio-restaurant" target="_blank">View Menu</a></p>
<h4>3. Rose &amp; Crown</h4>
<p>The recently refurbished <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Rose &amp; Crown" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2038" href="http://www.roseandcrown.ca" target="_blank">Rose &amp; Crown</a> rooftop patio offers cold beer and million-dollar views of Cascade Mountain in the heart of Banff. Locals love the lounge-style seating and prime location four stories above the ground.</p>
<p><a class="btn" data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Rose &amp; Crown" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2038" href="https://roseandcrown.ca/menu/" target="_blank">View Menu</a></p>
<h4>4. Elk and Oarsman</h4>
<p>The fourth rooftop patio on our list can be found at the <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Elk &amp; Oarsman Pub &amp; Grill" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2267" href="http://www.elkandoarsman.com" target="_blank">Elk and Oarsman</a>, also on Banff Avenue. This is another local favourite and a great spot for enjoying mountain views from downtown Banff.</p>

<h4>5. Juniper Bistro</h4>
<p><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Juniper Hotel Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2753" href="https://thejuniper.com/dine/" target="_blank">The Juniper Bistro</a> is already known for their award-winning food, and in the summertime, this is complemented by an award-worthy view. With panoramic views of Mount Rundle, Sulphur Mountain, Vermilion Lakes, the Fairmont Banff Springs, and the entire town of Banff, the <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Juniper Hotel Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2753" href="https://thejuniper.com/dine/" target="_blank">Juniper Bistro</a> is peacefully nestled at the base of Mount Norquay.</p>
<p><a class="btn" data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Juniper Hotel Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2753" href="https://thejuniper.com/dine/" target="_blank">View Menu</a></p>
<h4>6. Chuck's Steakhouse</h4>
<p><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Chuck's Steakhouse Ltd." data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2750" href="http://www.chuckssteakhouse.ca/" target="_blank">Chuck's Steakhouse</a> can be found at the end of Banff Avenue, where you can head up to a rooftop patio and enjoy your locally-sourced Alberta beef with an unforgettable view. The Taste of Alberta platter is the ideal sharing option, giving you the opportunity to try a little bit of everything. </p>
<p><a class="btn" data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Chuck's Steakhouse Ltd." data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2750" href="http://www.chuckssteakhouse.ca/" target="_blank">View Menu</a></p>
<h4>7. Park Distillery</h4>
<p>Glacier to glass is what <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Park Distillery Restaurant &amp; Bar" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2671" href="http://parkdistillery.com" target="_blank">Park</a> is known for, and from their balcony, you can gaze at the mountain peaks that were formed by the glaciers themselves. Savor the Cascade Cocktail while enjoying views of its namesake above the hustle and bustle of Banff Avenue.</p>
<p><a class="btn" data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Park Distillery Restaurant &amp; Bar" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2671" href="https://www.parkdistillery.com/menu" target="_blank">View Menu</a></p>
<h4>Peak Patio</h4>
<p>As far as open-air dining and views go, this could be the ultimate location in Banff National Park. Take the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://bll.fyi/lhpm" target="_blank">gondola</a> to the summit of Sulphur Mountain and be in awe of the amazing views from the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://www.banffjaspercollection.com/attractions/banff-gondola/dining/peak-patio/" target="_blank">Peaks Patio</a>. Watch as the sun sets behind the mountains over light bites and delicious drinks with your nearest and dearest.</p>

<h4>8. Deer Lodge</h4>
<p><a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Deer Lodge" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2369" href="https://crmr.com/resorts/deer-lodge/dining/" target="_blank">Deer Lodge</a> is part of the Canadian Rocky Mountain Resorts, which source their meats from their very own game farm. Venture two minutes down the road from the Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise to this off-the-beaten-path patio in Lake Louise. Enjoy farm-raised elk and bison with views of Fairview Mountain and Victoria Glacier at <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Deer Lodge" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2369" href="https://crmr.com/resorts/deer-lodge/dining/" target="_blank">Deer Lodge</a>.</p>
<p><a class="btn" data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Deer Lodge" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2369" href="https://crmr.com/resorts/deer-lodge/dining/" target="_blank">View Menu</a></p>
<h4>9. Lakeview Lounge</h4>
<p>Arguably the best seats in the house - the <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Lakeview Lounge" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2488" href="http://www.fairmont.com/lake-louise/dining" target="_blank">Lakeview Lounge</a> at the <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2474" href="http://www.fairmont.com/lake-louise/" target="_blank">Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise</a> is where you'll find lakefront dining at this iconic Banff National Park landmark. Priority seating goes to guests of the hotel, so reserve ahead of time if you fancy a drink or meal at the Lakeview Lounge.</p>
<p><a class="btn" data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Lakeview Lounge" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2488" href="https://www.fairmont.com/lake-louise/dining/lakeviewlounge/" target="_blank">View Menu</a></p>
<h4>10. Lake Louise Station Restaurant</h4>
<p>Another off-the-beaten-path option in Lake Louise is the <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Lake Louise Station Restaurant" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2239" href="http://www.lakelouisestation.com/" target="_blank">Station Restaurant</a>. This former railway station is the oldest building in Lake Louise and has an outdoor patio on the lawn. Enjoy views of passing trains and luscious gardens in a setting that feels as if it could transport you back in time. </p>

<h4>11. Whitehorn Bistro, Lake Louise Ski Resort &amp; Summer Gondola</h4>
<p>Sky-high patio views from the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="32.9500" data-gtm_label="The Lake Louise Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2238" href="https://www.skilouise.com/activities-and-events/dining.php" target="_blank">Whitehorn Bistro</a> are made possible by a gondola or chairlift ride over the vegetation below. More days than not, <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="32.9500" data-gtm_label="The Lake Louise Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2238" href="https://www.skilouise.com/explore-summer/" target="_blank">Lake Louise Ski Resort and Summer Gondola</a> has passing wild bears grazing below.
