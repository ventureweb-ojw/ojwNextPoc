<p>Dining and sky-high views go hand-in-hand with the Canadian Rockies. Both Banff and Lake Louise boast restaurants with spectacular outlooks over lakes, mountains and valleys. The views are jaw-dropping in their own right, but when combined with a mouthwatering meal they become part of an experience you’ll never forget. In this post, we'll give you the insider tips on the best restaurants with a view in Banff National Park. </p>
<h4>1. Sky Bistro</h4>
<p><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Sky Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2119" href="https://bll.fyi/w1qa" target="_blank">Sky Bistro</a> is a unique dining experience to Banff National Park that incorporates an adventure at the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://bll.fyi/lhpm" target="_blank">Banff Gondola</a>. Starting at the base of Sulphur Mountain, you ride the gondola up to the peak where you are greeted with a multi-level interpretive experience that features a theatre, <a data-gtm_category="Services" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Parks Canada" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Community &amp; Government" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2548" href="http://www.pc.gc.ca/banff" target="_blank">Parks Canada</a> ecology exhibit, gift shops, a rooftop viewing platform, and the boardwalk experience to Sanson Peak. Spend your time exploring before having lunch or dinner at <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Sky Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2119" href="https://bll.fyi/w1qa" target="_blank">Sky Bistro</a> while overlooking the surrounding mountain peaks.</p>
<p>Local tip: go for dinner in the colder months when the sun sets early, and witness a Rocky Mountain sunset from the top of Sulphur Mountain through the floor to ceiling windows in the restaurant. </p>

<h4>2. Juniper Bistro</h4>
<p>Another one of three Banff restaurants on OpenTable Top 100 is the <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Juniper Hotel Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2753" href="https://thejuniper.com/dining/" target="_blank">Juniper Bistro</a> at the <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Juniper Hotel Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2753" href="https://thejuniper.com/dining/" target="_blank">Juniper Hotel</a>. Perched at the beginning of Mount Norquay Road and overlooking Banff town, Mount Rundle, Vermilion Lakes, and the Bow Valley, the <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Juniper Hotel Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2753" href="https://thejuniper.com/dining/" target="_blank">Juniper Bistro</a> is the perfect place to relax after a day of adventures. In winter, it offers a unique après menu to compliment a day of skiing and in summer the patio offers breathtaking views over the valley.  </p>

<h4>3. Eden at the Rimrock Hotel</h4>
<p>The last of the three Banff restaurants to be featured in OpenTable Top 100 and the only five-diamond restaurant west of Ottawa, <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Eden" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2555" href="http://www.banffeden.com" target="_blank">Eden</a> is a fine dining experience like no other. The butler-like service at <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Eden" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2555" href="http://www.banffeden.com" target="_blank">Eden</a> remarks a past-time era of elegance and class. Located on Sulphur Mountain overlooking Mount Rundle, the Fairmont Banff Springs, and the Bow Valley, <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Eden" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2555" href="http://www.banffeden.com" target="_blank">Eden</a> has a new chef that has brought a new flare from his years of international experience at top restaurants around the world. </p>

<h4>7. La Terrazza</h4>
<p>Nestled inside the <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Park Lodge" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2389" href="http://www.BanffParkLodge.com" target="_blank">Banff Park Lodge</a> in the heart of downtown Banff you will find <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="La Terrazza" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2152" href="http://www.banffparklodge.com/dining/la-terrazza" target="_blank">La Terrazza</a>. The Italian menu features an Albertan twist that is sure to leave your tastebuds wanting more. The panoramic windows and glass ceilings offer views that span from Sulphur Mountain to Mount Norquay. Enjoy a glass of fine wine and savour dinner with a view. </p>
