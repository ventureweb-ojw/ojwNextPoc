<div><p>You’ve seen the stunning waters of Banff National Park in photos, now it’s your chance to go explore them for yourself. Whether you decide to paddle a canoe, hit some rapids in a raft or watch the sunrise from your SUP, you’ll never forget your time on the water. </p>
<h4>Canoe (and Kayak)  </h4>
<p>When in Canada, it’s essential to spend some time exploring the turquoise waters of Banff National Park by canoe. Those with their own canoe will enjoy exploring a variety of lakes, including Bow Lake, Two Jack Lake and Johnson Lake. If you need to rent a boat, check out the Bow River with help from <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="92.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Canoe Club" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2528" href="https://bll.fyi/yb11" target="_blank">The Banff Canoe Club</a> or explore the iconic waters of Lake Louise with <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2474" href="http://www.fairmont.com/lake-louise/promotions/canoeing/" target="_blank">The Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise</a>. </p>

<h4>Float Raft Tour  </h4>
<p>If you’re looking for a chance to really study the Bow Valley from the river, consider joining <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Rocky Mountain Raft Tours" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2030" href="http://www.banffrafttours.com" target="_blank">Rocky Mountain Raft Tours</a>. These scenic guided floats depart from below Bow Falls, winding their way along the Bow River before ending just after the famous Hoodoos. Families and photographers will appreciate the relaxed pace of this adventure.  </p>

<h4>Whitewater Rafting   </h4>
<p>Get your adrenaline flowing with a true whitewater experience in the heart of The Canadian Rockies. Choose from either full or half-day whitewater adventures, ranging in difficulty from Class II to Class IV+ rapids. There are a variety of tour operators: <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="110.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Rafting Centre" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2392" href="http://bll.fyi/j9th" target="_blank">Chinook Rafting</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="79.0000" data-gtm_label="Canadian Rockies Rafting" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2331" href="http://www.rafting.ca" target="_blank">Canadian Rockies Rafting</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Hydra River Guides" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2211" href="http://www.raftbanff.com" target="_blank">Hydra River Guides</a> and <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Wild Water Adventures" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2463" href="http://www.wildwater.com" target="_blank">Wild Water Adventures</a>.  </p>

<h4>SUP  </h4>
<p>Stand Up Paddleboarding (SUP) is the perfect way to explore Banff and Lake Louise. Paddle out into the centre of a crystal clear lake and soak in the surrounding peaks. If you’re looking to rent a SUP, check with <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="92.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Canoe Club" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2528" href="https://bll.fyi/yb11" target="_blank">The Banff Canoe Club</a> and <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Ultimate Ski &amp; Ride - Banff Avenue" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sporting goods &amp; Apparel" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2447" href="http://www.ultimatebanff.com" target="_blank">Ultimate Ski and Ride</a> in Banff. Boards are also available for purchase at <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Atmosphere" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sporting goods &amp; Apparel" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="1974" href="http://www.atmosphere.ca" target="_blank">Atmosphere</a> in Banff.     </p>
