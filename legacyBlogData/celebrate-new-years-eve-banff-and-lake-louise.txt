<div><p>Come celebrate New Year's Eve in Banff and Lake Louise! Whether you're searching for family-friendly festivities that wrap up in time for bedtime, or a late-night celebration that takes you straight into the new year, we have options to cater to every celebration as we close out 2021 in the Canadian Rockies.</p>
<p><em>This information is subject to change. Please check with businesses directly to confirm details.</em></p>
<h4>Nighttime Light Spectacle</h4>
<p>Add a little light show magic to your New Year's traditions with <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="65.0000" data-gtm_label="Mt. Norquay" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2098" href="https://banffnorquay.com/event/torchlight-parade-2/" target="_blank">Norquay's Torchlight Parade</a>! A select number of brave Norquay instructors and staff will take on the North American run from the Big Chair with torches in hand for a light spectacle you don't want to miss!</p>
<p>The best spot to view is right from downtown Banff. So, bundle up the family, grab a <a href="/blog/follow-banffs-hot-chocolate-trail">hot chocolate</a>, and enjoy the show! </p>
