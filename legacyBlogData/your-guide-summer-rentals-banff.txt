<div><p>You’ve got your bucket list planned for everything you want to do in Banff National Park. Wondering how you’ll get the gear to make it all happen? Leave your worries (and equipment) behind and travel light. Between rental companies and gear shops, you’ll be well-equipped for any adventure in Banff and Lake Louise.</p>
<p>Here’s where to get the goods:</p>

<h5><strong>Scooters </strong></h5>
<p>Put a motor with two wheels and you’ve got another great option for sightseeing in the mountains. Conveniently located in Banff, <a data-gtm_category="Transportation" data-gtm_event_value="40.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Scooter Company Ltd." data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Rentals" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2781" href="https://banffscootercompany.com/" target="_blank">Banff Scooter Company</a> has everything you need to hit the road.</p>
<p><strong>Tip:</strong> Many companies now also rent electric bikes. Not to be confused with scooters, these are battery-powered, but also pedal-assisted. You decide how much extra oomph you want as you cycle.</p>

<h4>Other Sports</h4>
<h5><strong>Water Sports</strong></h5>
<p>Banff National Park has some of the most beautiful lakes and rivers on the planet. The best way to experience them is to get right on the water. Rent a canoe, kayak, or stand up paddleboard (SUP) from the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="92.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Canoe Club" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2528" href="https://bll.fyi/yb11" target="_blank">Banff Canoe Club</a> and <a href="/paddling">paddle</a> your way up the Bow River. Easily transportable, inflatable SUPs are available at the <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="SkiBig3 Adventure Hub" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sporting goods &amp; Apparel" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2056" href="https://skibig3adventurehub.com/summer/" target="_blank">SkiBig3 Adventure Hub</a> on Banff Avenue allowing you to venture out of town to Two Jack Lake or Johnson Lake. Head to Lake Minnewanka and hit the water on a <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Jasper Collection by Pursuit" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2737" href="https://www.banffjaspercollection.com/canadian-rockies/banff/lake-minnewanka-boat-rentals/" target="_blank">rented motorboat, canoe, or kayak</a>. Enjoy an hour or more exploring this expansive lake, and try your luck at fishing (Don't forget to check with <a data-gtm_category="Services" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Parks Canada" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Community &amp; Government" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2548" href="http://www.pc.gc.ca/banff" target="_blank">Parks Canada</a> for your fishing license). Or, bob in a canoe while you enjoy the splendour of <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2474" href="https://www.fairmont.com/lake-louise/promotions/canoeing/" target="_blank">Lake Louise</a> or <a href="/moraine-lake">Moraine Lake</a> (boat rentals are available right on these lake shores).</p>
