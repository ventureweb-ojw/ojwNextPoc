<div><p>The end of May marks a special time in Banff National Park. Winter is a memory as Mother Nature puts the final touches on another amazing summer in the Canadian Rockies. And as the days get longer, the memories get sweeter as you take in the countless ways to make the most out of your visit. </p>
<p>You won’t find a more stunning setting to celebrate everything yoga has to offer the body and the mind at this year’s <a data-gtm_category="Spa &amp; Wellness" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Yoga Festival" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Yoga &amp; Dance" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2823" href="https://bll.fyi/3d9o" target="_blank">Banff Yoga Festival.</a></p>
<h4>WHAT TO DO</h4>
<p>If you’re flexible, there’s virtually no limit to the things you can do. </p>
<p>Start with seeing the park from a different perspective with a visit to the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="49.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Gondola" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2004" href="https://bll.fyi/lhpm" target="_blank">Banff Gondola.</a> Topping off at a lofty 7,468 feet (2,281 m), the panoramic view from the peak of Sulphur Mountain can only be described as spectacular. While you’re there, take your taste buds to new heights with a visit to <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Sky Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2119" href="https://bll.fyi/w1qa?pd00=547227914.1523904033%22%20%5Ct%20%22_blank">Sky Bistro</a>  – it’s food with a view. </p>
<p>Or connect with the unique spirit of the Canadian Rockies by heading out on one of the hundreds of hikes within minutes of your front door. Whether it’s a quick hour walk or a multi-day backcountry trek, hiking is one of the best ways to truly capture the spirit of one of the world’s most beautiful places. Check out <a href="https://www.banfflakelouise.com/hiking">our hiking overview</a> for a few ideas on where you might want to start.  </p>

<h4>WHERE TO EAT</h4>
<p>Like Yoga, eating is all about balance. Healthy to hearty. Decadent to delicious. Banff and Lake Louise has something for everyone. </p>
<p>Locals love it and you will too. Organic, healthy and delicious, the <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Wild Flour Banff's Artisan Bakery Cafe" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Coffee Houses &amp; Tea Rooms" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2797" href="http://www.wildflourbakery.ca/">Wild Flour Bakery</a> offers diners plenty of gluten-free, vegan and special-diet options that won’t disappoint. If it’s vegetarian you’re after, look no further than <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Nourish" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2107" href="https://www.nourishbistro.com/" target="_blank">Nourish Bistro</a>, Banff’s out of this world vegetarian restaurant. From their mouthwatering Bear St Burger to their Socca Tacos, eating healthy never tasted so good. If you’re looking to unwind with a warm beverage, check out <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Tea Co." data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2399" href="http://www.banffteaco.com" target="_blank">The Banff Tea Co.</a> and <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="15.0000" data-gtm_label="Natur'el Tea" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2103" href="https://www.natureltea.ca/" target="_blank">Natur'el Tea.</a> </p>

<h4>WHERE TO UNWIND </h4>
<p>We don’t have to tell you the importance of wellness. (You’re here for a yoga festival after all). But as you look to spend your weekend connecting mind, body, and spirit to the majesty of the Rockies, we thought we’d take a moment to connect you to a few of our favourite places that embrace living well. </p>
<p>For centuries, people have been seeking out the healing properties of <a data-gtm_category="Spa &amp; Wellness" data-gtm_event_value="7.3000" data-gtm_label="Banff Upper Hot Springs" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Day Spas" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2330" href="https://www.hotsprings.ca/banff">Banff Upper Hot Springs</a>. Today, it’s no different. Visitors come from around the world come to bask in their warm waters and the mountains that surround them. </p>
<p>If it’s the spa experience you’re after, there’s no shortage of places to relax and reset. From <a data-gtm_category="Spa &amp; Wellness" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Willow Stream Spa" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Day Spas" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2525" href="http://www.fairmont.com/EN_FA/Property/BSH/GuestServices/SPA/%22%20%5Ct%20%22_blank">The Willow Stream Spa</a> at the historic <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Banff Springs" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2473" href="http://www.fairmont.com/banff-springs">Fairmont Banff Springs</a> to the sauna (found 14 km into the wilderness), at <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Brewster's Shadow Lake Lodge" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Backcountry Lodges" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2439" href="http://www.shadowlakelodge.com">Shadow Lake Lodge</a>, no one knows what it means to take a moment to yourself quite like Banff and Lake Louise. In fact, here are a <a href="https://www.banfflakelouise.com/blog/spas-and-hot-springs-banff-national-park">few more</a> places to slow down and reflect.</p>
<p>Finally, while you’re here, be sure to check out <a data-gtm_category="Spa &amp; Wellness" data-gtm_event_value="18.0000" data-gtm_label="Rocky Mountain Yoga" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Yoga &amp; Dance" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2034" href="https://www.banffyoga.com/">Rocky Mountain Yoga</a>. As a supporter of the <a data-gtm_category="Spa &amp; Wellness" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Yoga Festival" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Yoga &amp; Dance" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2823" href="https://bll.fyi/3d9o" target="_blank">Banff Yoga Festival,</a> they’d be to help you make the most of your time in Banff National Park.    </p>
