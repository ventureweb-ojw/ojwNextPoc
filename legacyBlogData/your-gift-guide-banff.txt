<div><p paraeid="{e880fd68-40d3-4001-9a5f-26b903f1cdfc}{236}" paraid="665208884">There is something special about Christmas in the Canadian Rockies. The majestic, snow-capped peaks, warm fires, cozy clothing, and winter adventures – Banff is the real-life version of a Christmas postcard. Which makes it the perfect place to get classic holiday gifts that will evoke visions of mountain adventures and cozy nights in snow-covered cabins under twinkling stars. </p>
<h4 paraeid="{2356e6d3-ddc2-48ef-af3e-ed3715620c81}{1}" paraid="1857340702">Gifts to Get Adventurous </h4>
<h5 paraeid="{2356e6d3-ddc2-48ef-af3e-ed3715620c81}{11}" paraid="889228156">National Parks Pass </h5>
<p paraeid="{2356e6d3-ddc2-48ef-af3e-ed3715620c81}{17}" paraid="1798207449">The one thing every mountain lover needs every year, a new National Parks Pass. This pass ensures entry into Banff National Park and supports the park and its conservation efforts. Get your(s) directly from <a href="/park-pass-purchase">Banff Lake Louise Tourism</a>. </p>
<h5 paraeid="{2356e6d3-ddc2-48ef-af3e-ed3715620c81}{35}" paraid="598710342">Outdoor Gear </h5>
<p paraeid="{2356e6d3-ddc2-48ef-af3e-ed3715620c81}{41}" paraid="763278779">Banff is a hub of outdoor adventure, making it the perfect place to find the ideal gift for the adventurer on your list. Big or small, you can get something at any price point and for any adventurer. This time of year, winter gear is always loved and appreciated, and there are a few pieces particularly popular across Banff. </p>

<h5 paraeid="{2356e6d3-ddc2-48ef-af3e-ed3715620c81}{131}" paraid="350620282">Snowshoes </h5>
<p paraeid="{2356e6d3-ddc2-48ef-af3e-ed3715620c81}{137}" paraid="1868270337">Is your adventurer a bit slower pace and spends hours walking? Then snowshoes from locally-owned <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Monod Sports Ltd" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sporting goods &amp; Apparel" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2080" href="https://bll.fyi/b59h" target="_blank">Monod Sports</a> or <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Atmosphere" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sporting goods &amp; Apparel" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="1974" href="https://www.atmosphere.ca" target="_blank">Atmosphere</a> makes for a perfect gift. Learn more about <a href="/snowshoeing">snowshoeing</a> and what <a href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/en/pn-np/ab/banff/activ/randonee-hiking/etat-sentiers-trail-conditions">trails</a> we have in Banff. </p>
<h5 paraeid="{2356e6d3-ddc2-48ef-af3e-ed3715620c81}{167}" paraid="1723192557">Winter Traction </h5>
<p paraeid="{2356e6d3-ddc2-48ef-af3e-ed3715620c81}{173}" paraid="1984090147">Winter means snow and ice, and sometimes getting out on those trails requires just a bit more grip than boots provide. This is where winter traction devices come in! These little grips slip onto the bottom of your boot and help you stick to the snow and ice, allowing you to go and safely experience things like walking in an icy <a href="/johnston-canyon">Johnston Canyon</a>. You can find traction devices across the park, including the <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="SkiBig3 Adventure Hub" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sporting goods &amp; Apparel" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2056" href="https://bll.fyi/fgq3" target="_blank">SkiBig3 Adventure Hub</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Chateau Mountain Sports" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sporting goods &amp; Apparel" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2839" href="https://chateaumountainsports.com/" target="_blank">Chateau Mountain Sports</a> at the Fairmont Banff Springs, and <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Wilson Mountain Sports" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sporting goods &amp; Apparel" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2467" href="https://www.wmsll.com" target="_blank">Wilson Mountain Sport​s</a> in Lake Louise. </p>
<h5 paraeid="{2356e6d3-ddc2-48ef-af3e-ed3715620c81}{213}" paraid="2064680592">Western Wear </h5>
<p paraeid="{2356e6d3-ddc2-48ef-af3e-ed3715620c81}{219}" paraid="409525975">Banff is in Alberta, the centre of western and ranch culture in Canada. And the ranching spirit is alive and well in Banff, with plenty of <a href="/horseback-riding">horseback riding</a> and <a href="/sleigh-rides">sleigh rides</a>. And you can gift part of that rebellious ranching spirit this Christmas season. Visit the <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Trail Riders" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2205" href="http://www.horseback.com" target="_blank">Banff Trail Riders</a> store on Banff Ave for all types of western clothing or give a horseback riding adventure in Banff. You could also gift a day on horseback in the Lake Louise area from <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Brewster Adventures" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2434" href="http://www.brewsteradventures.com" target="_blank">Brewster Adventures</a> or <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Timberline Tours Ltd." data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2179" href="http://www.timberlinetours.ca" target="_blank">Timberline Tours</a>. Learn more about Banff's western roots and how you can explore the mountains by horseback. </p>
<h4 paraeid="{0242b217-2ce7-41fd-92e5-53f43e5e74cb}{10}" paraid="1415757986">Gifts to Get Cozy </h4>
<p paraeid="{0242b217-2ce7-41fd-92e5-53f43e5e74cb}{20}" paraid="1379452009">One of the advantages of the freezing weather is the opportunity to curl up in cozy clothing and blankets. There's something special about putting on that puffy coat or slipping into that down blanket and watching the snowfall with a warm drink in your hands. Banff has plenty of classic options for getting that perfect cozy Christmas present. Sometimes, the end of a big day needs something cozy. </p>

<h5 paraeid="{0242b217-2ce7-41fd-92e5-53f43e5e74cb}{156}" paraid="1639839166">Blankets </h5>
<p paraeid="{0242b217-2ce7-41fd-92e5-53f43e5e74cb}{162}" paraid="646517301">A good blanket is hard to beat. It's versatile and welcoming and feels like a big warm hug after a long day playing in the mountains. And you can get a few classics from stores in Banff, but one stands out as particularly Canadian. The <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Hudson's Bay Company" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sporting goods &amp; Apparel" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2210" href="https://www.thebay.com" target="_blank">Hudson's Bay Company</a> blanket is a truly Canadian item with its distinctive red, green, yellow, and white pattern from a company with a long history in the Banff area. </p>

<h5 lang="EN-US" paraeid="{9951af99-70b7-4e00-9912-66622bdb4645}{21}" paraid="1501956845" xml:lang="EN-US">Stones &amp; Jewelry </h5>
<p lang="EN-US" paraeid="{9951af99-70b7-4e00-9912-66622bdb4645}{77}" paraid="127713460" xml:lang="EN-US">Beautiful earrings, stunning bracelets, majestic mountain-shaped necklaces, all types of jewelry can be found along Banff's quaint streets. A local favourite is ammolite – a gemstone found right here in Southern Alberta. You can pick up the stone itself, or find it embedded in jewelry at various stores in Banff, including <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Rocks &amp; Gems Canada" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Jewelry &amp; Accessories" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2027" href="http://www.rocksandgemscanada.com" target="_blank">Rocks and Gems</a>, Ammolite in the Rockies, and Stone's Jewelry.  </p>

Try wandering through <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Rock Paper Silver" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Jewelry &amp; Accessories" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2026" href="http://www.rocksandgemscanada.com/" target="_blank">Rock, Paper, Silver</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Jewels &amp; Gems" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Jewelry &amp; Accessories" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2584" href="" target="_blank">Banff Jewels</a>, MJ Western Stones, <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Pandora Jewelry Banff" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Jewelry &amp; Accessories" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2914" href="https://www.pandorabanff.com/" target="_blank">Pandora</a>, or <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Castle Jewels" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Jewelry &amp; Accessories" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2345" href="http://www.fairmont.com/banff-springs/activities-services/destination-guide/shopping/#1" target="_blank">Castle Jewels</a>.  </p>
<h4 paraeid="{0242b217-2ce7-41fd-92e5-53f43e5e74cb}{206}" paraid="2065102216">Gifts to Fill the Soul </h4>
<p paraeid="{0242b217-2ce7-41fd-92e5-53f43e5e74cb}{218}" paraid="2075301053">Giving a holiday gift can be a beautiful exchange. The opportunity for you to share with someone how much they mean to you and fill their day with warmth and joy. And the gift you give can help nourish that person's inner well-being with options like chocolates, warm drinks, adult beverages, art, and the opportunity to relax and unwind. </p>

<p paraeid="{0242b217-2ce7-41fd-92e5-53f43e5e74cb}{252}" paraid="1317474988"><strong>Chocolate </strong></p>
<p paraeid="{64e28c45-d4a2-4621-a81f-5f35586ce2f0}{3}" paraid="402279476">Banff is home to some of the world's best chocolate and candy, made right in the town. Get your hands on local favourites, bear claws and chocolate Santa while getting your insta-worthy photo. Head along Banff Ave and visit Banff's <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Mountain Chocolates" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2086" href="https://mountain-chocolates.business.site/" target="_blank">Mountain Chocolates</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Rocky Mountain Chocolate Factory" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2956" href="https://rockychoc.com/" target="_blank">Rocky Mountain Chocolate Factory</a>, or <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="The Fudgery" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2163" href="" target="_blank">The Fudgery</a> (where you can watch them make fudge right in front of you!) </p>
<p paraeid="{64e28c45-d4a2-4621-a81f-5f35586ce2f0}{57}" paraid="1566331415"><strong>Warm Beverages </strong></p>
<p paraeid="{64e28c45-d4a2-4621-a81f-5f35586ce2f0}{65}" paraid="1613960695">Do you have someone in your life who loves starting their day with a piping cup of coffee or winding down with a warm mug of tea? Then we have two fantastic options for you to get the perfect gift. <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Roasting Company" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2502" href="http://www.banffroastingcompany.com" target="_blank">Banff Roasting Company</a> roasts premium quality coffee that is organic, Fair Trade, and rainforest responsible. Pick it up straight from the source or at several stores in town. </p>
<p paraeid="{64e28c45-d4a2-4621-a81f-5f35586ce2f0}{99}" paraid="2036647824">And, if you're looking for the perfect cup of tea to complement your gift-giving, <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Tea Co." data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2399" href="http://www.banffteaco.com" target="_blank">Banff Tea Company</a> is who you will want to see. Independently owned and operated, they are a Banff staple and feature exclusive teas from around the world. </p>
<p paraeid="{64e28c45-d4a2-4621-a81f-5f35586ce2f0}{125}" paraid="564844811">While you're thinking about hot drinks, why not try out Banff's <a href="/christmas/hot-chocolate-trail">hot chocolate trail</a> while shopping in town? </p>

<p paraeid="{64e28c45-d4a2-4621-a81f-5f35586ce2f0}{139}" paraid="180572606"><strong>Adult Beverages</strong> </p>
<p paraeid="{64e28c45-d4a2-4621-a81f-5f35586ce2f0}{145}" paraid="491378286">For those looking to get something spirited, Banff has a unique opportunity as we are the home to Canada's only distillery in a national park - <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Park Distillery Restaurant &amp; Bar" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2671" href="http://parkdistillery.com" target="_blank">Park Distillery</a>. They make award-winning handcrafted, small-batch spirits right on Banff Ave. If spirits aren't the right gift, a local beer from <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Avenue Brew Pub" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="1989" href="http://www.banffavebrewingco.ca" target="_blank">Banff Ave. Brewing Co</a> just may be. These locally-made beers are a favourite of Banff's residents and are sure to please anyone with a taste for hops. Make sure to try them before you decide which one to gift at their Banff Ave. restaurant.  </p>
<h5 paraeid="{64e28c45-d4a2-4621-a81f-5f35586ce2f0}{227}" paraid="711792397">Artwork </h5>
<p paraeid="{64e28c45-d4a2-4621-a81f-5f35586ce2f0}{235}" paraid="201605788">Banff is home to several world-famous artists and galleries that celebrate the majesty of this place. You can find paintings, drawings, sculptures, and photography, all handcrafted by local artisans. You can find a mix of everything from painting to stickers to housewares, many of which are made by local artisans at the <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Sideshow Gallery and Pop Up Shop" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts &amp; Galleries" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2925" href="http://www.sideshowgallery.ca" target="_blank">Sideshow Gallery, Shop &amp; Darkroom</a>. If you've got an animal lover on your gift-giving list, you'll want to check out the <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="All In The Wild Gallery" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts &amp; Galleries" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2617" href="http://www.bantlephoto.com" target="_blank">All in the Wild</a> gallery for stunning animal prints from around Banff National Park. If you're going to see one of Banff's most famous sites and gift some artwork, check out the <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="300.0000" data-gtm_label="The Quest Gallery" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Jewelry &amp; Accessories" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2492" href="http://www.thequestgallery.com" target="_blank">Quest Gallery</a> and the <a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Mountain Galleries at Banff Springs" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Arts &amp; Galleries" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2552" href="http://www.mountaingalleries.com" target="_blank">Mountain Galleries</a> at Banff's castle, the <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Banff Springs" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2473" href="http://www.fairmont.com/banff-springs" target="_blank">Banff Springs Hotel</a>. </p>
<p paraeid="{dd163c3b-21a9-4c35-906c-27a2063421f5}{54}" paraid="1112993286">If you're looking for more artisan goods, check out the brand-new <a href="/christmas/bear-street-holiday-market">Bear Street Holiday Market</a>. </p>
