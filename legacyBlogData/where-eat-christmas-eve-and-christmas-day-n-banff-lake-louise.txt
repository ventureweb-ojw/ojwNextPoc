<div><p><em>Things look a little different in Banff and Lake Louise this Christmas. Many adjustments have been made due to COVID-19 to keep people safe. Please refer to our <a href="/travel-information">Travel Information</a> page for more information.</em></p>
<hr/>
<p>Waking in a quiet little mountain town as the snow gently drifts its way to the ground on Christmas Day is like stepping into a Hallmark movie. There's no more Canadian way to spend the season than a magical holiday getaway, especially when it's complete with sleigh rides, ice skating on natural lakes, and last-minute shopping for handcrafted gifts.</p>
<p>But, no matter the friends and family you're surrounded by, Christmas isn't truly festive without a shared feast. We've got you covered for a belt-loosening meal - without the cleanup!  </p>
<h4> Traditional Christmas Feasts</h4>
<p>Ready to celebrate the holidays with a quintessential Canadian Rockies Christmas celebration? There are plenty of locations ready to serve up traditional offerings with a few local twists.</p>
<p>Located in the heart of downtown Banff, <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Maple Leaf Steak &amp; Seafood" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2255" href="https://www.banffcollective.com/maple-leaf-holidays-2021" target="_blank">The Maple Leaf</a> is open from 5:00 pm on December 25th, and you can find the regular a la carte menu as well as a special turkey dinner. Enjoy this Canadian-inspired experience featuring Alberta beef, game, fresh fish, and seafood. </p>

<h4> Distinctive Dishes</h4>
<p>If you're not craving the usual turkey dinner with all the trimmings, you will still be spoiled for choice when dining on Christmas Day. Create new holiday traditions surrounded by loved ones at the following locations.</p>
<p><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Park Distillery Restaurant &amp; Bar" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2671" href="http://parkdistillery.com" target="_blank">Park Distillery</a> specializes in holiday spirit. Visit from 12:00 pm - 10:00 pm for an all-day dinner menu featuring campfire-inspired cooking and spirits distilled in-house.</p>
