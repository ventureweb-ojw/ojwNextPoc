<div><p>It's time to start planning your essential summer mountain adventures! So lace up your boots, pack your bag and check out these summer adventures in Banff and Lake Louise. </p>
<h4>1. Saddle up for a horseback tour  </h4>
<p>Travel back in time and soak in the panoramic views of Banff and Lake Louise from the saddle of a horse. Several outfitters offer guided <a href="/horseback-riding">horseback tours</a> ranging from day trips to multi-day adventures. Watching the sunset from the door of your canvas tent after a day on the trail will make you feel like some of the intrepid early explorers of the area. Check out <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Trail Riders" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2205" href="https://bll.fyi/pyft" target="_blank">Banff Trail Riders</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Timberline Tours Ltd." data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2179" href="http://www.timberlinetours.ca" target="_blank">Timberline Tours Ltd.</a> and <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="150.0000" data-gtm_label="Brewster Mountain Pack Trains" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2637" href="http://www.brewsteradventures.com" target="_blank">Brewster Mountain Pack Trains</a>.  </p>

<h4>2. Join a guided hike</h4>
<p>If you want the full Banff National Park experience, consider hiring a guide for your next mountain adventure. Not only will they deliver you to the doorstep of breathtaking views, they will also be your encyclopedia of natural history. Have a question about that mountain? They’ll set you straight. Curious about the type of bird soaring overhead? No problem! There are several organizations that offer guiding services ranging from simple day trips to custom mountain adventures. Check out: <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="99.0000" data-gtm_label="Great Divide Nature Interpretation" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2302" href="http://www.greatdivide.ca" target="_blank">Great Divide Nature Interpretation</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Canadian Rockies Alpine Hiking" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2508" href="http://www.canadianrockiesalpine.com" target="_blank">Canadian Rockies Alpine Hiking</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="120.0000" data-gtm_label="Discover Banff Tours" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2380" href="https://padlock.link/ih77" target="_blank">Discover Banff Tours</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="White Mountain Adventures" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2458" href="http://www.whitemountainadventures.com" target="_blank">White Mountain Adventures</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Yamnuska Mountain Adventures" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2472" href="http://www.yamnuska.com" target="_blank">Yamnuska Mountain Adventures</a> and <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2474" href="http://www.fairmont.com/lake-louise/promotions/mountain-heritage-program-summer/" target="_blank">Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise</a>.</p>

<h4>3. Paddle on an alpine lake</h4>
<p>From the cover of National Geographic to countless postcards around the world, paddling on a turquoise mountain lake is truly a bucket list experience. <h4>4. Scale a Via Ferrata  </h4>
<p>Looking to scale a mountain but don’t know where to start? Why not try your hand at <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="250.0000" data-gtm_label="Mt Norquay's Via Ferrata" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2609" href="https://bll.fyi/2fyw" target="_blank">Via Ferrata</a>  - a guided experience on steel bridges and rungs up sheer rock faces. In the town of Banff you can <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="250.0000" data-gtm_label="Mt Norquay's Via Ferrata" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2609" href="https://bll.fyi/2fyw" target="_blank">explore several routes at Mt Norquay</a>. Those looking to extend the adventure can hop on a helicopter and fly up to the CMH Bobbie Burns Hut where you’ll explore the thrilling <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="CMH - Summer Adventures" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2547" href="https://www.canadianmountainholidays.com/summer/ferrata-glacier/" target="_blank">Mt Nimbus course</a>.  </p>

<h4>5. Buckle up for rafting</h4>
<p>One of the finest ways to explore the Canadian Rockies is from the seat of a river raft. For gentle river floats check out <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Rocky Mountain Raft Tours" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Sightseeing &amp; Tours" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2030" href="http://www.banffrafttours.com" target="_blank">Rocky Mountain Raft Tours</a>, for whitewater rafting on the Kicking Horse River take a tour with <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="110.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Rafting Centre" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2392" href="https://bll.fyi/j9th" target="_blank">Chinook Rafting</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Hydra River Guides" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2211" href="https://www.raftbanff.com" target="_blank">Hydra River Guides</a>, <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Wild Water Adventures" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2463" href="http://www.wildwater.com" target="_blank">Wild Water Adventures</a>, or explore the Kananaskis River with <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="79.0000" data-gtm_label="Canadian Rockies Rafting" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2331" href="http://www.rafting.ca" target="_blank">Canadian Rockies Rafting</a> or <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="110.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Rafting Centre" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2392" href="https://bll.fyi/j9th" target="_blank">Chinook Rafting</a>. </p>

<h4>6. Camp in the backcountry   </h4>
<p>Take your camping game to the next level with a trip into the backcountry. Banff National Park has extensive network of backcountry campsites to explore and ambitious backpackers will love linking together the many trails into a proper adventure. Prime backpacking season stretches from May to October, although trails at a higher elevation may remain snowbound until June. Visit the <a data-gtm_category="Services" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Parks Canada" data-gtm_subcategory="Community &amp; Government" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2548" href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/en/pn-np/ab/banff/activ/randonee-backpacking" target="_blank">backpacking page</a> at <a data-gtm_category="Services" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Parks Canada" data-gtm_subcategory="Community &amp; Government" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2548" href="http://www.pc.gc.ca/banff" target="_blank">Parks Canada</a> for all of your information and trip planning information. Campsites book up quickly, so be sure to book early or look for dates mid-week. Don't forget that you'll need a Parks Pass - find more information at by reading our <a href="/guide-parks-canada-pass">Guide to the Parks Canada Pass</a>.</p>
