<div><p>Pair iconic sights with indulgent sips as you explore all that Banff and Lake Louise have to offer. No matter where your adventures take you in the park - you are sure to find a cozy drink and sweet treats to enhance your day of exploring.</p>
<h4>Lattes in Lake Louise</h4>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Trailhead Cafe" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2193" href="https://www.facebook.com/lakelouiseAB" target="_blank">Trailhead Café</a></strong>: Enjoy a classic drip coffee or a range of specialty coffees, including the Maple Canadiano: A shot of espresso, hot water, pure maple syrup, and white chocolate.</p>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Laggan's Mountain Bakery and Deli" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bakery &amp; Deli" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2235" href="https://www.facebook.com/lllaggans/" target="_blank">Laggan’s Mountain Bakery and Deli</a></strong>: Pair your coffee with an assortment of comfort food, including an array of pastries. We highly recommend the sticky buns!</p>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="The Guide’s Pantry" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bakery &amp; Deli" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2494" href="https://www.fairmont.com/lake-louise/dining/guides-pantry/" target="_blank">The Guide’s Pantry</a></strong> (Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise): Venture into the newest addition to the Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise before your next adventure. Indulge in a premium assortment of freshly brewed specialty coffees and signature tea selections.</p>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Lake Agnes Tea House" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Coffee Houses &amp; Tea Rooms" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2236" href="http://www.lakeagnesteahouse.com" target="_blank">Lake Agnes Tea House</a></strong>: We think you’ll agree that coffee brewed in the mountains is best enjoyed on the mountains themselves! Enjoy a forested 3.5 km hike, with an elevation gain of 400m, to enjoy a sweet reward next to stunning Lake Agnes. Open from June 4 to Canadian Thanksgiving and closed in the winter.</p>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Plain of Six Glaciers Tea House" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Coffee Houses &amp; Tea Rooms" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2132" href="http://www.p6teahouse.com" target="_blank">Plain of Six Glaciers Tea House</a></strong>: Pass through glacial valleys and meadows populated with wildflowers as you hike approximately 5.6 km with an elevation gain of 360m. Settle into the historic tea house for a warm drink and a slice of the locals’ favourite chocolate cake. Open early June to late September and closing over the winter.</p>

<h4>Bear Street Treats</h4>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff National Perk CoffeeHouse" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Coffee Houses &amp; Tea Rooms" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2322" href="https://www.facebook.com/banffnationalperk" target="_blank">Banff National Perk</a></strong>: A local’s favourite since 1988, Banff National Perk Coffeehouse offers coffees, baked items, breakfast sandwiches, and more. You can also find boxed lunches for hiking and sightseeing trips.</p>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="JK Bakery &amp; Cafe" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bakery &amp; Deli" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2523" href="http://www.jkbakery.ca" target="_blank">JK Bakery Cafe</a></strong>: Stop by this staple of Bear Street for coffee from a local roaster, Mountain Blends. You can also find loose leaf tea bought locally from Banff Tea Co. and Canmore Tea Co.</p>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Wild Flour Banff's Artisan Bakery Cafe" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Coffee Houses &amp; Tea Rooms" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2797" href="http://www.wildflourbakery.ca" target="_blank">Wild Flour Bakery</a></strong>: Wild Flour Bakery has got your morning brewed and baked. Enjoy an array of delicious whole and healthy foods complemented with a fresh cup of coffee.</p>

<h4>Brews on Banff Avenue</h4>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="130.0000" data-gtm_label="Canalta Lodge" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2068" href="http://canaltalodge.com/" target="_blank">Happy Camper Café</a> </strong>(Canalta Lodge): Coffee and sweet drinks pair perfectly with the s'mores available at the outdoor fire pits. You may also enjoy a warm drink after a daring dip into the Canalta Hotel's cold plunge pool!</p>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Whitebark Cafe" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Coffee Houses &amp; Tea Rooms" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2535" href="http://www.whitebarkcafe.com/" target="_blank">Whitebark Café</a></strong> (Banff Aspen Lodge): Both a local’s favourite spot and voted “Best of the Bow: Coffee,” this well-kept secret features delicious coffee and tea sourced from our local Banff Tea Company.</p>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="10.0000" data-gtm_label="Good Earth Coffee House Banff" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Coffee Houses &amp; Tea Rooms" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2796" href="http://goodearthcoffeehouse.com/" target="_blank">Good Earth Coffeehouse</a></strong>: For a sweet change of pace, try any of their fun fall offerings ranging from a Pumpkin Spice Frappe to a Honey Espresso Chai with Steamed Oat Milk.</p>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Evelyn's Coffee Bar" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Coffee Houses &amp; Tea Rooms" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2276" href="http://www.evelynscoffeebar.com/" target="_blank">Evelyn’s Coffee Bar</a></strong>: Located in the heart of Banff Avenue, Evelyn’s offers up a selection of premium coffees and a mixture of mochas, mochaccinos, cappuccinos, and hot chocolates. This is the perfect location to grab a hot drink as you sip and shop along Banff Ave.</p>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="15.0000" data-gtm_label="The Uprising Craft Bake Shop" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bakery &amp; Deli" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2968" href="http://www.theuprisingbakery.com" target="_blank">Uprising Bake Shop + Espresso Bar</a></strong>: As you drop in for a warm drink, you’re sure to be tempted by freshly-baked loaves, baguettes, pastries, and other sweet specialties. Fresh coffee and fresh baking are the perfect combination for your day spent exploring Banff.</p>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Peak Cafe" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bakery &amp; Deli" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2687" href="https://peakcafebanff.com/" target="_blank">Peak Café</a></strong>: Stop into this bustling location on Banff Avenue for a selection of tea, coffee, bubble tea, fruit iced tea, and more. You'll be spoiled for choice at this convenient location.</p>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Little Wild - Coffee by Wild Flour" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Coffee Houses &amp; Tea Rooms" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2740" href="http://www.wildflourbakery.ca/" target="_blank">Little Wild</a></strong>: Little Wild adds the brew to mountain adventures and everyday hustle alike, giving you quick and reliably excellent cups and treats right on Banff Avenue.</p>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Milk Tea at Banff" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Coffee Houses &amp; Tea Rooms" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2982" href="http://www.milkteaatbanff.ca/" target="_blank">Milk Tea @ Banff</a></strong>: Choose from a wide range of bubble tea including Brown Sugar Milk Tea and Matcha Lattes. You'll also find a range of Tea Espresso including Honey Jasmine Tea and Brown Sugar Oolong Tea.</p>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Second Cup" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Coffee Houses &amp; Tea Rooms" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2048" href="http://www.secondcup.com/" target="_blank">Second Cup</a></strong>: With flavours to suit every season - Second Cup offers a wide variety of spiced and sweet specialty coffees. This Fall, be sure to try their Matcha Spice Oat Latte or an Iced Pumpkin Spice Latte.</p>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Starbucks Coffee" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Coffee Houses &amp; Tea Rooms" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2137" href="https://www.starbucks.ca/" target="_blank">Starbucks</a></strong>: Iconic coffee pairs well with a trip to iconic Banff and Lake Louise. You'll find two convenient Starbucks locations along beautiful Banff Avenue.</p>

<h4>Explore and Pour</h4>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="STOCK Food &amp; Drink" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bakery &amp; Deli" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2481" href="http://www.fairmont.com/banff-springs/dining/stock-food-drink/?genid=stock_bsh_bllt&amp;utm_source=stock&amp;utm_medium=paid_referrer&amp;utm_content=bsh&amp;utm_campaign=bllt" target="_blank">STOCK Food and Drink</a> </strong>(Fairmont Banff Springs): Stock up for your adventure with morning gourmet coffee and artisanal meals and snacks. Perfect for an authentic mountain moment!</p>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Buffalo Mountain Café" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bakery &amp; Deli" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2803" href="https://crmr.com/resorts/buffalo-mountain/dining/" target="_blank">Buffalo Mountain Café</a></strong> (Buffalo Mountain Lodge): Located on Tunnel Mountain Road, this is a great place to stop pre-or post-adventure for a casual bite and delicious coffee.</p>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="BARE Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2619" href="https://www.facebook.com/barebistro12/" target="_blank">Bare Bistro</a></strong>: Head to this locals' favourite location found off the beaten path for a selection of coffee, tea, and hot lunch options.</p>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Tim Hortons" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Fast Food" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2631" href="https://timhortons.com/" target="_blank">Tim Hortons</a></strong>: Nothing pairs better with a hot cup of coffee than a box of Timbits. Enjoy a classic Canadian favourite in the Canadian Rockies!</p>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Retail" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Tea Co." data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2399" href="http://www.banffteaco.com" target="_blank">Banff Tea Co.</a></strong>: Browse a selection of tea accessories and exclusive tea blends. You'll also find fun feature teas such as Pumpkin Spice Chai or Golden Autumn.</p>
<p><strong><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Alpha Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bakery &amp; Deli" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2127" href="http://rockymountainresort.com/restaurant/" target="_blank">Alpha Bistro</a></strong> (Banff Rocky Mountain Resort): You'll find their fresh coffee pairs perfectly with their Bear Omelette or Rocky Mountain Skillet. The Mountaineer Lunch to Go is also perfect for a day of exploring.</p>
