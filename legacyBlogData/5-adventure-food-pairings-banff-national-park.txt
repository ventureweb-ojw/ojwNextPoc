<div><p>Like fine wine, some activities pair with our favourite food better than others. Here are a few ways to plan your next nature escape from start to finish. </p>
<h4>Cross country ski + fondue</h4>
<p>Cross-country skiing is a favourite pastime in Banff, and it’s been that way for over a century. In fact, skinny skis on flat ground were the preferred winter activity before downhill took off. We’re sure the first Swiss explorers here had a soft spot for the fondue pot, too. </p>
<p>There are several excellent trails in Banff National Park. <a data-gtm_category="Services" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Parks Canada" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Community &amp; Government" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2548" href="https://www.pc.gc.ca/en/pn-np/ab/banff/activ/skidefond-xcski" target="_blank">Spray River trail</a> starting at the <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Banff Springs" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2473" href="http://www.fairmont.com/banff-springs" target="_blank">Fairmont Banff Springs hotel</a> is an easy scenic out-and-back trail, and the nearby <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Waldhaus Pub &amp; Patio" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2483" href="http://www.fairmont.com/banff-springs/dining/waldhauspub/?genid=waldhauspubandpatio_bsh_blltdining&amp;utm_source=waldhauspubandpatio&amp;utm_medium=paid_referrer&amp;utm_content=bsh&amp;utm_campaign=blltdining" target="_blank">Waldhaus Pub</a> offers an Alpin Cheese Fondue sure to satiate the soul. Or check out <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Grizzly House Restaurant" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2304" href="http://www.banffgrizzlyhouse.com" target="_blank">Banff Grizzly House Restaurant</a> - one of Banff's landmark fondue stops. </p>
<p>The Lake Louise area also hosts a variety of unique trails, best completed with a visit to the <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="The Walliser Stube" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2486" href="https://www.fairmont.com/lake-louise/dining/walliser-stube/" target="_blank">Walliser Stube’s</a> Fondue - a cozy restaurant in the <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2474" href="http://www.fairmont.com/lake-louise/" target="_blank">Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise</a> with several decadent styles of fondue. Indulgence at its finest!  </p>

<h4>Backpacking + burgers</h4>
<p>They say that food tastes better in the backcountry, but it’s just as good after the backcountry, too. A juicy burger with all the fixings is best enjoyed fresh off the trail and fresh with endorphins. Discover your burger potential with <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Eddie Burger &amp; Bar" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2264" href="http://www.eddieburgerbar.ca" target="_blank">Eddie Burger &amp; Bar</a>'s custom build menu. </p>
<p>Find a patio where you can sit back and appreciate the view of your objective to really savour your accomplishment, and your burger too! </p>
<p><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Elk &amp; Oarsman Pub &amp; Grill" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2267" href="https://elkandoarsman.com/" target="_blank">The Elk &amp; Oarsman</a> and <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Rose &amp; Crown" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2038" href="http://www.roseandcrown.ca" target="_blank">Rose &amp; Crown</a> have great summer patios with views of Banff’s prominent peaks - Rundle, Sulphur, Tunnel, and Cascade. Or if you’re hiking around Castle Mountain Junction, make a burger stop at <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Baker Creek Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2782" href="http://bakercreek.com/Baker-Creek-Bistro/" target="_blank">Baker Creek Bistro’s</a> rustic yet quaint patio. For more inspiration, check out our blog: <a href="/blog/9-patios-you-need-try-summer-banff-lake-louise"><em>9 Patios You Need To Try This Summer.</em></a></p>
<h4>Ski + Curry</h4>
<p>With day’s end of any skiing mission, whether resort or backcountry, the top priority is a hearty and spicy meal to warm us up from the inside out. </p>
<p><a href="/dining-nightlife/pad-thai-restaurant">Pad Thai Restaurant</a> in the Clock Tower Mall is a hidden gem in Banff, offering an excellent Thai Green Curry and Massaman Curry. Be sure to try the unique Thai Iced Tea, too!</p>
<p><a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Masala Authentic Indian Cuisine" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2260" href="http://www.masalabanff.com" target="_blank">Masala</a> is the only East Indian restaurant in Banff National Park and a delicious one at that. Madras, vindaloo, masala and more - you’ll have a hard time deciding which dish to try. Your best bet is to dine with a big pack of ski buddies and share as many as possible! </p>

<h4>SUP + Taco </h4>
<p>The lakes in Banff National Park may not be the warmest for swimming (unless you have pretty thick skin), but they’re excellent for stand-up paddleboarding! <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="92.0000" data-gtm_label="Banff Canoe Club" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2528" href="https://bll.fyi/yb11" target="_blank">The Banff Canoe Club</a> is located right in the town of Banff on the Bow River and will set you up for a great day exploring the water.  </p>
<p>Once you’re SUP-ed out, get that paddle out of your hand and replace it with a taco! <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Magpie &amp; Stump and Cantina" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2252" href="http://www.magpieandstump.ca" target="_blank">Magpie &amp; Stump</a> has no shortage of taco filled meals, and you might as well indulge in a lime margarita (or two) while you’re at it!</p>
<p>They have ample patio space - both streetside and rooftop - to soak in the last of the day’s sunshine and scenery.</p>
<h4>Climbing + Pizza</h4>
<p>Whether climbing a frozen waterfall in the winter or sport climbing at a sunny crag in the summer, pizza is a treat to top off your adventure year-round. </p>
<p>Beginners can look to <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Yamnuska Mountain Adventures" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Outdoor Recreation" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2472" href="http://www.yamnuska.com" target="_blank">Yamnuska Mountain Adventures</a> for the first courses and steps of trying out climbing, whether on rock or on ice. </p>
<p>While climbing can be gratifyingly challenging, keeping the post-adventure meal simple is a great balance. <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Bear Street Tavern" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2155" href="http://www.bearstreettavern.ca" target="_blank">The Bear Street Tavern</a> are well-known for their fresh ingredient pizzas (and calzones!) with a side of chili oil and honey, and <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Athena Pizza" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="1973" href="http://www.athenapizzabanff.com/" target="_blank">Athena’s</a> has a decades-long reputation of whipping up some of the best za in the valley. If you're looking to add a little bowling into the mix, don't miss <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="High Rollers" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2752" href="http://www.highrollersbanff.com" target="_blank">High Rollers.</a> </p>
