<div><p>The perfect pairing for an adventure is a cold beverage best enjoyed by a fire. Banff and Lake Louise are not short on cozy, Canadian, and tasteful spots to celebrate a day outside and reward yourself for any adventure. Here are 9 places to start your après adventure in Banff and Lake Louise.</p>
<h4>Waldhaus Pub </h4>
<p>Conveniently located next to the <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Banff Springs" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2473" href="http://www.fairmont.com/banff-springs" target="_blank">Fairmont Banff Springs</a> skating rink and tobogganing hill is the <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Waldhaus Pub &amp; Patio" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2483" href="http://www.fairmont.com/banff-springs/dining/waldhauspub/?genid=waldhauspubandpatio_bsh_blltdining&amp;utm_source=waldhauspubandpatio&amp;utm_medium=paid_referrer&amp;utm_content=bsh&amp;utm_campaign=blltdining" target="_blank">Waldhaus Pub.</a> Serving up German-style food and beers, the <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Waldhaus Pub &amp; Patio" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2483" href="http://www.fairmont.com/banff-springs/dining/waldhauspub/?genid=waldhauspubandpatio_bsh_blltdining&amp;utm_source=waldhauspubandpatio&amp;utm_medium=paid_referrer&amp;utm_content=bsh&amp;utm_campaign=blltdining" target="_blank">Waldhaus Pub</a> is the perfect addition to a day on the Fairmont rink at the base of Mount Rundle. </p>
<h4>Banff Ave Brew Co. </h4>
<p>Award-winning beers made from Alberta wheat and barley are served up seasonally and with a new menu as well. <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Banff Avenue Brew Pub" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="1989" href="http://www.banffavebrewingco.ca" target="_blank">The Banff Ave Brew Co.</a> is a friendly and local craft brewery that has a cool mountain flare and a beer for everyone.  </p>

<h4>Juniper Bistro </h4>
<p>Unbeatable views, an outdoor and indoor wood burning fireplace, and best of all – <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Juniper Hotel Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2753" href="https://thejuniper.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/2018-2019-Winter-Graze.pdf" target="_blank">a delicious après menu</a> starting at 4 p.m Monday to Friday, and 2 p.m. on Saturday and Sunday. <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Juniper Hotel Bistro" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2753" href="https://bll.fyi/kvse" target="_blank">The Juniper Bistro</a> is one of Banff's best-rated restaurants and is conveniently located at the base of <a data-gtm_category="Activities" data-gtm_event_value="65.0000" data-gtm_label="Mt. Norquay" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Ski &amp; Snowboard" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2098" href="https://bll.fyi/9jrv" target="_blank">Mount Norquay</a>.</p>
<h4>Park Distillery</h4>
<p>Many of the winter activities in Banff National Park have views of glacier-capped peaks that cast shadows in the low winter sun. <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Park Distillery Restaurant &amp; Bar" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2671" href="http://parkdistillery.com" target="_blank">Park Distillery</a> has coined the term ‘Glacier to Glass' because the very water that makes the spirits they distill, originates at six glaciers. Celebrate your day and ice in every form with a cocktail at <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Park Distillery Restaurant &amp; Bar" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2671" href="http://parkdistillery.com" target="_blank">Park Distillery</a> – all made with their own spirits.  </p>
<h4>Rose &amp; Crown Banff </h4>
<p>Banff's only pub to have live music every night of the week, the <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Rose &amp; Crown" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2038" href="http://www.roseandcrown.ca" target="_blank">Rose &amp; Crown</a> serves up beer, cocktails, wine, and entertainment. </p>
<h4>Nourish Bistro</h4>
<p>Cocktails, vegetarian cuisine, and an urban-like setting on Bear St. in Banff. <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Nourish" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2107" href="http://www.nourishbistro.com/" target="_blank">Nourish Bistro</a> is Banff's only all-vegetarian restaurant and also a unique cocktail bar that is known mostly to locals only. <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Nourish" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2107" href="http://www.nourishbistro.com/" target="_blank">Nourish</a> will be playing the modern music and shaking cocktails to impress when you're ready to reward a day of activity. </p>

<h4>Bear St. Tavern </h4>
<p>Another great local's spot located on Bear St. is the <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Bear Street Tavern" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2155" href="http://www.bearstreettavern.ca" target="_blank">Bear Street Tavern</a> – serving up signature pizza's, sandwiches, and most importantly, ‘bear sized cocktails' along with craft beer from the Bow Valley.</p>
<h4>High Rollers</h4>
<p>Pins, pints, and pizza! <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="High Rollers" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2752" href="http://www.highrollersbanff.com" target="_blank">High Rollers</a> has 48 beers on tap and 10-pin bowling for the ultimate après experience. Celebrate a day of adventure with an evening of bowling at Banff's only 10-pin bowling alley beer hall.  </p>

<h4>Alpine Social Club in Lake Louise </h4>
<p>Lake Louise's newest restaurant and the perfect place for a cheeky treat after a day of adventure in the iconic area itself. <a data-gtm_category="Food &amp; Beverage" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Alpine Social" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Bar &amp; Restaurant" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2768" href="http://thealpinesocial.com/" target="_blank">Alpine Social</a> at the <a data-gtm_category="Accommodation" data-gtm_event_value="1" data-gtm_label="Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise" data-gtm_no_track="N" data-gtm_subcategory="Hotels, Hostels &amp; Inns" data-gtm_trigger="website" data-memberid="2474" href="http://www.fairmont.com/lake-louise/" target="_blank">Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise</a> joins Canadian alpinism history with modern social culture to create a unique atmosphere at the shoreline of Lake Louise.  </p>
